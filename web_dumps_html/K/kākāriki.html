kākāriki

1. [Tāne] ing. He manu noho wao, noho ururua, noho mānia, he kākāriki ngā huruhuru atu i ngā mea whero i ngā papa, i te rae me tētahi wāhi i muri i ngā karu, he huruhuru kahurangi kei ngā parirau, he karu whero, he pango te pito o ngā ngutu kororā, he kiwikiwi parauri ngā waewae. Cyanoramphus novaezelandiae. {kākāwaiariki, kākawariki, kawariki, kawatere1, porete, pōreterete, pōwhaitere, torete, tōreterete}

2. [Tāne] He manu noho wao, noho tāwhao, he kākāriki te nuinga atu o ngā huruhuru, he tāhei whero kei runga ake i ngā ngutu, he kōwhai te tumuaki, he huruhuru whero kei ngā papa, he kahurangi ētahi o ngā huruhuru i ngā parirau, he pango te pito o ngā ngutu kororā, he kiwikiwi parauri ngā waewae, he whero ngā karu. Cyanoramphus auriceps. {kākāwaiariki, kākawariki, kawariki, kawatere2, porete, pōreterete, pōwhaitere, torete, tōreterete}

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. Rite ki te rau rākau ora, ki te pātītī ora rānei te tae. (i) Ātaahua ana ngā huruhuru kākāriki o te kākāpō. (ii) He kākāriki te tae o te kamokamo kāore anō kia pakari. {kārearea, kāriki}

4. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka karanga, ka whakatau i te waihoe, arā, i te tere o te hoe i te waka. (i) Te tangata kākāriki i te waka, arā te kaihautū (W 91). (ii) Mā mea kōhine e kākāriki (W 91).

[Tangaroa] ing. He moko karu nui e 95 mirimita te roa mai i te ngutu ki te kōtore. Naultinus elegans.  moko (moko kākāriki)
