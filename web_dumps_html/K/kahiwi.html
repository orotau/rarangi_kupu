He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kahiwi

1. [Tāne] ing. Te tinana o te rākau.  kōhiwi (3)

2. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. Te pae (o ētahi puke, o ētahi maunga rānei), he roa, he whāiti. (i) He rite ki te tāhuhu whare te takoto o te kahiwi (TT 38, t8). (ii) Ka eke rātou ki runga i tētahi kahiwi, he awaawa kei waenganui e titiro atu ai ki te pā o Rua (Ko 30, wh9). {hiwi2 (1), kaha2 (4), kaka1 (4), kāuaua, kāuki, kaweka, miki, ranga1 (3), ripa, tau4, taukahiwi, taukaka, taupae, tawā, tuahiwi, tūtūātanga}
