kōkopu

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika wai māori e 20 henemita te tipu, he hāurauri ōriwa te tuarā me ngā kaokao, he tāhekeheke horotea anō kei te tinana, he hiriwa ōriwa a raro, he kōtingotingo pango kei runga ake i ngā pakipaki. Galaxias fasciatus. {kakawai, karekopu, (kōkopu ruao), (kōkopu taiwhara), kōpakopako, kopu, kōriwhariwha, moruru, para2 (3), parakeke, reretawa, ruao, ruwao, tarare, tātarāwhare}

kōkopu ruao [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 20 henemita te tipu. Galaxias fasciatus.  kōkopu (1)

kōkopu ruwao [Tūmatauenga] He kupu whakarite mō te rangatira. Aku purapura tuawhiti, aku kōhatu turua, aku kōkopu rūwao kua riro atu nei i te iwi.  tētēkura (2)

kōkopu taiwhara [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 20 henemita te tipu. Galaxias fasciatus.  kōkopu (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-20 henemita te tupu, he parauri ōriwa te tinana me te rere anō o ōna tāingoingo kiwikiwi pango, he karaka te urutira me te paihau o te tou, he ira pango kei runga ake i ngā pakipaki. Galaxias postvectis.

3. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-24 henemita te tipu, he ika pango, he pūmā a raro, he tāingoingo ōriwa kei ngā pāpāringa, he pāpango ngā tāingoingo i te tinana, he iraira kōura e rere ana i ngā kaokao. Gobiomorphus gobioides. {hawai, kokopara, (kōkopu ruao), (kōkopu taiwhara), (kōkopu ururoa), kopu, kōpūtea, māruru2, pakoko, tīpokopoko, tītarakura, titikura2, toitoi1 (2)}

kōkopu ruao [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-24 henemita te tipu. Gobiomorphus gobioides.  kōkopu (3)

kōkopu taiwhara [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-24 henemita te tipu. Gobiomorphus gobioides.  kōkopu (3)
