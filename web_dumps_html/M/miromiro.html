miromiro

1. [Tāne] ing. He manu iti noho wao, noho ururua, he pango te upoko o te toa, he kōiraira mā kei runga ake i ngā ngutu, he pango a runga taka iho ki tētahi wāhi o te uma, he mā a raro me ētahi huruhuru o te hūmaeko me ngā parirau; he ōriwa parauri te upoko, te tuarā me te tumu o te uwha, he mā te korokoro me te uma. Petroica macrocephala toitoi. {hōmiromiro, kikitori, kōmiromiro, māuipōtiki, mīmiro, mirumiru2, ngirungiru, pīmiromiro, pīmirumiru, pīngirungiru, pipitore, pipitori, pīrangirangi, Tāne te waiora, toitoi1 (8)} tarapō (2)

2. [Tāne] He manu moroiti noho wao, noho ururua. Petroica macrocephala macrocephala.  ngirungiru (1)
