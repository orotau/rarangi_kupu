kānapanapa

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tāmutumutu te whakaata, te whakaputa rānei i te aho. (i) Kua mutu te āwhā, e whiti ana te rā, kānapanapa mai ana te moana (HMa 11, Wh1). (ii) Ko tō reira kōura ehara i te mea keri ki te whenua . . . ; ka kitea e te tangata e kānapanapa ake ana i ngā taha o ngā awa (WM 9, 11). (iii) Waihoki e koe i ngā kākahu hiraka, i ngā koti huru, e koe e rere rā i runga i te motokā kānapanapa (KRK 206). {ariari, hana, kākanapa2, kanapa, kapukapu, kawata, kohae, kohara, kora, kōwatawata, kowera, ramarama, rarama, tāuira, titiwa, titiwha, tuhi1 (7), uira}

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. Kei waenga i te kahurangi me te kākāriki te tae. He kānapanapa ngā huru i te tuarā o te takahē. {kārikiōrangi}

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tangaroa] Kākāriki uriuri tonu te tae. He kānapanapa te wai i ngā wāhi hōhonu o te awa.  pounamu (4)
