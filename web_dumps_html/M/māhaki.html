He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mahaki

1. ‑tanga [Whiro] ing, mahp, āhua. He tangata kua pāngia e te mate. He pai te manaaki a tō mātou hōhipera i ana mahaki.  tūroro1 (1)

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore i mau kia tina, kāore rānei i tānekaha, i mārō. Herea he miro ki tō niho mahaki, ka kukume kia makere mai ai.  tangatanga (1)

3. ‑tanga [Whiro] ing, mahp, āhua. He mate e mamae ai, e papī ai, e mangeo ai rānei te kiri. Kāore i roa ka pai haere ana mahaki i tana rongoā.  hakihaki1 (1)

māhaki

‑tanga [Rongo] āhua, ing, mahp. E ngāwari ana, kāore e tere riri, ka whai whakaaro ki ētahi atu, me te ngākau whakaiti anō. Ehara i te mea he pakanga tōna hoaketanga ake. Kia noho ki te kāinga, he tangata hūmārie, he tangata ngāwari, he tangata ngākau māhaki (TTR 1, 20). {hūmārie, hūmārika, waimarie}
