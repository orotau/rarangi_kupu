He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kūpapa

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke whakamua, whakararo te upoko me ngā pakihiwi, i te wā e tū ana, e tūturi ana rānei. I kūpapa mai te tāhae, koia hoki tē kitea ai (W 157).  tuohu (1)

2. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Kāore e āta tautoko, e āwhina rānei i tētahi taha (i te pakanga), ka tū mai ki waho. Mea ake ana tēnei, ka kūpapa tonu ngā Māori e tautotohe ana ki te whenua (KM 3, 1).

3. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He tangata ka āta tautoko, ka āwhina i te hoariri i roto i te pakanga, he tangata rānei kāore e tautoko, e āwhina i āna ake. (i) Ka whatia e Tōpia te rākau whakarawe ki te Kīngitanga, huri kē atu hei kūpapa mā te kāwanatanga (TTR 2, 195). (ii) Nō te tūnga ake o Te Hata ki te kōrero, ka kī ia kore rawa ana wehewehe i te hunga kūpapa me te hunga whana (TTR 4, 215). (iii) I te kore ōna e tautoko i te take a tōna iwi ake, ka kīia ia he kūpapa.

4. [Tāne] ing. He aka piki rākau. Passiflora tetrandra.  pōhue (1)
