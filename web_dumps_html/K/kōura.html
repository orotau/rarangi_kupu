He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



koura1

[Tāne] ing. He harakeke tino pai tēnei mō te whatu korowai me te mahi i ngā whāriki tarakinakina nei. Phormium tenax.

koura2

[Papatūānuku] ing. reo kē. He konganuku ka kaingākautia nuitia, he kōwhai te tae, he tino kōngohe, ka taea anō te paopao kia angiangi tonu, whakamahia nuitia ai hei hanga taonga whakapaipai, hei hanga kahurangi, aha atu, aha atu, he momo moni anō; he tae rite ki te konganuku nei te āhua. (Tohu pūtaiao: Au) (i) He mea waihanga te nuinga o ngā mōhiti mārena ki te koura. (ii) I te nui o te koura i kitea ki Taranawaara ka heke mai te mano o te tangata o ētahi atu wāhi ki te mahi koura (Pi 26, 6).

kōura

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika e 25-30 henemita te tipu, he matihao kei ana waewae tekau, he hihi kei te upoko, he putē ngā karu, he taratara te kapu, he karaka, he waiporoporo te tinana, he kuku kei ngā wae o muri. Jasus edwardsii. {(kōura papatea), matapara}

kōura papatea [Tangaroa] He ika e 25-30 henemita te tipu. Jasus edwardsii.  kōura (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika e 40-45 henemita te tipu. Jasus verreauxi.  pawharu
