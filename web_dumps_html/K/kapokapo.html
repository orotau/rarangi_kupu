He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kapokapo

1. ‑a ‑hia ‑nga ‑hanga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka hopu ki te ringa, ki ngā matihao rānei, ka pupuri kia mau. (i) Kia kaua noa tātau e kapokapo kōrero i te takiwā (HM 11, 3). (ii) Kei te kapokapo ia ki te mātauranga, kua mōhio ia he aha tōna hiahia i tēnei ao, kei te mahi tika tonu ia (Wh 14, 2). (iii) Kapokapo kau ai ō koutou ringaringa (W 96). {apu, aurara, kamu, kawa4, rapi, rarapi, whakakopa1 (3), whakakopakopa}

2. ‑nga ‑hanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka auau te whewhera me te kukuti i te ringa, he momo tohu. E kapokapo mai ana te tangata rā - he aha rā tāna e hiahia rā?

3. ‑nga ‑hanga [Ranginui] Ka tuku i te aho me te rarama anō, te kōpura anō, me te mea nei e kemokemo ana (o ngā mea pērā i te whetū, i te rama tawhiti). Tērā te whetū kapokapo ana mai (W 96).  hikohiko (2)
