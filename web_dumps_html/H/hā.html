He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hā

1. [Tāwhirimātea] ing, āhua. Te hau ka tukua e ngā pūkahukahu ki waho, e taea ana te rongo atu. Ka aroha te keha o tō hā - haere ki te horoi i ō niho! {hau1 (3), manawa}

2. [Tāwhirimātea] ing. Te mominga mai o te hau ki roto i ngā pūkahukahu, me te tukunga ki waho. Ka tūturi te tākuta i te taha o te tangata rā, ka āta titiro ki te kaha o tōna hā.

3. [Tāne] Tā te arero i rongo ai - pai mai, kino mai. Nō te taunga o te hā kawa ki tōna arero, ka tuwhatuwha i ngā kai.  tāwara (1)

4. [Tāne] Tā te ihu i rongo ai - pai mai, kino mai. Ka rongo te koroua i te hā o te rehu tai, mōhio tonu ia kua tae ia ki te hau kāinga. {ami, konakona, monomono2, piro, tuhi2}

5. [Tāwhirimātea] Te āhua o te tangi o te reo o te tangata. Ko te hā o tōna reo, he tioro ki te taringa, he āhua rite ki te kea.

6. [Tūmatauenga] Te ia, te wairua (o te kōrero). Kia whakarongo tonu mai koutou ki te hā o taku kupu atu ki a koutou (W 29). {hū2 (8), rangi, waihoe}

7. [Tāwhirimātea] He tōiriiritanga ka rere i te hau takiwā, i tētahi atu hanga rānei, ka rangona e te taringa; he mea ka rangona e te taringa. E rangona kautia ana te hā o te reo rangatira (W 29).  oro2

8. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakamahi i ngā pūkahukahu kia uru atu ai te hau ki roto i te tinana, ka whakaputa anō ai. Ka whakarongo atu te rata ki a ia e hā ana kia mōhio ai he pēhea tana ora.  ngā1 (1)

9. ‑nga [Tāne] Ka tawhitawhi ina kōrero, kāore e whakaputa noa i te kōrero. I konā ka hā ia, kei kore te hunga whakarongo e pai mai ki ana whakaaro.
