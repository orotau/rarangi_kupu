He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hora1

1. ‑hia ‑ina ‑nga ‑hanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whiuwhiu haere noa i ētahi mea kia tau atu ki tētahi mata, kāore e whai kia raupapa, kia nahanaha te takoto. (i) Ka tae mai ngā tohunga ki te hora rau ki te tūāhu (W 59). (ii) Horahia atu ngā maramara aniana nā ki runga i te ika. {rū, rure, tiri1 (5), whakakorakora, whakamarara}

2. ‑hia ‑ina ‑nga ‑hanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka mahi kia toro ngā tapa, ngā pito rānei o tētahi mea ki tawhiti, kia nui ake rānei te wāhi e kapi ana i a ia. Horahia te whāriki nui ki te papa, hei nōhanga mō ngā tāngata hiahia noho. {horahora, māroha, rora, tahora, tīkei, toha, towha, tūrara, whera, whewhera}

3. ‑hia ‑ina ‑nga ‑hanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakatakoto, ka whakatū, ka whakairi rānei i tētahi mea ki tētahi wāhi e māmā ana te kite atu, kia kitea ai e te tokomaha. (i) Ka hora ia i ana taonga ki mua i te aroaro o te whakaminenga hei tirotiro, hei rāwekeweke mā rātau. (ii) Kauaka te kōrero e kōmuhua ki te tara whare, horahia mai kia rongo te rau e pae nei (W 59).  whakaatu (4)

4. ‑nga ‑hanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka takoto mai ki hea, ki hea, kāore e whāiti mai ki te wāhi kotahi. He kai au ka whiua, ka hora ki te whare (W 59).  marara (1)

5. ‑nga ‑hanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka toro ngā tapa, ngā pito rānei o tētahi mea ki tawhiti, ka nui ake te wāhi e kapi ana i a ia. Kei te papa raima ngā kākahu kaukau e hora mai ana, kia tere maroke ai. {huarapa, kahora, kūrapa, mahora, māroha, māroharoha, ngahora, pōrararara, raharaha, rapa1 (4), roha, tāwhara1 (2), teretere1, toropā, uwhi2, whangawhanga, wharanui2, whārangi, wherawhera, whewhera}

6. ‑nga ‑hanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. E māmā ana te rōnaki o te tuanui, kāore i poupou. He māmā te peita i te tuanui o te whare hora.  pāparu

hora2

1. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka wehe atu i tētahi wāhi i runga i te whāwhai, he karo āhuatanga kino te kaupapa. I te taunga o te pōro ki te papa, ka hora ngā pōpokorua ki wīwī, ki wāwā.  oma (2)

2. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka motu iho i tētahi momo here, ka karo rānei i tētahi āhuatanga mōrearea. Warea ana ngā āpiha ki te pahupahu, huri rawa ake, kua hora te herehere.  oma (3)
