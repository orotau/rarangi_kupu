He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kei1

1. [Tāne] ptm. E tohu ana i te wāhi e noho ana tētahi mea i te wātū, i te wāheke rānei. (i) Rangi: Kei hea taku pōtae? Hine: Kei roto i te waka. (ii) Kei Rotorua a Kiri i te rangi nei, kei Tauranga ia āpōpō. (iii) He tangata kei te kūaha e uiui ana mōu.

2. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te wā e pā ai, i pā ai rānei tētahi mea. (i) He parekura kei te ata (W 112). (ii) Mahia ngā mahi kei tamariki ana. (iii) Rapua a Ihowā, kei kitea ana ia: karangatia atu, kei tata ana mai ia (PT Iha 55.6).

3. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te tangata ko ia e pupuri ana, e pīkau ana rānei i tētahi mea i te wātū, i te wāheke rānei. (i) Kei a wai taku pene whero? (ii) Kei te poari kē tō take e whakaroaroa ana. (iii) Kei a Hopa te kawenga ki te whakamōhio haere i tana iwi. (iv) Kua kore au e wātea ki te haere, kei a au aku mokopuna āpōpō.

4. [Tāne] Ina tū i mua i te kupu 'te', ā, he tūmahi, he tūāhua rānei ka whai i muri mai, e tohu ana i te haere tonu, i te mau tonu o tētahi mahi, o tētahi āhuatanga rānei. (i) Ei, kei te kōrero au ki a koe! (ii) Kei te haere au ki reira āpōpō. (iii) Kei te mea atu au ki a ia me tono ia i te tūranga rā. (iv) Kei te ora katoa tō āhua. (v) Kei te nui te kai i te kāpata.

5. [Tāne] E rite ana ki te 'he rite ki'. (i) Ko ōna niho, kei te koi matā (W 113). (ii) He mano te patupaiarehe, kei te tarakihi (W).

6. [Tāne] I muri i ngā tūmahi e tohu ana i te haere, i te neke, e rite ana ki te kupu 'ki', arā, e tohu ana i te wāhi, i te aronga e ahu atu ana tētahi mea. (i) Heoi ka puta (te tupua rā) kei waho o tōna rua (W 113). (ii) Tuaia te manga o Waīō, ka hinga kei te whenua (W 113). (iii) Ko taku tau kahurangi ka riro kei Paerau (HM 10, 4).

kei2

1. [Tāne] hono. E whakatūpato ana ka pā he raruraru ki te whāia tonuhia tētahi mahi, tētahi āhuatanga, ki te kore rānei te tangata e whai ki te ārai atu i taua raruraru. (i) Kei taka koe i te rākau nā! (ii) Kia kamakama, kei mahue tāua. (iii) Me hari tō koti, kei tūpono ua. (iv) Me whakarite he kaikōrero tuarua, kei kore ia e puta ake.

2. [Tāne] E rite ana ki te 'kaua e', ki te 'kaua rawa e' rānei. (i) Kei pakē mai tō reo ki a au. (ii) E hoa mā, ko te mahi mā koutou ko te tika anake. Kei titiro mai koutou ki ngā mea kūare o te ao (KtKM 7, 8). (iii) Kei noho koe ka hoatu moni ki te pōkokohua rā.

kei3

[Tāne] ing. Te pito whakamuri o te waka. Ko Rakapāwhare, i te kitenga mai i te waka taua, ka peke ki roto i te wai, ka huna ki raro i te kei o te waka (IwiT 9:14). {noko, paremata}
