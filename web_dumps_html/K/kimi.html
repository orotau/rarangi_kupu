He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kimi1

‑hia ‑hanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia kitea mai tētahi mea e ngaro ana, e huna ana, kāore rānei e āta kitea atu ana. Haere koe ki te kimi i ō hū hou - ka rīria koe e Māmā ki te kore e kitea. {apa3, arataki, ārohi, haha, hāhau, hahu, hau2, hauhau1, kimikimi, mohaha, pohau, pōrangi, rangahau, rapa3, rapu, tīhaha, tūhaha, unga}

kimi2

[Rongo] ing. He hue pakari kua oti te karu te tango ake, whakamahia nuitia ai hei kawe wai. Utaina ngā kimi wai maha tonu ki te waka kia kore ai koutou e matewai i tā koutou haere.  tahā
