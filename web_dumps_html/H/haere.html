Haere

[Ranginui] ing. He wairua, ko te imurangi e tū mai ana ki te pūrehurehu tōna kāinga. Tokotoru ngā Haere he tuakana teina katoa, ko Haere-kohiko, ko Haere-waewae, ko Haere-atautu (MRM II, 307).

haere

1. ‑a ‑hia ‑tia ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka neke i tētahi wāhi ki tētahi atu. Kaua kōrua e haere ki te hoko tina i te tāone - kei te nui te kai i te kāinga. {ake2, auraki, awhe, hiemi, mā5, makara, manatu2 (2), nau, ngawī1, pere2 (1), ripoi, rō2, rōrō, wetoki, whana2, whanatu, whanau, whano, whetoki}

haere kōtui [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka haere (ētahi tāngata) me te whiri o ngā ringaringa o tētahi, ki ō tētahi. (i) Anei rāua e haere kōtui mai nei. (ii) Kia kaha ake te haere kōtui o ngā whakahaere me ngā kaupapa whakaora ake i te reo Māori (HM 12, 1).

haere mai [Rongo] kīa. He karanga, he pōhiri ki te manuhiri, ki te hoa, ki te whanaunga kia whakatata mai, kia uru mai. E te manuhiri tūārangi, nau mai, haere mai. {haramai, nau (nau mai), tahuti (tahuti mai), tautimai}

haere mā raro [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka waiho mā ngā waewae tonu e kawe te tangata i tana haere, kaua tētahi momo waka. Kua pakaru te waka - kua mate tāua ki te haere mā raro ki te toa. {(haere pakituri)}

haere pakituri [Rongo] Ka waiho mā ngā waewae tonu e kawe te tangata i tana haere, kaua tētahi momo waka. Atu i konei ki te tihi, me haere pakituri tātou - he poupou rawa mō ngā paihikara.  (haere mā raro)

2. ‑nga [Tāne] Ka whakarere i tētahi wāhi, ka takahi i te huarahi ki wāhi kē. Kua haere ia me tana iwi ki te whakanoho i ngā whenua hou.  wehe1 (5)

3. ‑nga [Tāne] Ka āta rerekē (te maha, te nui, te pai, te aha atu) i te takanga o te wā.  Ka mātua whai mai i te tūāhua, i te tūmahi e hāngai ana te kupu nei ki a ia. (i) I reira ka nui haere te māramatanga (W 30). (ii) Ka hoki haere te pupuhitanga o tōna poho (W 30). (iii) I a ia i Itari ka matatau haere ki tō rātou nā reo.

4. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka āta rerekē te āhua i te takanga o te wā.  Ka pēnei te whakatakoto: ka haere ka (tūmahi). (i) Ka haere ka mōmōhangatia te manu (W 30). (ii) I tōna matenga, ka haere ka kaingākautia āna mahi toi.  whanatu (3)

5. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka neke whakamua. Ka haere tonu ngā hōia ahakoa te pakō mai o ngā pū a te hoariri. {manatu2 (1), nawaki, tukatuka, whano}

6. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He huinga tāngata e whai tahi ana i te huarahi kotahi ki tētahi wāhi. Kia rahi te haere mō tā tātou kēmu, kia haruru pai te papa tākaro i te reo umere.  ope (6)
