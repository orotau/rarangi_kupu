He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



puru1

1. [Tāne] ing. He hanga ka kuhuna ki te kōhao kia kikī tonu, kia kore ai tētahi mea pērā i te wai e puta, e uru atu rānei. (i) Unuhia ana te puru o te hōpua kaukau, kōripo ana te wai i te putanga. (ii) I te kore e kikī o te puru, ka papī mai te wai ki roto i te waka.

2. [Tūmatauenga] He kupu whakarite mō te rangatira. (i) Haere, e te puru o Hokianga, te whakamarumaru o ngā iwi, te herenga o ngā tikanga nunui, te marae o ngā iwi (Pi 47, wh8). (ii) Kua unuhia te puru o Hawaiki (W 314).  tētēkura (2)

3. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kuhu atu i tētahi hanga (ki roto i tētahi puare) hei aukati i te rere o tētahi mea. Purua te puta o te waka ki te kākahu (W 314). {ārai, ārei, kati1 (1), pā2 (2), pani3, papani, tairi}

4. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka mahi kia uru atu, kia noho atu rānei tētahi mea ki roto i tētahi atu hanga. (i) Ka purua ia ki roto i te whare herehere kāore ōna hara (HP 56). (ii) Ki te purua atu he poaka ora i tētahi pito o taua mīhini ka puta mai he tōtiti katoa i tētahi pito (Pi 20, wh12).  kuhu (1)

5. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakakī (i tētahi mea, i tētahi wāhi) kia kore rawa tētahi atu mea e uru atu. (i) Ka maoa te kai, ka purua e ngā tāngata rā he kai ki te waha o te wahine (W 314). (ii) Kia kaua rawa tētahi tangata e patua e rātou, me tango ngā matā o ngā kariri me puru ko te paura anake (WM 11, 17). {opuru, whakapuru}

puru rourou [Tūmatauenga] ing. He wāhanga mīti pūwharu ka whakanōhia mai ki runga i te rourou huawhenua. Kei te patua au hei puru rourou (W 314).

6. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua kī mārika, e kore rawa e uru tētahi atu mea ki roto. Kua puru kē ngā pahi i te mahi a te kaka, me noho rawa e kati ai. {apuapu}

7. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Kua kati, kua tino iti rānei te putanga i te mea e kī ana i tētahi mea (o tētahi momo ara). Ina puru te ihu, ka riro ko te waha kē te ara whakahā.  purutiti

8. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. E tōrino ana te āhua o te tupu (o ngā makawe).  māwhai

puru2

‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kua kapi katoa i te hōpurupuru. Kua puru katoa ā tātou kai i te kore e noho mātao tonu.  hōpurupuru (2)

puru3

[Tāne] ing. Te wāhanga o te huawhenua, o te huarākau e ngaore ana, e kōpēpē ana; tētahi atu hanga e pēnei ana te āhua. (i) Ka tīkarohia mai te puru o te tōmato rākau, ka ruiruia ki te huka, kātahi ka kainga. (ii) Kia roa tonu e kōpirotia ana ngā kaka o te tipu, ka oti mai he puru e taea ana te takapapa hei pepa.

purū

‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. reo kē. Rite ki te rangi i te rā paki te tae. He whero, he mā, he purū ngā tae o te haki Wīwī.  kahurangi (4)

pūru

[Tāwhirimātea] ing. reo kē. Te toa o tētahi kararehe āhua nui tonu ka whāngaihia e te tangata hei oranga mōna, mā rāua ko te kau e puta ai he kāwhe. (i) He reo tāmāori te reo o Kīngi Tāhiwi . . . He mea tito anō hoki e ia he waiata, ā, ko ētehi o aua waiata ko 'He pūru taitama', ko 'Tō ringa, e hine', ko 'Kāore he wāhine', ko 'Tākiri atu, tākiri mai' me 'E whiti te marama' (TTR 3, 191). (ii) Ka meatia he mōhiti ki te ihu o te pūru hei herenga mō te taura, e taea ai a ia te taki haere.
