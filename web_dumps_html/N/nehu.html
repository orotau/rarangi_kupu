He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



nehu

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. He maramara maroke tino moroiti rawa atu (o te oneone, o te aha atu). I te nui o te nehu ka pāngia anō e tana mate huangō.  puehu (1)

nehu raupō [Tāne] Te hae o te raupō i āta kohikohia hei mahi i tētahi momo parāoa. I kohia te nehu raupō i te atatū i mua i te maroketanga o te hune i te rā, ka kawea hoki e te hau. {kōnehu (kōnehu raupō), pungapunga, renga2 (3), tāhuna2 (3), tāhune, tāhunga}

ua nehu [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua. He ua ka āta rere ā-kōpatapata moroiti nei. Ka pai kē te ua nehu nei, ka pipī hoki ki roto i te oneone pakapaka. {hāuaua, tararere, ua2 (tātāriki), ua2 (ua kōnenehu), ua2 (ua pūkohukohu)}

2. [Tangaroa] ing. He kōpata wai tai ririki ka rere ki te hau takiwā i te ākinga a te hau. Ka rere te nehu o te tai (W 220).  rehutai (1)

3. ‑a ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka tuku ki te kōpū o te whenua, ka uhi ai ki te oneone. (i) Ko ēnei reta nā ōna hoa atu ki a ia, kua oti te tuhituhi i waho, me nehu tahi ahau me ēnei reta (PH 3, 2). (ii) Me nehu te Kotahitanga ki roto i Waiapu (Pi 50, wh5).  tanu (1)
