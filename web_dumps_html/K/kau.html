He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kau1

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] tūkē, āhua. Kāore he kākahu, he aha rānei e uhi ana i te tinana. Tū kau ana a Hewa - kāore he pūweru, kāore he aha.  tahanga (1)

2. ‑tia [Ranginui] E noho wātea ana, kāore he aha o roto. (i) Ka kite atu a Raumati i ngā pā o Maketū e tū kau ana mai, kāore he tāngata (W 104). (ii) Ko āna kāpata i takoto ai ana waipiro, kua takoto kau ērā, kāhore kau he kai o roto (Wa 2, 13).

3. [Tāne] āhua. E whakaū ana i te pakupaku, i te hauarea rānei o tētahi mea. (i) Ko ngā patu he patu poto kau (W 104). (ii) Ināianei he wāhi iti kau nei te wāhi o Rutana Moutere kei a rātou (WM 9, 3).

4. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] tūkē. E tohu ana e whāiti mai ana ki tērā e whakahuatia ana. (i) Ko ngā whenua anake e takoto kau ana tā te Pākehā e hiahia ana kia hokona (WM 9, 3). (ii) E tama ē! Ka riro hoki rā nge Atutahi-mā-Rehua; waiho ki muri nei tangi kau ko te mapu (NM4 375, 20). (iii) Me rongo kau noa ake te taringa i te ingoa o Ngoi, mōhio tonu te ao Māori whānui ko wai tērā e kōrerotia rā (H 29).

5. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] E tohu ana kāore e taea ana he mahi i tua atu i tērā e whakahuatia ana. (i) Ka ohorere te iwi hī ika i te putanga atu o te waka taua. Kore rawa he rerenga, ka titiro kau iho ngā tāngata o roto i te pā ki ērā o rātou e patua ana ki te moana (IwiT 9:13). (ii) Nā, kei reira e noho mokemoke ana, kei reira e tangi kau ana ki tōna kāinga kua mahue, ki tōna rangatiratanga kua kore (Ha 3, 8). (iii) Ka mauria hoki ki roto i te whare karakia, e rima tekau anō e uru ki roto, ko ngā mano tāngata e rima ka tū kau atu i waho (PH 7, 10).

6. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] tūkē, āhua. E tohu ana i te rere angiangi o tētahi hanga, arā, kāore he mea e whakapōrearea ana, e whakararu ana, e here ana rānei i te rere. (i) Kīhai i taea te korikori, hahau kau ana te patu (W 104). (ii) E mihi kau ana, e mihi kau ana, kei te mātātahi pūmanawa rau (HM 9, 3).

7. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] tūkē. E tohu ana kāore i puta he hua, kāore rānei e puta he hua i te mahi e whakaaturia ana. (i) A tangi kau ana mai ki ana taonga, ki tana wahine (W 104). (ii) E amuamu kau ana mō te hē o te whakatau a te kaiwawao.

8. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] He wā anō e whakakaha noa ana i te kupu e tū ana i mua. (i) Kāhore kau he tangata kotahi i ora (W 104). (ii) Pai kau hoki ki a au kia mōhio ai au (W 104).

9. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] E tohu ana i te wā tonu ka pā te āhuatanga e kōpaki ana i te kupu, i paku muri mai rānei, ka pā ko tērā atu āhuatanga ka whai i muri iho. (i) Mutu kau anō tā rātou haka, tino katanga o Kae i kata ai (W 104). (ii) Rongo kau, kite kau i te raru, kia wawe te whakanunumi (KWK 26).

kau2

1. ‑ria ‑anga ‑nga ‑ranga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka whakakorikori i ngā ringa me ngā waewae e tere haere ai te tinana i te wai. (i) Ka kau ia, ā, ka ū ki te kāinga o Te Whatuiāpiti (W 104). (ii) Tēnā rā kauria mai te moana e takoto nei (PH 3, 15). (iii) Ko Hinemoa, nāna i kau te roto o Rotorua (W 104).  kauhoe (1)

kau āpuru [Tangaroa] ing. He momo kauhoe e toro whakamua ai ngā ringa ka hoe whewhera mai anō, ko tā ngā waewae he koropeke ki raro i te tinana, ka whana whewhera whakamuri. Ka kī noa iho te puku i te wai ina kau āpuru i te wai pōkarekare.

kau kiore [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahp, āhua. He momo kauhoe e takoto tuarā ai te tangata ki te mata o te wai, ka whanawhana ngā waewae, ka huri kōpae ngā ringa ki te hoe kia haere whakamua ai te tinana. Ki te ngenge te tangata i a ia e kauhoe ana, he pai te kau kiore.

kau tāwhai [Tūmatauenga] ing. He momo kauhoe e tāpapa ai te tangata i te mata o te wai, ka whanawhana ngā waewae, ka huri kōpae ngā ringa ki te hoe kia haere whakamua ai te tinana. E toru ngā wāhanga o te whakataetae; he kau tāwhai, he eke pahikara, he oma. {kauhoe}

2. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka nekeneke, ka hīkoi i roto i te wai āhua hōhonu, me te āhua uaua anō o te haere. Kei ngā hope te wai i a rātou e kau atu ana ki rahaki o te awa.  kautū

kau3

[Tāne] ing. Te wāhanga roroa o te tipu e piki whakarunga ana mai i te papa, e tautoko ana i ērā atu wāhi ōna; te wāhanga o te tipu e mau mai ai ngā rau, ngā pua, ngā hua rānei.  tātā2 (1)

kau4

kau o te kanohi [Tāne] ing. Te puare pango kei te pokapū o te karu. Kia pōuri haere, ka rahi haere te kau o te kanohi.  whatupango

kau5

[Tāne] ing. He huinga tāngata tino tokomaha. E whano ai koe ki tō tini, ki tō mano, ki tō kau ariki (W 105).  tini1 (2)

kau6

[Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. Tētahi o ngā tāngata i heke mai ai te tangata i roto i ngā tātai whakapapa, i tua atu i ōna mātua ake me ērā atu o tō rāua whakatupuranga. Ko te tikanga o tēnei kupu 'Nā ōna kau i waiho', nā ōna tūpuna (W 105).  tupuna

kau7

[Tāne] ing. reo kē. Te uwha pakari o tētahi kararehe āhua nui tonu, ka whakaratangia e te tangata hei kai māna, hei kutētē anō māna. Ki ētahi me whai kāwhe rawa e kīia ai he kau. (i) He maha noa atu ngā pāmu miraka kau i Aotearoa nei. (ii) E waru tekau ana kau e whakatētē ā-ringa ana rātau. Kāore hoki he mīhini kutētē kau i taua wā (HP 22).
