He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



matamata1

1. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. Te wāhi kei te mutunga, te wāhi tino tawhiti rānei o tētahi mea - kāore he wāhanga ōna i tua atu. (i) He whero pūmangu, he tiwha nui kai te rae, he mā kai te matamata o te ihu, e toru waewae mā, he raho poka, he hōiho āhua nui (Wa 5, 51). (ii) Kei te matamata o te ure te kaha o te tāne ki te whakaputa uri ki te ao.  pito

matamata rongo [Rongo] Ka whakapā (i te ihu, i te rae), ki te ihu, ki te rae o tētahi atu ki te mihi atu, he tohu rongomau. Nō te kitenga i ngā kanohi o te tangata whenua o te marae, ka oho te ngākau aroha, ka whai i muri ko te matamata rongo.  hongi (1)

2. [Ranginui] Te wāhi whakarunga rawa (o tētahi mea pērā i te maunga, te rākau, te taputapu, te kaupapa, te aha atu). (i) He roto kei te matamata o Ruapehu. (ii) Ka whakatūria a tameheihei tohuhau ki te matamata rā anō o te tuanui.  tihi (1)

3. [Tāne] Ngā kōpukutanga iti i ngā ū o te wahine e rere ai te waiū, e ngote ai te kōhungahunga; ngā mea pēnei te hanga i te uma o te tāne. Kia makariri, kua mārō tonu ngā matamata, me te mamae anō.  kōmata (2)

4. [Tangaroa] Te pū o tētahi awa, o tētahi kōawa rānei. He nui ngā kino kei roto i te awa i tēnei wāhi, he pai kē atu ngā wai i te matamata o te awa.  mātāpuna

5. [Papatūānuku] He pito whenua teitei e toro atu ana i te tahatika ki te moana, ki te roto rānei. Kei waenganui i ngā matamata rau o Pēwhairangi ngā tauranga marumaru, e kore e pākia e te hau.  mātārae (1)

matamata2

1. [Tangaroa] ing. Ngā poto a te kōpūtea. Retropinna retropinna.  īnanga2 (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 9-10 henemita te tipu. Retropinna retropinna.  kōpūtea (2)
