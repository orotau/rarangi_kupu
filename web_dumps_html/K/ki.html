He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ki

1. [Tāne] ptm. E tohu ana i te wāhi, i te aronga e ahu atu ana tētahi mea. (i) Kei te kōrero te pouako ki ngā ākonga. (ii) Nāku i hoatu ngā āporo ki ngā tamariki. (iii) Kua haere rātou ki Murihiku, noho ai. (iv) Whiua ngā whēkau ika ki te wai. (v) Kei te whakawhirinaki atu te teina ki te tuakana.

2. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te hanga e pāpā atu ana, e taupatupatu atu ana tētahi mea. (i) E kore e mutu tā rātou whawhai ki te kaunihera. (ii) Ka riri te kuia ki ngā tamariki haututū.

3. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te mutunga o tētahi mea kua tū mō tētahi wā. (i) E tuwhera ana te toa mai i te iwa ki te rima. (ii) Kei Hokitika rātou tae noa ki te Rātapu. (iii) I tupu mai i reira ngā kino o te iwi Māori. Haere mai i runga i ngā whakatupuranga, tae noa mai ki tēnei whakatupuranga (H 62).

4. [Tāne] I muri i te nuinga o ngā tūmahi 'wheako' (pēnei i te 'hiahia' me te 'mōhio'), me ētahi atu tūmahi whiti torutoru nei, e whakataki ana i te mea ka hāngai pū taua mahi ki a ia. (i) Kei te hiahia au ki te aihikirīmi. (ii) Kua pīrangi pea a Tama ki a koe. (iii) Kei te mōhio koe ki a rāua? (iv) Kia mārama rātou ki ngā tikanga o tēnei whare. (v) Ka tango hoki a Hatupatu ki te māipi (W 116). (vi) Kua whiwhi rātou ki te taonga whawhai.

5. [Tāne] E whakaatu ana i te taputapu, i te mea rānei koia kei te whakatutuki i tētahi mahi. (i) Me hāro tō harakeke ki te kuku. (ii) Horoia ō ringaringa ki te hopi. (iii) Ka tuhia tana reta ki te reo Māori.

6. [Tāne] Ina tū i mua i te kupu 'te', e tohu ana i te mahi e whāia ana kia tutuki. (i) Kua haere ia ki te kōrero ki ō mātua. (ii) Kei te mahi ia ki te utu i āna nama katoa i mua i tana haere ki tāwāhi. (iii) He mea whakatū a Whakaata Māori ki te whakatairanga i te reo me ngā tikanga Māori ki te pouaka whakaata.

7. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te tangata nōna te whakaaro e whakaaturia ana. (i) Ki a ia, me hoko kē ērā whenua. (ii) Ki tā Mere, he wāhi pai a reira mō te hī ika. (iii) E ai ki Te Komihana Tikanga Tangata, kei te takahi ia i te ture.

8. [Tāne] E rite ana ki te kupu 'mō', ki te 'e pā ana ki' rānei. (i) Ka kōrero mai a Hotunui ki te kino o tōna iwi e noho nei ia (W 116). (ii) He pēhea ōu nā whakaaro ki tāku e kōkiri nei?

9. [Tāne] Ina tū i mua i te kupu 'te', e tohu ana kia eke rawa tētahi āhuatanga e whakaaturia ana i muri tonu mai, kua haere, kua eke, kua tūturu rānei ko tētahi atu āhuatanga e whakaaturia ana i te rerenga.  Mō ngā āhuatanga tērā pea ka eke. Kāore e taea mō ērā e kore rawa e eke. (i) Ki te paki āpōpō, kua haere tātau ki tātahi. (ii) Ki te whakaae mai koutou, māku e tuhi he reta ki a ia. (iii) Ko au e haere atu ki a ia ki te kore ia e haere mai.  mehemea

10. [Tāne] E tohu ana e whakapau kaha ana, e warea ana (te tangata) ki tētahi mahi. (i) Ko Hira tērā ki te āwhina i tana kuia i ngā mutunga wiki katoa. (ii) Ka taona ngā kai . . . ko Māia ki te kawe.

kī1

1. ‑ia ‑nga ‑anga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa whakaaro ki tētahi atu, ka tohutohu i tētahi atu. (i) Ka tae mai a Te Teira ki ahau, ka kī mai, 'E pēhea ana koe?' (Wh 16, 2) (ii) I kī mai ia māna e tunu te purini. (iii) Ka kīia atu kia kopi ngā waha, ka pahupahu tonu.  kōrero (1)

e kī [Tāne] thu. He kupu tū motuhake e whakaatu ana i te ohorere, i te mīharo. E kī! He aha rāua i mahue ai?

kī taurangi [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahp. He kōrero ka puta i te tangata e whakaatu ana i tana whai kia mātua tutuki tētahi āhuatanga, ahakoa te aha. (i) Ka mau tonu te tangata pono ki tana kī taurangi, ā, eke rawa tāna i kī taurangi ai. (ii) Ko taku kī taurangi ki a kōrua, ka oti tō kōrua whare hou te hanga i mua i te Kirihimete. {oati}

2. ‑ia ‑nga ‑anga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakamahi i te reo kia puta ai he kupu e pā ana ki tētahi mea, ka whakamōhio. (i) Ka tae ki te awa i kīia mai rā e Kupe (W 115). (ii) Kīhai i mahi tika i tā rātou tiaki i te whenua e kīia nei, nō te mea, kīhai i mahia he whare kura (Wa 4, 48).  kōrero (3)

3. ‑ia ‑nga ‑anga [Tāne] Ka tohu i te ingoa o tētahi mea, ka tohu rānei he momo aha taua mea. (i) He kupu tēnā mō te Porowini e takoto nei i waenganui i a tātau, arā, e kīia nei ko Tararua (Wa 3, 19). (ii) Ko tā Hakawau patu he parāoa; he wheua nō te parāoa, koia i kīia ai he parāoa (W 115). (iii) Nō neherā tēnei kōrero, nō ngā rā e whakahāweatia ana te wāhine, e kīia ana he pononga (KRK 22).

4. ‑ia ‑nga ‑anga [Tāne] Ka whakatau i te āhua o tētahi mea. (i) He aha ngā tino āhuatanga e kīia ai te Māori he Māori - ko ngā toto, ko te reo Māori, ko te aha atu rānei? (ii) I te ao pūtaiao o te Uru, kāore te ika e kīia he ika, i te mea he whāngote, engari ki te Māori, he ika tonu.

5. ‑ia ‑nga ‑anga [Tāne] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka hihiri ake, ka toko ake he āhuatanga (i tua atu i te kare ā-roto) i te hinengaro, i te ngākau. (i) Ka tae a Kae ki ngā pāua, ka whakapiria ki ngā kanohi, kia kī atu ai ngā wāhine rā kei te oho ia (W 115). (ii) E kīia ana ka tere ngā inanga ki te takiwā o Tonga rā anō, ki reira whakaputa uri ai.  whakaaro (5)

6. ‑nga ‑anga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakamahi i te reo kia puta ai he kupu. Ka tatari rātou kia kī mai te rangatira.  kōrero (4)

7. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He whakaaro ka whakahuatia, ka whakapuakina e te waha tangata. (i) He tao rākau e taea te karo; he tao kī e kore e taea (W 115). (ii) Nāna te kī, kāore ia e kino ki te Kāwana, kāore ia e kino ki te Kīngi (WMA 2, 50). {kōrero, kupu}

kī tao [Tūmatauenga] He karakia ka whakahaeretia mō ngā rākau whawhai i mua i te pakanga.  mata3 (mata rākau)

kī2

‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua eke ki te nui, ki te maha rānei e taea ana (e tētahi mea) te pupuri. (i) Ka ringihia atu te wai ki roto i ngā pounamu, ka kī, ka taupokina. (ii) I te nunui o ngā tuna i mau i a au, e rua noa iho ka kī taku hukapēke. {kohura, kōhure, kopū, pangoro, poha, puha1, puhapuha1, renarena, turuki}

kī3

[Tāne] ing. reo kē. He taputapu iti motuhake tōna āhua ka hangā ki tētahi momo maitai, ka kuhuna atu ki tōna anō raka, ka hurihia kia huaki mai, kia raka rānei tētahi mea, kia haruru mai, kia weto rānei tētahi mea, kia haere tonu ai rānei tētahi mea. Arā atu anō āna mahi. (i) Ka rakaina atu te kūaha, ka raua atu te kī ki tana pūkoro. (ii) Kotahi huringa i te kī o te waka, ka haruru mai. (iii) Ka hurihurihia te kī kia haere anō ai te karaka.
