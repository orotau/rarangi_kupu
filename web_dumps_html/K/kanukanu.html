He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kanukanu

1. [Rūaumoko] āhua. Kua hē katoa te hanga, me te mea nei kua tīhaea rawatia kia hukahuka te āhua (o te kākahu, o te papanga). Ehara ēnei i te hunga kanukanu i kite atu rā ia i te ahiahi. Ānō nei he hunga kē, i te papai o ngā kākahu me te tau o te āhua (Wh 17, 2).  pākarukaru2

2. ‑nga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua oti te kukume kia wehe taretare tētahi wāhanga, kia whai haenga rānei. He pukapuka tawhito, kua kanukanu ētahi o ngā whārangi.  tīhae (3)

3. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kāore e ū ana ngā whakaaro ki te mea kei mua tonu i te aroaro, kua riro ki wāhi kē, ki kaupapa kē. Ehara i te hanga ahau ka kanukanu taku puku ki te riri (W 94).  pōraruraru (2)

4. [Tāne] ing. He kākahu kua taretare, kua kino te āhua nā te roa e whakamahia ana. He kai mō te poho o ana pōtiki, he kanukanu rānei hei uhi mō te tuarā o tana whāereere (H 67). {hautai, kanu, karukaru, kowheka, kōwhekawheka, ngetangeta, petapeta, tāweru, tawhetawhe}
