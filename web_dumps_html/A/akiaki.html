akiaki1

‑tia ‑hia ‑ngia ‑tanga ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakatō i te wairua kaha ki tētahi atu e whāia ai tētahi mahi, e kaha tonu ai rānei ki te mahi. Me akiaki ngā tamariki kia uru ki ngā hākinakina. {kipakipa2, tena, toitoi2 (3), whakahau1, whakahauhau, whakakipakipa, whakamanawa, whakatenatena, whakatītina}
