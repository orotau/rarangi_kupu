He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ora1

1. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. E rere tonu ana te waiora i roto, arā, kāore anō i mate. (i) Kei te ora tonu tō mātou tipuna kuia, ka kotahi rau ōna tau hei te marama nei. (ii) Ko ngā rau kākāriki o te mamaku e ora tonu ana, ko ngā mea parauri kua mate. {kaiao, mataora1}

2. ‑nga [Tāne] Ka mataora tonu ahakoa mate atu tētahi atu, ētahi atu rānei. (i) He atua, te hia ora i te makariri! (W 48) (ii) Kātahi ka patua; hinga iho e rua, ora ake kotahi (N 173). (iii) I te putanga mai o te wai i te pō, ka noho aua kau i roto i te wai, engari ko tētahi o ngā pūru i mate, ko ētahi i ora katoa (PH 3, 17). {puta1 (8)}

3. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka motu iho i tētahi momo here, ka karo rānei i tētahi āhuatanga mōrearea. Ko ngā mea i tere ki te kāwhaki i a rātou i ora, ko ētehi i mauherehere (IwiT 45:3).  oma (3)

ora noa [Tūmatauenga] He waimarie nui i ora ai, i puta ai (te tangata, te aha atu) i tētahi āhuatanga mōrearea. I te mea he whare tawhito, pau ana i te ahi, kotahi te tangata i ora noa.  oraiti

ora pito [Tūmatauenga] He waimarie nui i ora ai, i puta ai (te tangata, te aha atu) i tētahi āhuatanga mōrearea. Ahakoa whati te waewae, ka oma tonu ā, ora pito rawa.  oraiti

4. ‑nga [Tāne] Kua hoki ake i tētahi mate, kua pai anō, kua hauora anō. (i) Ko te wahine nei kua ora i runga i tēnei mate whakarihariha, me te kaha hoki o te tākuta tae atu hoki ki te nēhi (PH 5, 5). (ii) Ka murua ngā rimurimu me ngā kohukohu i tōna tinana, na ka ora ia i reira (W 241).

5. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E pai ana te tinana, te wairua, me te hinengaro, kāore e pāria ana e te mate. (i) Tama tū, tama ora; tama noho, tama matekai (W 240). (ii) Ka nui te ora o Tāwhai, engari kei roto o tōna ngākau e pōuri ana, e mamae ana mō tōna tuakana (KRK 86). {tūhauora}

6. ‑tia ‑tanga ‑nga [Rongo] tūkē, ing, mahp, āhua. Me te kore e whara, e pākia rānei e tētahi āhuatanga kino. (i) Ka riro, ka hoki ora atu ki tōna kāinga (N 144). (ii) Kātahi ahau ka kite ko te nuinga o ngā tāngata kua mate, ko ētahi kua tae ora atu ki uta (WM 9, 10). (iii) Me takoto pai te rori i ngā wā katoa kia haere ora ai te tangata, me tōna hōiho (WM 9, 12).

7. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] maho, ing, āhua. Kua kī te puku, kua ngata te hiakai; kua ngata ngā hiahia. Ka puritia hoki e ngā iwi o Takakōpiri ki reira, ka noho, ka kai, ka ora (W 241).  mākona

8. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He tangata kore mana ka mauheretia hei kaimahi mā tētahi atu. Ka whiua te ora, he kore nōna i aro tika atu ki te tono a tana rangatira.  pononga2 (1)

ora2

[Tāne] ing. He wāhanga rākau, he wāhanga maitai, he wāhanga aha atu, he whāiti tētahi pito, he whānui tētahi, ka paoa atu ki roto i tētahi mea kia kōihi ai, kia hāmama ai, kia hinga ai, ki waenganui rānei i ētahi mea e rua kia kikī ai.  matakahi

ora3

[Tāne] ing. He toa hihi. Notiomystis cincta.  kōtihe (kōtihe wera)
