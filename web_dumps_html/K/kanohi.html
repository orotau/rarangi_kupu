He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kanohi1

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te taha whakamua o te upoko, mai i te kauae ki te rae. (i) Ahakoa mangu taku kanohi he pounamu taku ngākau (AM 1, 2). (ii) Otirā, kei te āhua whakaaro anō au ki te haere kia kite i a ia, kia tūtaki māua, he kanohi ki te kanohi (HP 204). {konohi, mata1 (1), nohi, whakangita2}

kanohi wera [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te hunga ka taka kai mō tētahi huihuinga tāngata. Kia tika a muri, arā te wāhi ki te kanohi wera, ka tika a mua.  ringawera

2. [Tāne] Ngā wāhanga e rua i te kanohi e taea ai te kite. Ka ngoikore hoki ngā kanohi ki te titiro, ngā taringa ki te whakarongo (KRK 106).  karu1 (1)

kanohi aua [Tāne] He tauira mō te raranga, te whatu, te tāniko me te tukutuku.

kanohi mōwhiti [Tāne] He manu iti noho wao, noho ngahere, noho mōheuheu. Zosterops lateralis lateralis.  pihipihi (1)

kanohi o Tāwhaki [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtaka pūhuki te hanga. Turbo smaragdus.  pūpū2 (1)

rekanga kanohi [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He whakaaro, he āhua, he ariā ka whakaaturia ki te hinengaro i te wā e moe ana. Na ka haere ia ki waho, ka aru i a ia; ā, kīhai ia i mahara he pono tā te ānahera i mea ai; hua noa he rekanga kanohi tāna i kite ai (PT Mah 12.9).  moemoeā

kanohi2

[Tāne] ing. He manu noho wai māori pāpaku. Himantopus novaezealandiae.  kakī2
