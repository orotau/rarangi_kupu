hakeke

1. [Tāne] ing. He harore taringa te hanga, he pīngore, he tipu ki te rākau pirau, he parauri tētahi taha, he pūmā tētahi. Auricularia polytricha. {hakeka, hākekakeka, hākēkeke, hākekekeke, hōkeke2, hōkekekeka, keka2, kekakeka, keke2, kēkeke, paheke, tarawhata, taringa (taringa hakeke), taringa (taringa kurī), taringa (taringa o Tiakiwai)}

2. [Tāne] He rākau e 5 mita te tupu, he rau makiki, he pūngarungaru ngā tapa whai niho roa, he pua mā pōī nunui te tupu i te kōuru. Olearia ilicifolia. {hākēkeke, haki1, hakihaki2, kōtaratara1 (2)}

3. [Tāne] He manu tāwhai tai. Puffinus griseus.  tītī2 (2)

4. [Tāne] He manu tāwhai tai. Puffinus tenuirostris.  ōi1 (2)
