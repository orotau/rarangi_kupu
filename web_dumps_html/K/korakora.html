He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



korakora

1. [Rūaumoko] ing. He maramara iti wera ka puhaina mai i tētahi mea e kā ana, ka takoto mai rānei ina tata poko te ahi, ka puta ake rānei ina hikaia ētahi mea mārō e rua. (i) Kei te kaha tonu te wera o te timera me te mumura a roto, a runga hoki, me ngā korakora ahi kei te puhaina ki te rangi (Wh 22, 2). (ii) Ina hoki, i ngahoro ngā korakora o aua whetū ki tēnei takiwā (T 1, 12). (iii) Engari i te mea kei te kāngia te ao e te ahi, ā kei te rērere haere ngā korakora, nā reira kia noho mataara tātou kei wera i taua ahi (Ko 28, wh5). {kora}

2. [Tāne] He maramara mōkitokito. Ka hokihoki tonu te ringa ki te mirimiri i te korakora kei roto i tana karu.  pakuriki

3. ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. E kā ana, e kanapa ana. (i) Ki te motokia taku karu e te tangata ka kite au i ngā whetū, ehara anō ia i ngā whetū o te rangi, i a Pareārau, i a Tāwera, i tōna huhua noa iho, engari kei roto kau i te hinengaro e korakora ana (Pi 41, wh2). (ii) Kua korakora tonu ōna whatu i te kaha whakatakariri.
