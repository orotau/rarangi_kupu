He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



memenge

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka pūreherehe katoa, ka iti iho, i te maroke, i te wera, i te makariri, i te roa rawa e takoto ana, i te roa rawa rānei ki tēnei ao. Ka koroheketia, ka memenge ngā ringa, ngā uaua (W 201). {hūnonoi, kauere, kōpiri, korehe, kōriorio, kōwhewhe, kukupi, kupi, menge, mimingo, ngingio, popoko, pūtoki, pūtokitoki, roiroi2 (2), tingongo, whakatārehe, whewhengi}

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] Kua maroke katoa, kua kopakopa katoa. (i) Ko ētahi i ngahoro ki ngā wāhi kāmaka, ki ngā wāhi kīhai i nui te oneone: nā pihi tonu ake, kāhore hoki i hōhonu te oneone. Ā, nō te whitinga o te rā, ngaua iho; ā, nō te mea kāhore he pūtake, memenge noa iho (PT Mat 13.6). (ii) Kāore e pakari te paukena kia memenge rā anō ngā rau. (iii) Piri tonu ō rātou kiri ki ō rātou wheua; kua memenge, kua rite ki te rākau (PT Tan 4.8). {hūmengemenge, kōmae, komeme, kongene, kōngenengene, kōriorio, mae, maemae, matiti1, menge, ngingio, ngongohe, paro2 (2), parohe, pāroherohe, paroparo, pohe, popohe, popoko, pūtutu, rio, ririo, whewhengi}
