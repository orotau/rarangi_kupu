manawapā

1. ‑kanga ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore e tino pīrangi, ka whakaroaroa rānei (ki te mahi i tētahi mahi). (i) E manawapā ana ahau ki te kōrero, kāhore hoki he mea hei hoatutanga māku ki a ia (W 174). (ii) Nā te uaua pea hoki o te kitea o te moni ka manawapā tātou ki te moumou noa iho i ā tātou hereni (TT 86, wh859). {horokukū, korongatā, manauhea, whakakumu, whakatenetene, whakatohetohe, whakauaua, whakawhēuaua}

2. ‑kanga ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka āhua mataku tonu i runga i te whakapono tērā ka pā he raru, he āhuatanga kino. Ko wai te tangata kāore e manawapā mehemea e noho tahi ana ki te kōhuru? {hopo, hopohopo, manaka, mānakanaka, manawa (manawa kino), mōrearea, pāwera, tāhuhunu, teatea, tīoho, tūmatakuru, tūtakarerewa, whakapūhana}

3. ‑kanga ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kāore e tau ngā whakaaro i runga i te whakapono tērā ka pā he raru, he āhuatanga kino. Kua manawapā katoa au kei riro taku kāri kirihou i te tāhae.  māharahara
