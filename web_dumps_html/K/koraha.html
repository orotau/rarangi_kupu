He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



koraha1

1. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. He whenua hāhā, kāore e tino tipuria e te aha, tērā anō pea he whenua kore wai, e kore e tino kitea he oranga mō te tangata. (i) Mimiti rawa te wai o ngā awa, pau ana te whenua katoa atu i te rā, pūkatakata ana tēnā; he nui ngā kurī o te koraha e mate ana i te kore wai, tare ana ngā arero ki waho (WM 10, 5). (ii) I hāereere rātou i te koraha i te wāhi mokemoke, tē kitea tētahi pā hei nohoanga. I matekai rātou, i matewai: hemo noa tō rātou wairua i roto i a rātou (PT Wai 107.4). {tahora}

2. [Papatūānuku] He whenua e noho wātea ana, arā kāore e tino tipuria ana e te rākau, e te ngāherehere. (i) Ka tae ki Waiōuru, ka whakawhiti ai i te koraha ki Tūrangi. (ii) Ka kite ia i te kauae o tōna tupuna, ka tangohia e ia hei matika mōna, ka maua ki te koraha hanga ai hei matika (N 14). {korehe, manaha, mānahanaha, pākihi, tahora, tārake (nuku tārake)}

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tangaroa] āhua, ing, mahp. E pāpaku ana (o te wai e hora ana i te taikoraha). Kua haere rātou ki te mahi pātiki i ngā wai koraha.

4. ‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka toro kia whānui ake, kia nui ake, kia roa ake. Kia koraha ngā parirau o te kea, ka kitea atu ngā huruhuru whero i ana kēkē.  raha

koraha2

‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tuku i ngā tūtae o te tinana. Ka roa au e tiaki ana kia mutu mai te koraha a taku tungāne, ka mōhio au he pānui pukapuka kē te mahi i te wharepaku.  tiko1 (1)
