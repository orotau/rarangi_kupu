He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



angi

1. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka māmā noa te neke i te kore pōreareatanga. Kua noho tonu mai ko ō tātou iwitanga hei whakakōpekapeka i te kaupapa, tē tuku kē ai i te kaupapa kia angi te neke whakamua (HM 8, 3). {whakaangi1}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka noho mai, ka rere rānei i te mata o te wai, ka rere rānei i te hau takiwā. (i) Kua angi haere te hune i te hau o te takiwā. (ii) Tērā te kaipuke te angi mai rā i waho.  rewa (2)

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Kāore he here, he pōreareatanga, he aha atu hei whakararu mai. Ka karakia a Rātā i tōna waka kia angi ai tōna haere (W 11).  māhorahora

4. [Rūaumoko] ing. He āhuatanga e pā ana ki te heke o te wairua ki Rarohenga. Kātahi ka huakina te tatau o te angi nuku ki te pō ki Rarohenga (W 11).
