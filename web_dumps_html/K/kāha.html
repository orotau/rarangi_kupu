He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kaha1

1. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He āhuatanga i te tinana e taea ai e te tangata, e te aha atu rānei te āta kori ake ki te mahi i tētahi mahi. Kua pau taku kaha, kua hoki au ki te takoto.  ngoi1 (1)

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. E mārō ana, e whai ngoi ana, me uaua ka pakaru, ka whati, ka motu, ka whara rānei. (i) Moea te tangata kaha, te tangata ringa raupā! (ii) He iwi kaha te Māori ki te pupuri i ō rātou whakapapa (KRK 53). (iii) Taura nui, taura roa, taura kaha, taura toa, taura here i a Tamanuiterā, whakamaua kia mau, kia ita (Tki pū). {akuaku, aumangea, aumangeo, hohe, kōmārohi, marohi, mārohirohi2 (1), mārōrō, mōrohirohi, ngana, pāuaua, pūioio, rīrā, tāngutungutu}

kaha2

1. [Tāne] ing. He taura kei te tapa o te kaharoa. Ka horahia ngā kupenga rā, ko te kaha o raro he mea pou ki te pou, here rawa te kaha ki te pou (W 82).

2. [Tāne] Ngā here hei tui i te rauawa ki te tahiwi o te waka. Ko ngā waka katoa me tapatapahi ngā kaha (W 82).  aukaha (2)

3. [Tāne] He aho ka herea kia āhua porohita mai te hanga, kia mahuhu anō te pona, ina kumea, kua kikī haere. (i) Pōtaea te kaha ki te mātenga o te kererū (W 296). (ii) I uia te kaha i te kakī o te manu (W 466). {kono, koro2 (1), koromāhanga, pahuhu, patatari, pōtari, tari3, toherere, tukutuku, whāpiko}

4. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. Te pae (o ētahi puke, o ētahi maunga rānei), he roa, he whāiti.  kahiwi (2)

kaha3

1. [Tāne] ing. He ingoa whānui mō ētahi karakia e pā ana ki ngā tikanga mahi ika, mahi manu, me ētahi atu mahi pērā. Ko Tuota, he kaha, he karakia mō ngā manu, kiore, ika, kia mate (W 82).

2. [Tangaroa] ing, āhua. He tā rimurimu ka taona i te umu, ka karakiatia, ka kawea i roto i te ihu o te waka i ngā haerenga.

kāha

[Tāne] ing. He manu noho roto, noho hikuawa rānei i ngā maunga o Te Waipounamu. Podiceps cristatus.  pūteketeke
