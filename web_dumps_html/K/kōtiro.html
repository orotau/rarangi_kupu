kōtiro

‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. He wahine e tamariki tonu ana, e taiohi tonu ana rānei. (i) Nō te whānautanga o Te Ataihaea, he kōtiro, kua hiahia tō mātau māmā kia riro mai i a ia hai whāngai māna (HP 19). (ii) Kāore hoki i ārikarika te hinga a te pakeke, a te taitama, a te kōtiro, a te tamariki hoki (HP 37). (iii) Kātahi ka kī atu te tangata rā, 'Ko koe tonu te wai i haere mai ai ahau.' Ka rongo te kōtiro rā i aua kupu, ka mahara ia, 'Ha, kua hiahia te tangata nei ki a au' (Wh 2, 6). {hengahenga, hika2 (2), kōhaia, kōhine, taitamāhine, tamāhine}
