He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kino

1. ‑nga ‑hanga ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. E tino hara rawa atu ana, ko te mutunga kē mai o te hē. (i) Ko ngā ture kino a te Kāwanatanga e pēhi nei i te iwi Māori, me whakarere (Wa 4, 16). (ii) E tau ana te titiro a te tangata kino ki te tangata tika, ā, e whai ana kia whakamatea ia. (iii) He mahi kino te kōhuru. {kikino}

2. [Whiro] āhua, ing. Kāore e pai ki te titiro atu i te kore e pai o te āhua. (i) Ka reia tonutia te tangata kino mēnā he tangata whai mana, whai rawa rānei. (ii) He kākahu pai o ētahi, he kākahu kino tō Paoa (W 118).  anuanu1 (4)

3. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing. E tino pai ana, e tino kaha ana rānei. (i) He kino kē te tamaiti rā ki te oma, kāore ētahi e kātata atu ki a ia. (ii) Tino kino kē te kaha o Te Putu ki te tuhituhi i ana kōrero ki ana rātaka, hei whakaatu i te āhua o āna haere maha (TTR 4, 85).

4. ‑ngia ‑hia ‑tia ‑nga ‑hanga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka ngākau kawa, kāore e pai ngā whakaaro (ki tētahi tangata, ki tētahi mea rānei). Tētahi take i kinongia ai ia e ōna hoa, he nui nō te moni i a ia (Pi 20, wh4).  matakawa

5. ‑nga ‑hia ‑tia ‑nga ‑hanga ‑tanga [Whiro] Ka mahi kino ki te tinana, ki te wairua rānei o tētahi atu, e whara ai, e haumate ai ia. Ka waeahia atu te ture ki te kino tonu koe ki ō kurī.  tūkino (1)

6. ‑nga ‑hanga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua hē te āhua, kua hē te mahi, kua takakinotia. (i) Kua kino katoa te māra a Piko i ngā poaka a tana kiritata (KK 93). (ii) Ko tōna whaea e tū ana i te taha, me te oka anō i roto i tōna ringa kua kino katoa i te toto (PH 3, 15). (iii) Kotahi rau ōna pēke witi kua kino, kua mākū i te wai (WMA 4, 4).

7. ‑tia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] tūkē. Me te hē anō (o te kawenga, o te mahinga, o te wairua, o te aha atu). (i) I te manawapā o Maniatiemi, kei mahia kinotia e tana tamaiti tōna mōhiotanga ki tērā karakia (IwiT 32:2). (ii) He hunga rawa kore, e tūkinotia ana, e whakatupuria kinotia ana; kīhai nei te ao i pai mō rātou (Pi 30, wh10). (iii) I kōrerotia kinotia a Pīhopa Herewini e te Pākeha mō tana whakawhirinaki ki te Māori (TT 75, wh683).
