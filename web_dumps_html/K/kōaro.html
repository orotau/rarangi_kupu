He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōaro

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Ko runga (o tētahi mea) kei raro kē, ko raro kei runga, ko roto rānei kei waho, ko waho kei roto. (i) Kua noho kōaro te pēpē ki te kōpū - ko ngā waewae kē e puta tuatahi mai ana, kaua te māhunga. (ii) Me huri te pepa whai moko i roto i te pūreretā, mā reira e kore ai e puta kōaro mai ngā kōrero ki te whārangi. (iii) Kua kōaro tō hāte - kua noho mai te tapanga i te kakī ki waho. {kōwaro1}

huri kōaro [Tāwhirimātea] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka huri i tētahi mea kia noho ko roto ki waho, ko waho ki roto. Ka huri kōarotia tana tarau, kāore tonu i kitea te puruhi kua kaikai rā i ana waewae. {hurirua, kōhure}

huri kōaro [Tāwhirimātea] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka huri kia kōaro te noho mai, kia rerekē katoa rānei te ahunga. (i) Me huri kōaro te paepae, ka pākōkō i te tou. Ko te tikanga ka makere noa mai te keke. (ii) Kua huri kōaro ngā whakaaro, kua kore rātou e hiahia tautoko ā-pūtea mai i te kaupapa.

huri kōaro [Whiro] ing. He aituā ka pā i te hapa o te whiu i te tao.

2. ‑tanga ‑hanga ‑nga [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore e pai te tupu (o ētahi kai o te māra) i te pāngia e tētahi mate. (I te nuinga o te wā, he mate harore.) Ruia tā tātou rīwai ki te rongoā nei kia kore ai e kōaro. {kōwaro1}

3. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika wai māori 16-18 henemita te tipu, he kākāriki ki te ōriwa parauri te tinana me te rere anō o ōna tāingoingo kākāriki parauri, he iraira pango kei runga ake i ngā pakipaki, he manauri ngā paihau katoa. Galaxias brevipinnis. {hiwihiwi2, kakawai, kōwaro2, maeha, mohimohi1, ngohongoho, pangare, raumahehe, rāwai, rōrōai, taiwharu, tarare, tohitohi}
