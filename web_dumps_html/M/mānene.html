He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



manene1

[Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua, tūkē. He tangata kāore e mōhiotia ana, he tangata rānei kua tatū mai i whenua kē, i iwi kē, he rāwaho. (i) He manene ahau ki tōu whenua (W 175). (ii) Kia kotahi te tikanga mō koutou, mō te manene hoki e noho tahi ana me koutou (PT Tau 15.15). (iii) Ka piri tonu ngā manene ki a rātou anō, koinei e kaha tonu nei ō rātou reo me ā rātou tikanga.  tauhou (3)

manene2

‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore (te tinana, tētahi wāhi rānei o te tinana) e rongo i te aha. Kua manene te ringa e moea ana e pēpē.  kēkerewai (2)

mānene

‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka rite tonu te inoi i te tangata kia mahi i tētahi mahi, kia whakaae rānei ki tētahi mea. Me mutu tō mānene - hei reira kē pea tahuri ai te tangata ki te whakangata i tō hiahia.  kākari (1)
