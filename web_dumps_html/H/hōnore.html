He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hōnore

1. [Tūmatauenga] ing. reo kē. He āhuatanga e rekareka ai, e harikoa ai, e whakahīhī ai te tangata. (i) Nā Tarati anō au i whai haere hai mārena i a rāua, ā, koa ana hoki au ki tēnei hōnore (HP 245). (ii) He hōnore nui ki a rātou te kōrero me te haere tahi i te taha o ngā tāngata o ngā koroni, engari ko te Māori te iwi e tino whāia ana (Pi 55, wh6).

2. [Tūmatauenga] He tohu ka hoatu, he ingoa rānei ka uhia e te Karauna ki te tangata, e ingoatia ai ia mō tana māia, mō te nui rānei o tana whakapau kaha ki tētahi kaupapa. (i) Engari i whiwhi rawa ia ki te hōnore nui a Kuīni Riripeti Tuarua, Tā Turi, i mua o tōna matenga (HP 33). (ii) Ko taua hōnore i tau ki tō tātou hoa ki a Āpirana Ngata. Kua puta noa atu tēnei taitara hōnore ki a ia, engari kāore a Apirana i whakaae (TT 70, wh610). (iii) Kua rongo atu i te toa nui whakaharahara o koutou ki te whawhai i te hoariri, ā, i te whiwhinga hoki o tēnā o koutou, o Te Moana-nui-a-Kiwa, ki te hōnore tino nui rawa atu, arā, i te Rīpeka Wikitōria (HP 59).

3. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp. E tika ana kia mihia, kia whakanuitia, he tohu rangatira. (i) Heoi anō te mea nāna i ora ai te ngākau nā ā koutou reta e tuhi mai nei, e mea nei he mate hōnore te mate ki te pakanga (KRK 85). (ii) Ko ia te mātāmua o Niu Tīreni ki te hinga ki roto o te pakanga. He mate hōnore te mate ki roto o te whawhai (Pi 23, wh7).
