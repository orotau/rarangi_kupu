He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mohimohi1

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika wai māori 16-18 henemita te tipu. Galaxias brevipinnis.  kōaro (3)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika tai 15-20 henemita te tipu, he kikorangi, he pāua a runga, he kākāriki whai kōtingotingo pango ngā kaokao, he hiriwa a raro. Sardinops neopilchardus. {mohi}

mohimohi2

1. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore he pukupuku, he wāhi taratara, me te mōhinuhinu anō (o te kiri, te makawe, te huruhuru). Ātaahua ana ngā huruhuru mohimohi o te tūī. {karengo, koutata}

2. ‑tia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi i ngā mahi o ia rā, i ngā mahi auau rānei e mau tonu ai, e pai ake ai rānei te noho ora o tētahi mea. I noho atu a Māmā ki te mohimohi i a Hera i a ia e māuiui ana.  tiaki (1)

3. ‑tia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka āta tiaki (i te tangata, i te kararehe) i a ia e māuiui ana, e hē ana rānei te wairua. Kāore i haere i ā te rangatahi haere, i noho ki te kāinga ki te mohimohi i tana whaea e kainga rā e tana mate.  tapuhi (1)
