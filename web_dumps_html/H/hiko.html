He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hiko

1. ‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tuku, ka whakaata rānei i te aho kaha tonu, otirā he poto, he ohotata, he taratahi te āhua o te whakaputa. (i) Tērā te uira e hiko i te rangi (W 50). (ii) I hiko mai ngā rama o te waka kātahi tonu ka hipa atu, ki te whakatūpato i a au. {huki, kapo, karamu, kōhā, kohiko, kōkirikiri1 (3), kōpura2, kōrapu1, kōwhā, muramura, nanamu, rapa1 (5), rarapa, rīraparapa, uira, wheriko}

2. ‑nga [Ranginui] Kua mārama haere te rangi i te ata, engari kāore anō kia piki te rā ki runga ake i te pae. Ka hiko te ata, ka maranga au, ka haere ki te oma.  ao1 (5)

3. ‑ia ‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka whakaata, ka whakaputa rānei i te aho. (i) Ka āta mukumukua tana waka ki te papanga kia hiko tonu. (ii) Hikoia e te awatea, anō he pounamu kei ngā karu pango e titiwha ana (W 50).  kōwatawata (2)

4. ‑nga [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka oraora ake (o te manu i te tākiritanga o te ata). E hiko ana te manu i te ata, ka whakatika māua, ka haere (W 50).

5. ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka tere tonu te rere o te ringa o te aha atu rānei, ki te tango i tētahi mea. Ka hiko ia ki te tahā, ka ringitia (W 50).  kapo (1)

6. [Ranginui] ing. He uira kei tawhiti. Arā te hiko e kōhā mai rā i te tāepaepatanga o te rangi.

7. [Ranginui] He momo pūngao whai whana ka whakamahia nuitia e te tangata hei whakahaere i ngā whakamahana, i ngā rama, tae atu ki ngā mīhini maha o te kāinga me te ao ahumahi. He tino taonga te hiko, heoi anō tā tātou he pēhi i te pātene kua kā mai ngā tūrama.
