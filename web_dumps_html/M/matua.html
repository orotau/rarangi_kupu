He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



matua

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te tāne nāna i puta ai tētahi tamaiti ki te ao, nāna rānei ia i whakatipu. (i) Nō konei tonu te whaea, a Rīria, ko te matua nō Te Whānau-a-Rua. (ii) Tokorua rāua he tamariki whāngai, ko Tame tō rāua matua. {hākoro, kohake, koro3 (2), pāpā1, pāpara, uretū}

2. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He wāhanga nō te ope taua. Ka tūtū ia matua ki mua i te aroaro o te tianara (Ng 62). {tohu1 (6), whare}

3. [Papatūānuku] He huinga pūtau kōpae te hanga ka tipu i te kōpū o te whaea, o te whāereere, hei hono i ana toto ki ō te kukune, e whakawhitihia ai te hāora, te kai, me ngā para. Kāore i roa i muri i te whānautanga, ka puta mai te matua me ērā atu wāhi o te whenua.  ewe (1)

matua kihikihi [Tāne] Te punua a te kīkītara ka noho i raro i te whenua mō ētahi tau nei ki te momi tarawai māna. He tarawai te kai a te matua kihikihi, ka momia e ia i ngā paiaka o te rākau.  ngengeti (1)

4. [Tāne] Te tinana o te waka i mua i te hononga atu o te haumi, o te aha. Ka whakaekea ngā rauawa ki runga ki te matua (W 195).  kaunaroa

5. ‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. He tāne, he wahine rānei kua neke atu ōna tau i ō te tamariki, i ō te taiohi, engari kāore anō kia koroua rawa, kia kuia rawa. Tēnā kia matua ia, he tangata mātau rawa ia (W 195).  pakeke (5)

6. [Tūmatauenga] āhua. E noho ana hei mea nui rawa, e noho mātāmua mai ana. (i) He mea nui te kōrero i te reo o tōu ake kāinga hei whakapūmau i te tuakiri ahurea, ā, koinei te kaupapa matua o te wānanga (TTW 04, 23). (ii) Kei te huarahi matua o Te Ika-a-Māui a Taupō. {aporei}

mātua1

1. [Tāne] tūkē. E tohu ana i te mahi me tino mahi kia tutuki ai, kia tika ai tētahi atu āhuatanga. (i) Me mātua mōhio koutou ki ngā tikanga o te marae ka tahi, me ngā āhuatanga e pā ana ki ngā tamariki ka rua (H 26). (ii) He wāhi kūiti hoki e tuhera ana i waenganui o aua kōhatu, ā, me mātua takitahi te haere atu ka puta ai te tangata i taua wāhi (IwiT 36:5). (iii) E kore anō hoki e tika kia tukua tētahi āhuatanga hou kia uru ki roto, kia mātua kōrerorerotia rā anō, kia mātua whakaaetia rā anō, e ngā hunga katoa e uru ana ki taua kanataraka, kātahi ka tika (PH 4, 9).

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E tino whai take ana, e tino whai tikanga ana; e whai mana ana, e noho mai ana ki tētahi taumata teitei. He kai mātua te niu ki ngā iwi o Te Moana-nui-a-Kiwa.  nui (3)

3. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Inā kē te maha, te nui e wātea ana, kāore e wawe te pau. He puna pūngao mātua te hau i Te Whanga-nui-a-Tara.  huhua (1)

4. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te wāhanga matua o te ope taua. Kātahi anō te mātua ka pakaru, ka patua (W 195). {hope, tukunui}

mātua2

[Tāne] ing. Te takitini o te kupu 'matua', te tokorua nā rāua i puta ai tētahi tamaiti ki te ao, nā rāua rānei i whakatipu. Kua oti e au te kōrero, kāore e kōrerohia ana te reo Māori i roto i ō rātau kāinga, me pukuriri rawa ngā mātua ki ā rātau tamariki, kātahi anō ka kōrero Māori (HP 97).
