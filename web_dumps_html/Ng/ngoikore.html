He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngoikore

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka kaha kore, ka ngao kore. (i) Ki te kore e pai te tiaki e kore rawa ia e ora, engari ka heke tonu atu, ka ngoikore, ka hīroki, ka poto te hau, kāore e kaha ki te kai (PH 4, 4). (ii) Kua piri ngā pānui kaua tētahi waka e haere mā runga i tēnei piriti kei te ngoikore (HP 288). {hangenge1, hauaitu, hauarea, hauhauaitu1 (2), hauwarea, hūngoingoi, hūngongoi, kahakore, kongange, kopī, kōpīpī, kūī1 (4), maero1 (2), maiangi, mohorīrīwai, ngahengahe1, ngehengehe, ngōio1, ngongori, ngori, parohe, pāroherohe, pōrorotua, ruhi, tīmohea, tūoi, tūpaku}

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka kaha kore, ka ngākau kore, kāore e hiahia kori ake i te ngenge, i te māuiui. Nā whai anō i ngoikore ai rātou. Ka hia kē nei ngā tau kāore e paku arohia ana tā rātou take. {ānewa, anuhea, ārangirangi, hauaitu, hauhauaitu1 (1), haumaruru, hōnene, hūnene2, iwingohe, koroukore, mae, maero1 (3), maninohea, ngongori, ngori, paruhi1 (3), parure, pōngenge, taharangi3 (1), takarure, tara1 (tara kore), tārure, tauwherū, tāwheta, toupiore, tūpaku, tūrohea, tūrowhea, waikauere, waikorohuhō, wairuhi, waitau, whakaparure, whakaroau, whakatairuhi}
