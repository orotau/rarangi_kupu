He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ara1

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. He takotoranga whenua whāiti ka takahia e te waewae, ka haerea rānei e te waka, e tae atu ai te tangata, te aha atu ki wāhi kē. Kotahi noa iho te ara atu i tēnei taha o te pae maunga ki tērā taha. {huanui1, huarahi, whakatika}

ara pikipiki a Tāwhaki [Papatūānuku] He tauira whai, e rua ngā kaikori, 12 ngā whiringa ka hua ake i waenganui ko te ara e piki ai a Tāwhaki ki te rangi, e waru atu ngā tauira ka hua mai i tēnei. Ka mahia e rāua te ara pikipiki a Tāwhaki, kotahi atu whiringa ko te korokoro o kiwi, kotahi atu anō ko te tākoko.

ara tamatāne [Tūmatauenga] He kāwai whakapapa heke iho i te taha tāne. Ko taku ara tama tāne tēnei, heke iho i te koroua o taku pāpā ki tōna pāpā, ki a ia tonu, tae noa mai ki ahau.  ure (ure tārewa)

2. [Papatūānuku] He ahunga ka whāia - taha tinana, taha hinengaro, taha mahi rānei, e tutuki ai tētahi mea. (i) Kaua e whāia tētahi ara takahi i te ture hei kimi utu mō tōna hara, kei mauheretia ko koe. (ii) Ka pai hoki te pānui pukapuka hei ara whakamahea i ngā whakaaro. {huarahi}

3. [Tāne] He mea hei hari i te tangata, i tētahi atu mea rānei ki tētahi atu wāhi. Kātahi ia ka haere ki te tiki ara mōna i tōna hungawai, homai ana ko Aotea hei waka mōna (W 13).

ara2

1. ‑ia ‑tia ‑hia ‑nga ‑tanga ‑hanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia neke whakarunga (tētahi mea). Kua arahia ake tā tātau haki kia kapakapa i te hau.  hiki (1)

2. ‑nga ‑tanga ‑hanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka piki, ka neke rānei ki runga. (i) Āwhea ara ai te marama? (W 13) (ii) Mate atu he tētē kura, ara ake he tētē kura (W 13). {hī1 (5), hiki, māhuahua, mahuta, maiangi, manana, totō, whakatairangaranga}

3. ‑nga ‑tanga ‑hanga [Tūmatauenga] Kāore e moe ana; e korikori ana te hinengaro, me te tinana anō pea. Kāore e kore kei te ara tonu te kuia rā, ahakoa e kati ana ngā whatu.  oho1 (3)

ara3

‑hanga ‑nga [Tāne] thu. He kupu tū motuhake e whakaatu ana i te mīharo, i te ohorere. Ka heua ake, ara, he tangata! (W 14)

ara4

[Tangaroa] ing. Te poto a te upokororo, he ika wai māori kua korehāhā. Prototroctes oxyrhynchus.  nehe2

arā1

[Tāne] hono. E hono ana tēnei kupu i ētahi rerenga e rua, me te tohu ko te mea tuarua he momo whakamārama, he momo whakapuaki tuarua rānei i te mea tuatahi. (i) Nāna hoki i tiki ngā kete o te wānanga i te Toi-o-ngā-rangi, arā i Tikitiki-o-rangi (KRK 43). (ii) E tika ai te whakahua i te reo Māori, kia tika te whakahua i ngā reta pakupaku, arā, i te a, e, i, o, u (KRK 49).

arā2

[Ranginui] wāhi. E tohu ana kei tētahi wāhi kē atu tētahi mea i te kaikōrero, i te kaiwhakarongo rānei. (i) Arā te miro hei puhipuhi manu mā tāua, kei te rae o te hiwi rā. (ii) Kua kite anō koe i aku hū? Arā, kei te kūaha. {arārā, arāraka, ira2, rā2}
