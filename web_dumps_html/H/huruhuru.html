He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



huruhuru

1. [Tāne] ing. Ngā weu i te tinana o te tangata, o te kararehe rānei. (i) Kāore te taiohi e hiahia kia kitea mai ōna huruhuru e tētahi atu. (ii) Kua hina haere a runga, engari kei te pango tonu ngā huruhuru o raro. (iii) He huruhuru hipi, he taewa, he poaka ngā utanga (WMA 1, 6). (iv) Kua wera ngā huruhuru o te kurī i te ahi.

huruhuru o Hinenuitepō [Tāne] He tipu toro nuku. Acaena anserinifolia.  piripiri (1)

huruhuru o ngā waewae o Paoa [Tāne] He huruwhenua whai kahiwi poto 1 mita te tupu. Leptopteris superba.  tētēkura (1)

huruhuru whenua [Tāne] He tipu kāore e whai pua, he huru manu te hanga o ngā rau, he tihenga atua kei te angamate o ngā rau.  huruwhenua

2. [Tangaroa] ing, āhua. He huru poto, he makiki, ka tipu i te kiri o te kararehe, i te kanohi rānei o te tāne. Kia tika te hunuhunu i ngā huruhuru o te poaka.  tarakina (1)

3. [Tāne] ing. He hanga papatahi ka tipu mai i te kiri o te manu, he tātā piako āhua mārō kei waenganui, he weu e rere ana i ngā taha e rua. (i) Me te huruhuru manu ka rere i te hau (W 72). (ii) He mea puhi te māhunga ki te huru kererū, me ngā huruhuru huia anō (KM 2, 2). {hau8, huru1 (2), rau1 (3), raukura, tā3 (2), whā2 (3)}

4. [Tāne] Te huinga weu e tipu ana i te upoko o te tangata. Ka āta herua ngā huruhuru o te tamaiti ki te heru niho iti hei tango mai i ngā kutu.  makawe (1)
