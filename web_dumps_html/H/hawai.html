He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hawai

1. [Tāne] ing. Te harore tūturu ka tipu i te rākau pirau. Pholiota adiposa.  harore (2)

2. [Papatūānuku] He rerenga wai māori tino iti e rere ana ki te awa, ki te kōawa, ki te roto, ki te moana rānei. He maha ngā hawai ka whāngai i te kōawa. {haemanga, kuikuinga, kuinga, manga}

3. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 30-40 henemita te tipu, he hāurauri ki te ōriwa te upoko, te tuarā me ngā kaokao, me te rere anō o ōna tāingoingo kōura, he hiriwa ōriwa a raro, he iraira kōura kei te pū o ngā paihau. Galaxias argenteus. {kokopara, taiwharu}

4. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-24 henemita te tipu. Gobiomorphus gobioides.  kōkopu (3)

5. [Tangaroa] āhua. Kāore i hōhonu te wai o roto (o te hāpua me te wairepo). E rapu kai ana te matuku māna i ngā wāhi hawai o te wairepo.
