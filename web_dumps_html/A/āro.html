He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



aro1

1. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka huri te ahunga kia tauaro ki tētahi mea. Me aro te kaikōrero ki te hunga turi, kia kite ai rātou i tōna waha e kōrero ana. {hei2, whakaarorangi}

2. ‑hia ‑tia ‑nga ‑hanga [Rongo] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka āta whakarongo ki tētahi kōrero, ki tētahi tohutohu. (i) Tērā ka mau koe i te pirihimana ki te kore e arohia e koe ngā ture taraiwa waka. (ii) Kaua noa e aro atu ki a ia - kei te amuamu ia mō te kore noa iho.

3. ‑ngia ‑hia ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka manako te ngākau ki tētahi mea, ki te mahi rānei i tētahi mahi. (i) Ehara te mako i te ika e arongia ana (W 16). (ii) I te pai o ngā kōrero a tana tuakana mō taua whenua, ka tae te aro ki a ia kia haere ki reira.  hiahia (1)

4. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhi e noho ai, e toko ake ai te whakaaro, te kare ā-roto. E kore te aroha koe puakina atu e au ki waho rā, me ruru tonu iho ki roto i taku aro mōkai (W 16).  hinengaro (1)

5. [Tāne] Ngā mōmona e uhi ana i ngā tākihi. Ka tau atu te kea ki te tuarā o te hipi ki te tīhaehae mai i te aro.

aro2

‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka mārama (ki te hinengaro). (i) Kāore i aro i a au ngā kupu a te rōia. (ii) Tē aro i a au te tikanga o tēnā kōrero - he hōhonu rawa. {aroā, arowā}

āro

‑tanga ‑hanga ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua meatia kia mau pai, kia mau tika ki te hinengaro. Maringi noa mai ngā kōrero o mua e āro ana i a ia.  mōhio (4)
