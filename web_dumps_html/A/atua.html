He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



Atua1

1. [Ranginui] ing. Te pō tekau mā whā o te marama. He kino mō ngā mahi katoa. Ka tūpou te marama ki te wai, he kino.  Ōhua (1)

Atua-whakahaehae [Ranginui] Te pō tekau mā whā o te marama. He kino mō ngā mahi katoa. Ka tūpou te marama ki te wai, he kino.  Ōhua (1)

2. [Ranginui] Te pō tekau mā rima o te marama. Ka pā te marama i te wai, he kino. Ki ētahi he āhua pai paku nei mō te ika. {(Atua-mate-o-hotu), Hotu, Ōhua, Ōturu, Turu}

Atua-mate-o-hotu [Ranginui] Te pō tekau mā rima o te marama. Ka pā te marama i te wai, he kino. Ki ētahi he āhua pai paku nei mō te ika.  Atua1 (2)

3. [Ranginui] Te pō tekau mā toru o te marama. He kino mō ngā mahi katoa, o uta, o tai. Ki ētahi he pai mō te ika, he kino mō te kai.  Māwharu (2)

Atua2

[Ranginui] ing. Te ariki wairua matua o te Hāhi Karaitiana, o ngā iwi o Iharaira, o Ihirama hoki. Ā ka kī te Atua, kia mārama: nā ka mārama (PT Ken 1.3).  atua (1)

atua

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua. He mana wairua whāioio tuauriuri nō te wāhi ngaro, kei reira te ora, kei reira te mate. (i) Ko Tāne te atua o te wao nui. (ii) Ko Hine-te-iwaiwa te atua o te Whare Pora. (iii) Whakahokia ai te ika tuatahi hei whakarata i te atua o te moana, i a Tangaroa.

2. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. Te wairua o te tangata kua mate, ka kitea, ka rangona rānei e te tangata ora. Ka tīkina mai māua ka pokea e te atua (W 289).  kēhua

3. [Whiro] He taniwha weriweri, he taniwha whakamataku, he taniwha whakatūpato. Ehara i a Hinepoupou tangata, ko Hinepoupou atua (W 20). {Aitu, tipua, tupua}

4. [Tūmatauenga] He mea e wehingia ana, nō te ao wairua tōna kaha. Whiwhi nei a Ngāti Raukawa i taua atua taiaha nei (W 20).

5. ‑tanga [Tāne] He mea kāore e pai ki te tangata, he hanga houhou. Tōku atua he tangata haurangi.

6. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E rerekē ana i tērā e mōhiotia nuitia ana, e kitea nuitia ana, e whakaaetia nuitia ana. He atua, te hia ora i te makariri (W 20).  korokē (3)

7. ‑tanga [Whiro] Kāore e tino pīrangi whakapau, hoatu rānei i tētahi mea ki tētahi atu. He moumou tāima te inoi i te tangata atua rā kia homai he koha mā te hunga kāpō.  matapiko (1)
