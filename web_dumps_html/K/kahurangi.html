Kahurangi

[Tūmatauenga] ing. hou. He hōnore nui ka ūhia e te Karauna ki runga i te wahine mō āna mahi whakahirahira. I tū te hui whakanui i ngā tāngata taumata rau i ūhia ki te taitara 'Tā', ki te Kahurangi rānei ki te whare o te Kāwana.

kahurangi

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore e tau ngā whakaaro i te kore e mōhio he aha ka pā; kāore e ū ngā whakaaro i te mea e māharahara ana. Haere kahurangi atu ana ki te uiuinga, hoki mauri tau mai ana.  ārangi

2. ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e taea te whakawhirinaki atu; he kōrero kē i te ahiahi, he mahi kē i te awatea. Ki te kī mai koe kei te haere mai koe, ā kāti haere mai! Kāore au e pai ki te tangata kahurangi!  hārekereke

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] E kaingākautia ana, e noho taonga ana ki te tangata. (i) He iti kahurangi (W 85). (ii) Taku hei kahurangi, e kore e maunu i taku kakī. {kura1 (2), māpuna1, marihi, matahīapo, puiaki, puipuiaki, tongarerewa, tongarewa}

4. ‑tanga [Ranginui] Rite ki te rangi i te rā paki te tae. He ātaahua ngā werewere kahurangi o te kōkako o Te Ika-a-Māui. {kikorangi, ōrangi, purū}

5. [Tangaroa] ing. He pounamu whakatīaho, he karera te tae, kāore ōna koha, ōna aha, paruhi kau ana. Nāku rawa i rere te whanga ki Kaiapohia ki te mōtoi kahurangi (W 85).

6. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He kupu e tohu ana i te tangata e aroha nuitia ana. Taku kōmata, taku kahurangi pīataata (W 131).  tau3 (3)

7. [Papatūānuku] He uri wahine nō ngā tātai rangatira. Ka tau taua ingoa a Kahurangi ki te tamaiti wahine a ngā mātua momo rangatira (W 85).
