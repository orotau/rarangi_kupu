He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hūhē

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua pau te hau, kua pau te kaha, mā te whakangā rawa, mā te moe rawa e whai kaha anō ai. E ono hāora ia e taraiwa ana, tae rawa ake ki te kāinga, he hūhē te kai.  ngenge1

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] Kua tino pau te hau - taha tinana, taha wairua rānei - i te whakapaunga kaha ki te mahi, i tētahi āhuatanga pēpēhi wairua rānei. Kua hūhē pai i te roa o te oma.  tūrohi (2)

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka raru te ngākau, ka hē te wairua i te mahinga o tētahi mahi hē, i te mōhiotia rānei o tētahi mahi e whakaarotia ana kāore i tika, kāore i pai. Ka hūhē noa iho tātou ki te kore e ora ā tātou manuhiri.  whakamā (2)
