He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōkōwai

1. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. He oneone i tahuna kia whero, ka konatua ki te hinu hei pani. (i) Ka takoto he kai a te tangata whenua ki tana aroaro, ka whakatakoto hoki ia i tana roi he mea pani rā ki te kōkōwai, kia kitea mai e te tangata whenua. He whakamōhio tērā ki ngā iwi ko tana haere he whakatūtū ope taua māna (IwiT 46:5). (ii) E rua ngā pō e noho ana i te kāinga, i te ata ka titiro atu a Pūroku, e piri ana te kōkōwai i te pāpāringa o Tupeteka, nō Parewhete. Kua mōhio a Pūroku kua taea te wahine rā e Tupeteka (Wh 3, 1). {hōrū, hōrua2, karamea, kura1 (5), pōrakaraka, tākou, tāreha}

2. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Rite ki te toto maroke te tae. (i) He huru kōkōwai kei te tuarā me te tou o te tieke. (ii) Kitea nuitia ai te kōkōwai, te pango me te mā i ngā kōwhaiwhai. {tūākura}
