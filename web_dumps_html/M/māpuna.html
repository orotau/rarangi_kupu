He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



māpuna1

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E kaingākautia ana, e noho taonga ana ki te tangata.  kahurangi (3)

hei māpuna [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He kupu e tohu ana i te tangata e aroha nuitia ana. Taku hei māpuna kua riro Titapu, ē (W 180).  ate1 (tau o te ate)

māpuna2

1. ‑tanga [Tangaroa] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka piki ake, ka pupū ake, ka toko ake (o te wai, o te roimata, o te kare ā-roto). (i) Homai me riringi ki te hua roimata e māpuna i aku kamo (W 180). (ii) I reira ka māpuna te aroha o te rangatira nei o Te Poihipi ki a Puhiwahine (TTR 2, 93). {kōhuhū, kōmanawa, pāhīhī, puhake, pūhakehake, puke2 (1), puna, pupuke}

2. ‑tanga [Tangaroa] Ka karekare ake he ngaru iti i te mata (o te wai, o te aha atu). He rawe te titiro ki ngā wai o te roto e māpuna kau ana i te matangi.  pakare

3. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka neke kia ngāwari noa, mai i tētahi taha ki tētahi, mai i mua ki muri rānei, me te mau anō o tētahi pito, me te mea nei rānei e mau ana tētahi pito. Rū ana te whenua, māpuna ana ngā whare teitei.  pīoioi2 (2)

4. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka rongo i te pōuri nui i te rironga o tētahi mea, pērā i te matenga o te tangata e aroha nuitia ana. Ki konā a Te Kehu māpuna ai ki te ata o te tau (W 180).  kōingo (2)

5. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka whakaputa i te hā kia roa tonu, kia rangona tonutia, hei tohu i te ngenge, i te hōhā, i te pōuri, i te tānga o te ihu, i te aha atu. Kua māpuna kau i te rongonga ka tōmuri anō te tereina.  mapu (3)
