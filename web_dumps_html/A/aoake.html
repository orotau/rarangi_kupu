He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



aoake

1. [Ranginui] tūkē. I te rā ka whai iho. (i) Kotahi rā i patua ai e ia e rua pā; aoake e toru pā; ao ake, e rua pā (W 11). (ii) Ka meatia ngā tuangi ki te pākete waitai kia tuhaina ai ngā onepū, aoake ka kainga. {ao1 (ao ake), aoinaake, aoināke, auinaake, auināke, auina rawa ake}

2. [Ranginui] I te rā tuarua ka whai iho. (i) Ka mekea atu tana karu, aoake, kua pango katoa. (ii) He rā whakatā āpōpō, aoake ka haere anō ngā whakataetae.  (aoake nui)

aoake nui [Ranginui] I te rā tuarua ka whai iho. (i) Ka noho ki Ōpōtiki, aoake nui, ka haere ki Te Aroha. (ii) Aoake nui, ka hoki mai aku mātua, nō reira he tahitahi whare te mahi nui āpōpō. {aoake, āwake}

aoake nui atu [Ranginui] I te rā tuatoru ka whai iho. (i) Aoake nui atu, kua ngingio haere ngā otaota i te ngau a te paitini. (ii) Aoake nui atu, ka riro te tūpāpaku ki te kōpū o te whenua.

3. [Ranginui] I te rā o mua atu. Kāore au e mōhio he aha i pūhore ai te haere i tēnei rā - aoake, he nui ngā ika i mau.

4. [Ranginui] E rua rā i mua atu. Aoake ka pāngia e te mate, i roto i te rua rā ka tino taumaha.  (aoake nui)

aoake nui [Ranginui] E rua rā i mua atu. (i) Nō te Wenerei ia i mate ai; aoake nui, kua mate ana roro i te aituā waka. (ii) Aoake nui ka hinga, hei āpōpō nehua ai. {aoake}

aoake nui atu [Ranginui] E toru rā i mua atu. Nō te Paraire i hinga ai. Aoake nui atu i haria ai ki te hōhipera.
