He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngū

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika tai e 20-25 henemita te roa o te tinana, e rua ōna kāwai roroa, e waru ōna kāwai poto, he tapatoru te hanga o te hiku, kia manawawera kua whero, kua kākāriki te kohiko mai, kia mate he parauri kiwikiwi te tae. Nototodarus sloanii, N.gouldi. {wheke}

2. [Tangaroa] He momo wheke nō tai hōhonu, ka tere te uwha i te kare o te wai me tōna anga mā, anga rauangi, he pakupaku noa iho te toa, 1.5 henimita te roa. Argonauta nodosa. {muheke, pū1 (8), pūpū2 (pūpū tarakihi)}

3. [Tangaroa] He anga mā ka tere i te kare o tai hōhonu, he kōmiro te hanga, 12-15 henemita te tupu, he pōngarengare e rere ana i ngā kaokao me te kōpako, ko te anga nei te putunga hua o te uwha ngū.

4. [Tangaroa] He ika tai e 45-50 henemita te tipu. Genyagnus monopterygius.  kourepoua

5. [Tūmatauenga] He tangata kāore e mōhio ki te kauhoe. Me whai pōito te ngū, kia kore ai e toromi.  karaka1 (karaka maoa)

6. [Rūaumoko] He tānga moko kei ngā taha o te ihu. Ka tāia te ihu ki te ngū, me te pōniania.

7. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Te wairua o te tangata kua mate, ka kitea, ka rangona rānei e te tangata ora.  kēhua

8. ‑tanga [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Kāore e kōrero, e hamumu te waha. I ngā pikitia ngū o mua, ka riro mā te kori o te tinana me te kanohi e kawe te whakaaro.  wahangū (2)

9. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tangi kia roa, kia mārū i te mea e mamae ana, e pōuri ana rānei. Ka ngaua koe e te mate, ka ngū koe (W 235).  aurere

10. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Kāore he reo kōrero. I te whānautanga mai o tana tamāhine he turi, he ngū.  wahangū (1)

11. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp. E tino kaha ana ki te kai me te kore e whakaaro ki ētahi atu. He tangata ngū - koinā e tautau nā te puku.  pukukai (1)
