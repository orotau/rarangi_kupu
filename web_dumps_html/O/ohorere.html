He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ohorere

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tupana te tinana i te tumeke, i te pānga o tētahi āhuatanga matawhawhati. (i) Kātahi ka tukuna mai he uira, he whatitiri, he ua, ā he hau nui whakaharahara, ā ngateri ana te whenua, ā ohorere ana te mauri o ngā mokopuna, ā tirotiro kau ana ki ētahi rākau hei whakaruru (T 1, 26). (ii) Ka puta mai tētahi tuna whakaharahara i tōna nohoanga ngaro i raro i ngā toka, ka haere mai ka pā ki tōna kiri, ka ohorere rawa ia i reira (WM 1, 39). {hiki, hoto1 (1), koemi, kohema, korohiti, korowhiti, kōwhiti, mahuki, ohomauri, ohotata, poupourere, pūoho, tupa, whiti3 (1)}

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Ka mīharo me te āhua tumeke anō, ka tumeke rānei me te āhua mīharo anō. (i) 'Kei a koutou katoa atu. He kau tahu tēnei, he taewa tahu ēnā, he kāpeti ēnā, he purini ēnā, he inu waireka, he tī, he kawhe. Mā koutou katoa atu, kāore he utu.' Ka ohorere mātau ki tēnei manaaki (HP 226). (ii) Ka kī mai ki a au, 'E tama, kāore au i te whakaae nō te mea he rangatira rawa koe mā taku tamāhine. Me moe anō te rangatira i te rangatira.' Ka ohorere hoki au ki tōna kōrero (HP 242).

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Inamata, i te kimonga kanohi (kua pā, kua puta tētahi āhuatanga, kua mahia rānei tētahi mahi), kāore he whakatūpato, kāore he whakamōhio tōmua. (i) He mate ohorere rawa tōna mate, nō te mea he tangata ora tonu ia tae noa mai ki te rā i mate ai (WM 11, 2). (ii) Koinei tetahi o ngā kāinga i tino kaha rawa te mate i te ohorere o te putanga mai o ngā wai (TT 33, wh21). (iii) I te ohorere tonu o taku haere ki Ingarani kore ana i taea e au te whakaputa aku mihi ki ngā tāngata o tōku rohe pōti (Ko 31, wh5). {kaikapo, matawhawhati, ohotata, whiti3 (5)}
