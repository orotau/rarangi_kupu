He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hanga

1. hangā ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakakotahi i ētahi mea, ka whakarerekē rānei i ētahi rawa, i tētahi rawa rānei, kia oti mai ai tētahi mea kē atu. (i) Ka hangaia tana whare ki te rākau, kia kore ai e tanuku ina rū te whenua. (ii) Ka hangaia te pune tote nei ki te tāwhaowhao, he mea āta tītipi ki te māripi.  mahi (2)

2. [Tāne] ing. Te waihanga o tētahi mea ki te titiro atu, arā, te waihanga o ōna tapa whakawaho. He porotaka te hanga o te poi.  āhua1 (2)

3. [Tūmatauenga] Te hua, ngā hua rānei ka puta i te whakapaunga kaha, i te hekenga o ngā werawera. Mīharo ana ngā hanga a te ringa rehe, ngā kete whakairo, ngā mahi toi!  mahi (3)

4. [Tāne] He taonga, he āhuatanga, he aha atu rānei kei tēnei ao e mōhio nei tātou; koia anō te kupu ka whakamahia hei tohu i tērā kāore e taea ana e te kaikōrero te āta whakaingoa, hei aha noa iho rānei te whakaingoa. Kātahi ka pōkaikai i ā mātau hanga (HP 85).  mea1 (1)

ehara i te hanga [Tāne] kīw. He kōrero e whakaahua ana i te mīharo, i te ahurei o tētahi mea. Ehara i te hanga! Harawiniwini ana te kiri i te mātaotao (W 167).

hanga kino [Papatūānuku] ing. Te pū (i te tinana wahine, i te tinana uwha) atu i te kauraho ki te kōpū.  tara6

5. [Tūmatauenga] He mea kei te tangata e mau ana, nāna ake, nōna ake rānei. Koia te whakarite a tō rāua kuia mō ana hanga, ko te whare ki te tuakana, ko ngā mea o roto ki te taina.  rawa1 (1)

6. [Tāne] He huinga tāngata ka taka ki raro i te karangatanga kotahi. Kei roto te hanga tautoko e pakipaki ana, e umere ana, kei waho te hanga mautohe e kaioraora ana.  hunga1 (1)

7. [Tāne] Ngā manga me ngā rau e noho kōpuni mai ana i te pito whakarunga o te kahiwi o te rākau; te pito whakarunga o te rākau. Ka tuaina te rākau, ka haria te kahiwi, waiho ana te hanga kia popo, kia hanehane.  kāuru (1)
