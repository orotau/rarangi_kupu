He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngākihi

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He mātaitai pōtae pāpaku te hanga, e 5 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki taihua, he tāhei kiwikiwi, he porotea e awhe ana i te anga, he tāhekeheke parauri e heke ana i te tumuaki ki ngā tapa. Cellana radians. {kākihi}

2. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae hōhonu te hanga, e 5 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki taihua, he tāhei pango, he pukupuku mā e awhe ana i te anga. Cellana ornata. {kākihi}

3. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae te hanga, e 6 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki taihua, he tāhei teatea, he karaka e awhe ana i te anga. Cellana flava. {kākihi}

4. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae te hanga, e 5 henemita te tupu, he piri ki raro toka ki tai takoto, he pakini kei tētahi pito o te anga pākā kōrito, he niho ririki kei te tapa. Tugali elegans.

5. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai whai kokopi, tipirori te hanga. Saccostrea cucullata.  tio (2)

ngākihi awaawa [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae te hanga, e 3 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki taihua, he awa kei tētahi taha o te anga, he mā he poroporo ōna whakapaipai. Siphonaria australis.

ngākihi hahae [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae te hanga, e 2.5 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki tai takoto haere ake, he haenga kei tētahi pito o te anga pākā kōrito. Emarginula striatula.

ngākihi hiwihiwi [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pāengaenga te hanga, e 4 henemita te tupu, he piri toka, he piri kūtai rānei ki tua o tai takoto, he tāwakawaka e heke ana i te anga parauri, he pūngarungaru ngā tapa. Crepidula costata.

ngākihi kōpia [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pāengaenga te hanga, e 3 henemita te tupu, he piri toka, he piri kūtai rānei ki tua o tai takoto, he parauri a waho whai kiri pākā kōrito, he mā a roto, he porohita te hanga. Sigapatella novaezelandiae. {(ngākihi kōpio)}

ngākihi kōpio [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pāengaenga te hanga. Sigapatella novaezelandiae.  (ngākihi kōpia)

ngākihi pō [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pōtae te hanga, e 3 henemita te tupu, he piri toka i ngā paringa a tai aronui, he tāhei pango e huri ana i te tapa whakaroto. Notoacmea pileopsis.

ngākihi tea [Tangaroa] He mātaitai pāengaenga te hanga, e 2.5 henemita te tupu, he piri anga ki tua o tai takoto, mā tāna e piri atu ai e tohu ka pēhea te āhua o tana anga mā. Crepidula monoxyla.
