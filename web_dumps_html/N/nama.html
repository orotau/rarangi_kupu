nama

1. [Tāne] ing. reo kē. He tohu (pērā i te 1, i te 2), he kupu rānei (pērā i te 'tahi', i te 'rua') ka whakamahia hei tatau i ētahi mea. He aha te nama kei runga i tō tīkiti?  tau5

2. ‑hia ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahw, āhua. reo kē. He pepa e whakaatu ana i ngā rawa i hokona, i ngā mahi rānei i mahia, me te utu e tika ana kia utua e te kaihoko mō aua mea. (i) Kua tae mai te nama a te kāmura mō ana mahi whakahou i te whare. (ii) Ka kī te ture, me utu e koe ō nama, ki te kore ka murua ko āu taonga hei whakaea, ka whiua rānei ko tō tinana ki te herehere, hei whakakaha i a koe ki te kimi huarahi e ea ai tō nama (TT 29, wh7). (iii) I hē tana nama mai i a au. {kaute, pire1}

noho nama [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Kāore anō i utu i tētahi nama, kāore anō i whakahoki i tētahi whakaaro. (i) Kei te noho nama tonu au ki a ia - kāore anō kia whakahokia atu te rima tekau tāra nāna i homai i tērā wiki rā. (ii) Kua noho nama ahau ki a koe mō tō āwhina nui (PH 6, 22).
