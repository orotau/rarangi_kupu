He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kauae

1. [Tāne] ing. Te kōiwi kei te wāhi whakararo o te kanohi, e mau mai ana ngā niho o raro. (i) Ko te kauae o tōna tupuna, o Murirangawhenua, kua riro mai koa i mua atu, koia raka tāna patu (N 13). (ii) Nā te kaha o te meke a tōna hoa whawhai kua whati tana kauae. {kauwae, paewai}

kauae raka [Whiro] He mate rere ka hua ake i te urutomo mai a tētahi momo huakita i te tinana, mā roto i tētahi tūnga, e kikī ai, e hukihuki ai ngā uaua.  (kauae timu)

kauae timu [Whiro] He mate rere ka hua ake i te urutomo mai a tētahi momo huakita i te tinana, mā roto i tētahi tūnga, e kikī ai, e hukihuki ai ngā uaua. (i) Ki te tū tō waewae i te whao waikura, me haere kia werohia ki te rongoā kia kore ai koe e pāngia e te kauae timu. (ii) Arā anō tētahi ingoa Pākehā mō te kauae timu, ko te 'kauae raka'. {(kauae raka)}

kauae raro [Tāne] Ngā kōrero o te wānanga tapu e pā ana ki ngā mea o te whenua. Nā te iwi whānui o Hauraki a ia i tangi i runga i te mōhio kua whakangaro atu nei a ia, kua ngaro anō hoki tētehi wāhi rahi tonu o te toi kauaerunga me te toi kauaeraro, ā, tē taea te whakahoki mai (TTR 3, 234). {kauwae (kauwae raro)}

kauae runga [Tāne] Ngā kōrero o te wānanga tapu e pā ana ki ngā mea o te rangi. Ko ngā mana poropiti, ko ngā mana tohunga ngā kaha o Hikapuhi, i āhei ai āna tūroro ki ngā tikanga o te kauaerunga o te Māori, ki te whakaora i ō rātau wairua, whakaū hoki i te tūmanako (TTR 3, 55). {kauwae (kauwae runga)}

2. [Tāne] Te wāhi whakararo o te kanohi kei raro iho i te waha, kei runga ake i te kakī. Tokorima anō ngā wāhine i tāia ngā kauae, ko te nuinga he pangore katoa (T 1, 26). {kauwae, pūkauae, pūkauwae}

kauae tehe [Rūaumoko] ing, āhua. He wahine kua tāia te kauae. Ko te wahine i whakaahuatia e ia, he kauae tehe, mau rāpaki, e kai paipa ana i waho i tana kāuta.

3. [Rūaumoko] ing. He tānga moko kei te kauwae. {kauwae, pūkauae, pūkauwae}

4. [Tāne] He hanga roa tonu, he mātotoru tonu, he tino kaha, ka hangā ki te rākau ki te maitai rānei, hei tautoko i ngā wāhanga taumaha o te whare.  kauwae (4)

5. [Tūmatauenga] Te parirau o te ope taua. E rua ngā wāhanga o te taua, kotahi kauae i haere mā te uru, kotahi kauae i haere mā te rāwhiti. {kauwae, pākau, pākaukau}

6. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. Te patunga ohorere i te hoariri e tētahi ope e noho huna ana; te ope nāna tēnei mahi. (i) Ko te kauae te tikanga pai katoa hei patu i te hoariri i rō ngahere. (ii) Ka tae ki te uru kahikatea, kei runga te kauae me ā rātou pū, tumeke tonu atu ngā hōiho o te hoariri.  kokoti (6)

7. [Whiro] He kōrero kāore i pono. Nā wai ēnā kauae i whakaputa? Nā tama horihori?  parau1 (kōrero parau)
