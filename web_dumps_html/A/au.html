He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



au1

1. [Rūaumoko] ing. He hanga rite ki te kōrehu ka rewa ake ki te rangi, ka tāiri mai rānei ki te rangi ina wera tētahi mea. Ka puia ōku kanohi i te au (W 20).  auahi

2. [Ranginui] ing, āhua. He huinga tōtā ka kitea e te karu, e rere ana i te rangi.  kapua1

3. [Ranginui] He huinga pata wai moroiti mano tini ka iri mai ki te hau takiwā, ka pātata tonu ki te mata o te whenua te iri mai. (i) Ātaahua ana te tāiri mai o te au i runga ake i te roto i te ata nei. (ii) Kua kore ngā waka rererangi e rere i te kino o te au.  kohu1

4. [Tangaroa] He wāhanga i tētahi takotoranga wai, takotoranga aha atu rānei, e ū ana ki tētahi ahunga, e kaha kē atu ana te rere i ētahi atu wāhi. Pere taku titiro te au kai te moana o Tūhua i waho (NM1 84, 9).  ia3 (1)

au o te moana [Tangaroa] ing. He horanga moana nui tonu e tawhiti ana i te whenua. E rima rā rātou e pōteretere ana i te au o te moana, ka kitea.  aumoana

5. [Tangaroa] He ara wai karekare ka mahue ki muri i te waka haere moana. E kaikainga ana te tahatika e te au nui o ngā waka e whakawhiti ana i Te Moana-o-Raukawa. {kōrinorino, kōriporipo}

6. [Tangaroa] He wāhi i tētahi awa e tino pōkarekare ana, e tino tere ana te rere o te wai. Rere pāhukahuka ana te wai i te au o te awa.  tāheke (5)

7. [Tangaroa] He rerenga wai tere tonu te āmiomio, he au kukume tōna e romia ai ngā mea ka tūpono atu ki roto i a ia. Ka kāwhakina e te au kume, e te au rona, o te au hīrere, hīrere ki te pō (W 20).  ripo (1)

8. [Tāne] He weu kua kōmiroa, kua whiria kia ū, kia roa, kia kōrahirahi. Tuia ki runga ki te au kotahi (W 20).  aho1 (1)

9. [Tāne] He titi ka hangā ki te wheua, ki te rei, ki te rākau, ki te anga, ki te pounamu rānei hei here i te kākahu. He au rei katoa te here o ngā kākahu (W 20). {mania}

au ika [Tūmatauenga] He kōiwi mō te kaui ika. Ki te kore taku au ika ka roa tonu au e kaui ana i ngā ika nei. {autui, auwai}

au mārō [Tūmatauenga] He ngākau māia ki te whawhai. I rongonui te au mārō o ngā hōia Māori i ngā pakanga o te ao.

au2

[Tāne] tkp. Te kupu matua ka whakamahia e te kaikōrero hei tohu i a ia anō, hei kōrero mōna ake. Ko ia te tuakana, ko au te teina.  ahau

au3

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua tūturu te piri, kua mārō rānei te tū, he mahi nui e neke ai, e turakina ai, e whakakorea ai. Au rawa taku noho (W 21).  mau2 (1)

2. ‑tanga [Rongo] Ka hōhonu tonu (te moe), kāore e rite tonu te oho ake. I taku kaha ngenge me te pai anō o te moenga, au ana taku moe.

au4

1. ‑tanga ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua, maho. Ka whakaputa (te kurī) i tētahi oro kaha, āhua rite ki te pakō, ki te pakē rānei. Ka au te kurī hei whakatūpato i tana rangatira he rāwaho e whakaeke ana i tōna kāinga.  auau3

2. ‑tanga ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka auē, ka tangi tīkapa mō tētahi wā roa nei pērā i te kurī. Ka tangi, me te au kurī te rite (N 170).  ngawī2 (1)

āu

[Tāne] pmu. Te takitini o te kupu tāu, e tohu ana nā te kaiwhakarongo, te kaipānui rānei ngā mea ka whakaingoatia i muri tonu mai, i mua tonu atu rānei, ā, nō te rōpū-'a' aua mea. (i) He aha āu nā whakamārama mō te kupu nei? (ii) Nōnahea i titoa ai ēnei tuhinga āu? (iii) He poto ake āku kōrero i āu. {āhau1}
