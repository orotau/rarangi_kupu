He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōaka

1. [Rongo] ing. He hue pakari kua oti te karu te tango ake, whakamahia nuitia ai hei kawe wai.  tahā

2. [Tāne] He whāriki kōkau hei hora ki te papa. Ka māngere te wahine ki te raranga whāriki, takapau, kōaka, ka kīia, 'He uri nō Hinerangi pakihore' (KRK 141).  kaitaka1 (2)

3. [Tāne] He rākau e 50 mita te tupu. Dacrycarpus dacrydioides.  kahikatea

4. [Papatūānuku] He whārua tino whāiti, he tino hōhonu, he poupou ngā tahataha. Ka whakaaro ia ki te haere ki roto i tetahi kōaka ururua kimi haere ai i te tia kua tū i te matā. {parari}

5. [Rūaumoko] He haenga, he matiti e rere ana i te whenua. He kōaka e rere ana i te whenua i ngā mahi a te rū.

6. [Papatūānuku] He takotoranga whenua roroa, he whāiti, he puke e rārangi ana i ngā taha e rua, tērā tonu pea he awa e rere ana i waenganui. Tō rātau urunga atu ki roto ki te kōaka o Te Kōpane, ka wepua ngā ope a Kōpū rāua ko Whaanga e te taumahatanga o te pupuhi o ngā pū a te hoariri, ā, ka noho pōnānā, rangirua katoa rātau (TTR 1, 393).  whārua (1)
