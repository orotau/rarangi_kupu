He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kora

1. ‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tāmutumutu te whakaata, te whakaputa rānei i te aho. Ka whakahāngaitia te rama ki te rākau, ka kitea ngā karu o te paihamu e kora mai ana.  kānapanapa (1)

2. [Tāne] ing. He wāhanga tino iti ka whati mai, ka pakaru mai, ka motu mai rānei i tētahi mea. He rawe ki a ia kia kapi katoa ana hēki i ngā kora pepa.  kongakonga (2)

3. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He kōiraira tino iti, he wāhanga tino iti rānei (o tētahi mea). Kua kōiraira katoa te tuanui i te kora hamuti ngaro.  tongi (2)

4. ‑nga [Rūaumoko] ing, mahp, āhua. He maramara iti wera ka puhaina mai i tētahi mea e kā ana, ka takoto mai rānei ina tata poko te ahi, ka puta ake rānei ina hikaia ētahi mea mārō e rua. (i) Kia rere ko te kora ki runga te rarauhe (W 139). (ii) Me kai e au te kora o Mahuika i hunuhunua ai tō kiri haepapa (IwiT 50:21).  korakora (1)

5. [Rūaumoko] ing, āhua. Te mura, te wera me te auahi anō pea ka puta ake ina kā tētahi mea. Nā Mahuika i whai kora ai tātou, te tangata.  ahi1

6. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He tangata kore mana. Kaua e kōwhiria he kora hei kanohi mō tō kaupapa - me kimi kē he tangata whai mana, he tangata ingoa nui.  kurumetometo

korā

[Tāne] wāhi, ing. He wāhi e tawhiti ana i te kaikōrero me te kaiwhakarongo. (i) Kei korā te mīti e whata ana i tērā rākau (Wh 6, 1). (ii) Arā kē a Mea, kei korā.
