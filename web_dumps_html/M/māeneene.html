He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



māeneene

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore he pukupuku, kāore he wāhi taratara, he kōmāmā ki te ringa, me te mōhinuhinu anō pea. (i) Ko tana kiri māeneene, me te karengo nei te rite. (ii) Nāna nei i tāoro ngā wāhi taratara, waiho ana he ara māeneene (PH 3, 11). (iii) I whiriwhiria anō ētahi kōhatu māeneene e rima māna i roto i te awa (PT 1Ha 17.40). {newanewa, ngerungeru}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka pā he āhuatanga ki te kiri e puta ai te hiahia ki te rakuraku i taua wāhi. I te ngārara nei e kari ana i tōna huarahi ki roto i te kiri, ka māeneene te kiri, ka tīmata te raraku a te tamaiti (TT 72, wh 640).  mangeo1 (1)

3. [Tāne] ing. He harakeke āhua roa tonu, he pīngore ngā whā, he parauri ngā tapa me te kāmata, he harakeke raranga, he pai mō te raranga whāriki. Phormium tenax.
