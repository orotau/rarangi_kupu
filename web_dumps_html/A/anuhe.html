anuhe

1. [Tāne] ing. Te punua oreore a te pūrerehua. Ka mutu te kai a te anuhe i te kāpeti, ka hoki ki tana tūngoungou, moe ai, ā huri rawa hei pūrerehua.  torongū (1)

2. [Tāne] Te torongū kai rau kūmara a te hīhue. Agrius convolvuli. {āwhato, āwheto, hōtete, huarangi1, kauā, kauwaha, mūharu, mūwharu, ngūharu, ngurengure}
