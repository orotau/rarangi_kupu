He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



more

1. [Tāne] ing. He pūtake kōekoeko tipu torotika ki roto i te oneone, koia nei te pū e tipu ai ngā piakaaka. (i) He more te kāroti. (ii) He kōpura te ingoa o te more o te tī kōuka. {kōmore, kōmoremore, tāmore}

2. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Te wāhi kei te mutunga, te wāhi tino tawhiti rānei o tētahi mea - kāore he wāhanga ōna i tua atu. E tā, e tautau ana te hūpē i te more o tō ihu.  pito

3. [Tāne] Ngā manga me ngā rau e noho kōpuni mai ana i te pito whakarunga o te kahiwi o te rākau; te pito whakarunga o te rākau.  kāuru (1)

4. [Papatūānuku] He pito whenua teitei e toro atu ana i te tahatika ki te moana, ki te roto rānei. Ka whakatūria he tīramaroa ki waho atu o te more hei whakatūpato i ngā waka e hipa atu ana i te pō.  mātārae (1)

5. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E noho kau ana, kāore he mea e whakakākahu ana, e whakapaipai ana i a ia. Ko ngā pou o te tomokanga he pou more, kāore he whakaniko, he aha o runga. {hura1 (6), māmore, momore, tāmore}

6. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Kore niho. Me hīroro rawa te āporo e taea ai e te tangata more te kai. {(niho more), ngore3 (niho ngore)}

niho more [Tāne] Kore niho. Ka haere te wahine niho more kia mahia mai he niho kēhua mōna.  more (6)

7. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Kua kore i koi te mata. Kua more noa ngā kutikuti i te nui o te onepū e mau ana ki te wūru o ngā hipi.  pūhuki

niho more [Tāne] ing. Te tumu noa iho o tētahi niho, arā, kua kore katoa te wāhi o runga ake. Ka mahi te tākuta ki te tango i te niho, ka whati, mahue ana ko te niho more.
