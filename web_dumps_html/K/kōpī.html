He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kopi

1. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tūtaki, ka piri tahi ngā wāhanga e rua (o tētahi mea) e hono ana ki te kaurori, ki te pona rānei, ka kati. (i) Kua kore he kōrero, kua kopi te waha. (ii) Me waiho ngā waewae o te arawhata kia kopi tonu, ka whakawhirinaki noa atu ki te pakitara.

2. ‑nga [Tāne] Kua tutuki (tētahi mahi), kua tae ki tōna mutunga tūturu, kāore he wāhi e toe tonu ana hei mahi. (i) He kōrero anō hei whakauru atu e kopi ai te rangahau. (ii) Kotahi atu anō te pou kia mahia, ana kua kopi te wāhi ki ngā whakairo.  oti

3. ‑nga [Tāne] Kua tūtakina (te kūaha), kua pāia rānei (tētahi momo ara), kua kore rānei (tētahi mea) i tuwhera. (i) Kāore e taea e te hōiho ngā nati nā ki te kopi tonu tō ringa - me whewhera. (ii) Ka tū au i reira, ka tuohu taku māhunga, ka kopi aku kanohi, ka inoi whakamoemiti au ki Te Atua, mōku kua tae mai ki tēnei wāhi tapu (HP 155).  kati1 (5)

4. [Papatūānuku] ing. He whārua whāiti, he hōhonu, he poupou ngā tahataha, he rerenga awa. E ngunguru ana te wai i ngā tāheke o te kopi.  tāwhārua (2)

5. [Tāne] hou. He tohu rite ki te tongi te hanga ka noho mai ki te mutunga o te rerenga kōrero. Ka kite koe i te kopi ka mōhio koe kua mutu tēnā rārangi kōrero.  irakati

6. [Tāne] He taputapu rite ki te pona e hono ana i ētahi wāhanga e rua, e taea ai tētahi mea (pērā i te kūwaha, i te taupoki) te huaki, te kati; he hanga pēnei e hono ana i ngā wāhanga e rua o ētahi mātaitai pērā i te pipi, i te tuangi. Kua waikuratia te kopi, kua uaua te huaki i te taupoki.  kokopi (2)

kopī

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. E ngohe ana, e tārewa kau ana; kāore e mārō, e tū mārō rānei.  pingohe

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka kaha kore, ka ngao kore.  ngoikore (1)

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E mataku ana, e āmaimai ana, e whakamā ana rānei i mua i ētahi atu. Me āta whakangungu te hunga kopī kia māia ake.  kōpīpī (3)

4. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kāore anō i pakari, i maoa, i rite hei kai (o te huawhenua me ētahi atu mea). He kopī rawa ēnā, waiho kia eke ki te rahi e tika ana.  waitau (2)

kōpī

1. [Tāne] ing. He rākau 15 mita te tupu. Corynocarpus laevigatus.  karaka1 (1)

2. [Tāne] Te hua o te kōpī kua kino i te tutunga ki te wai.
