He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



koko1

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He taputapu whai kakau, he arearenga pāpaku kei te pito, he taputapu hei whāngai kai ki te waha, hei tohatoha, hei kōrorirori kai, hei aha atu, hei aha atu. Nō Rarotonga ana koko huamata, he mea mahi mai i te anga kōkōnati.  pune

koko kahawai [Tāne] He kupenga hei koko mai i te kahawai i te wai. Ka tango ake ki te koko kahawai hei koko ake i tana ika ki te waka. {tīkoko}

koko o te pokohiwi [Tāne] Tētahi o ngā iwi papatahi rahi e rua he tapatoru te hanga, kei te wāhi whakarunga o te tuarā tangata. He karukaru noa iho tana hingareti, ka kitea te koko o te pokohiwi.  hakikoko

2. [Rongo] He taputapu kakau roa hei keri, hei ahu whenua, hei koko rānei; he whānui tonu te mata, he hakoko, ka koeko haere te pito. I tangohia ngā kōhatu o te hāngī ki te koko. {hango, hāwara, tākoko} koko1 (3)

3. ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka keri ake, ka kapo ake ki ngā ringaringa, ki tētahi taputapu hakoko rānei. (i) E koko ana te wahine i ngā hamuti o tana kurī (Ng 461). (ii) Ka kokoa ake e ngā tamariki te hukarere mā ō rātou ringa. {awhe, kaku, kakukaku, ope}

4. ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tango ake, ka kume whakarunga. He taimaha te punga nei - māu au e āwhina ki te koko.  huti (1)

koko2

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. He wāhi whānui i te takutai e toro whakaroto ai te moana. Mā runga waka tere ia whakawhiti ai i te koko o te Whitianga ki te urupā o Tokatā, nehua ai (TTR 4, 27).  whanga (1)

2. [Tāne] Te wāhi e tūtaki ai ētahi mata, ētahi tapa e rua, neke atu. Nekehia ngā tūru rā ki ngā koko o te rūma kia pai ai tā tātou kanikani.  kokonga

koko3

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka rere ki te rangi, ka tauhōkai noa rānei i te rangi. E koko ana te manu, he tongi noa i te rangi.  topa (2)

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka āta neke haere i te hau takiwā (o te manu, te waka rererangi, tētahi atu hanga whai parirau rānei). Kua whati te parirau o te toroa - e kore rā e taea e ia te koko.  rere (1)

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tangaroa] āhua, ing, mahp. Kua mutu te pari mai (o te tai), kua tū mai ki tōna taumata. Kia koko ake te tai, kua maunu mai tō tāua waka i ngā toka nei.  aronui (5)

tai koko [Tangaroa] ing. He tai teka tino nui ka pā i muri tonu mai o Ōturu me Whitireia, hei konei kitea ai te tai rere.  tai1 (tai toko)

koko4

‑nga ‑tanga [Haumia] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e nui te kiko, te mōmona rānei i te tinana. Kua koko i te kaha kōingo ki te tau kua motu ki tawhiti.  tūpuhi (2)

kokō

‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa oro pērā i te wai e pupū ana, e koromiomio ana. (i) Kokō ana tōku puku i te hiakai. (ii) Kokō ana tōku manawa (W 121). (iii) I mua i tana matenga, ka kokō te korokoro, ka hukahuka te waha.

kōkō1

1. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Rongo] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kari, ka ono kai rānei ki te kō. Me kōkō aku tinaku (W 121).

2. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tango ake (i tētahi mea) i te whenua, mā te tapahi haere me te nukunuku i te oneone. Tō tāne i moe, he tāne kōkō para, he tāne kari aruhe (W 121).  kari1 (2)

3. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka hopu kehe mā te ā i ngā ika nei ki te pou, kia whai haere i ngā kawa ki roto i tētahi kupenga e pupuritia ana e tētahi atu. Ki te tae mai te manuhiri i te pō, ki te kore he kīnaki ka haere a Heremāia rāua ko tana wahine ki te kōkō kehe (Pi 52, wh9).

4. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] Ka hahae i te kiri i te tāinga ki te moko. He rerekē ngā momo uhi hei kōkō i te kiri.

5. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He pou hei ā i te kehe. Homai te kōkō ki a au, māu e haere ki te pupuri i tā tāua kupenga.

kōkō2

1. [Tāwhirimātea] ing, āhua. Te hau takiwā e rere noa ana i te mata o te whenua. Pai ana te whakatopa a te manu taratahi rā runga i te kōkō.  hau1 (1)

2. [Tāne] ing. He manu noho wao, noho ngahere. Prosthemadera novaseelandiae.  tūī

kōkō iti [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te tamaiti tamariki katoa o te whānau kia whānau mai. He pango kerekere te mātāmua, taka mai ki te kōkō iti, he tino urukehu.  pōtiki (1)

kōkō tatakī [Tūmatauenga] He kaikōrero kakama te hinengaro, he mōhio anō ki te whakatakoto i te kupu kia ngahau tonu.  Kupu whakarite. E rekareka ana te hui i ngā tārainga kupu a te kōkō tatakī.

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahp, āhua. He waiata kia noho ohooho tonu ai ngā tūtei o te pā, hei whakaohooho rānei i te pā i ngā wā o te riri. Ko Kanoa e whakaaraara ana, e kōkō ana (W 121).  whakaaraara (1)
