He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hui1

1. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka haere katoa ki te wāhi kotahi mō tētahi kaupapa kua whakaritea. I hui puku ngā minita inanahi nei, ā, ko te whakatau, kia panaia tō rātou kaihautū. {huihui, huirua1, ikapahi, kaputi, karahui, karapinepine, karawhiti, kerepinepine, mekemeke2, mene1, mine, pōkai, rāmemene, ramene, rāpoi, rukuruku, rūmene, rūnā, tāmene, whāiti, whakaopeti, whakarauika, whakawhāiti, whatiwhati}

2. ‑nga [Tāne] Ka tūpou, ka tūturi rānei kia koromemenge rawa te tinana. Hui ake ngā turi ki porokakī, e tuohu kau tātou (W 67).  koropeke

3. ‑a ‑nga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakanoho mai (i ētahi mea) kia piri tētahi ki tētahi. Hei te patunga kaua e huia katoatia te witi, engari me wehe kē ngā mea pai me ngā mea kino, me pēke kē tētahi, me pēke kē tētahi (WMA 6, 15).  āpiti (1)

4. [Tāne] ing. He tāngata tokomaha ka whakawhāiti mai ki tētahi wāhi mō tētahi kaupapa kua whakaritea. Whakaae ana te hui ko Hare hei tumuaki hou mō rātou. {huihuinga, minenga, pūrangi, whakaminenga}

5. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te whakawhāititanga mai o ētahi tāngata ki tētahi wāhi mō tētahi kaupapa kua whakaritea. Ka tū te hui whakawhanaunga ki tō tāua marae ā te 15-17 o Kohitātea. {huihuinga}

hui2

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka tāhae, ka kāhaki i ngā hanga a ētahi atu i te wā o te pakanga. Ki te hui koe i taku kākahu e kore e mahue i a au (W 67).

2. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka hopu (i te tangata) ka pupuri ai kia kore ai e oma. Rere ā-manu ki te hui mātāngohi (W 67).

hui3

‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tākiri (tētahi wāhanga o te tinana) i runga i te ohotata. He tohu kino. Kei te hui te karu o Rangi, ānō nei kei te kimokimo mai ia ki a au.  korowhiti (2)
