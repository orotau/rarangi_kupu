He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



motu1

1. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua tapahia rawatia, kua oti te hahae kia takoto wehe i tētahi atu mea. (i) Ka tapahia te arero, ka motu (W 211). (ii) Haumia ai ngā waero motu o ngā rēme hei kai. {tōkari}

2. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kua wehea kia noho motuhake mai i te wāhanga matua, i te nuinga, i tētahi rōpū, i tētahi atu hanga rānei. Ka motu ngā tamariki me ō rātou mātua kia whai wā ai ia whakatipuranga ki te kōrero tahi.  tauwehe (1)

3. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] maho, āhua. Kua neke ki tawhiti. (i) Ka motu ki Hawaiki (W 211). (ii) Ka motu ki tawhiti atu i Hana Tomingo tō rātau kaipuke, kātahi ia ka puta ake ki waho o taua kāho (PH 6, 7).

4. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] maho, ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Kua oma i tētahi wāhi e mauheretia ana, e pupurihia ana, kua tukua rānei kia haere. (i) Me te weka ka motu i te māhanga (W 211). (ii) Ka motu ngā wāhine me ngā tamariki, engari ka mauheretia tonutia ngā mea tāne.

5. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] maho, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka piere mai, ka takakinotia te kiko, i te ngau o tētahi hanga koi pēnei i te toki, i te māripi, i te aha atu rānei ki a ia. (i) Kei motu koe i te toki nā (W 211). (ii) Ko te āhua nei, i a au e reti haere atu ana i runga i taku puku, ka reti atu au mā runga i ētahi pātara pākarukaru, i ētahi tini rānei. Heoi, kua motu a runga i taku waewae mauī (HP 21).

6. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. Tētahi hanga e tawhiti ana i ētahi atu, e wehe ana i ētahi atu. E noho kotahi nei tōna motu, ko ia anake (W 211). {pūrei}

7. [Papatūānuku] He pito whenua e karapotia ana e te wai. Ko Te Ika-a-Māui tētahi o ngā motu matua o Aotearoa, ko Te Waka-a-Māui tētahi.  moutere

8. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He huinga rākau e tū tahi ana, e tū motuhake mai ana i ētahi atu. Āia atu ngā kau nei ki te taiapa e tū mai rā te motu tōtara.  uru1 (5)

motu2

‑nga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Te iti o te pāmahana, ina whakaritea ki ētahi atu mea; te mahana kore. Hei ngā pō ka haere a Hine ki Tokatā ki uta, ki te tiki wai mō rāua i te wai i Taranaki, he wai motu (KRK 25).  makariri (1)

mōtū

[Papatūānuku] ing. He momo kōhatu taumaha hei mahi māhē mō te kupenga me te aho. Kāore he take o te kōhatu māmā hei mahi māhē, me kōhatu taumaha tonu pērā i te mōtū.
