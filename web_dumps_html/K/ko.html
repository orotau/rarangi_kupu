He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ko

1. [Tāne] ptm. Ka tū i mua i te ingoa, i te tūkapi, i te tūingoa, hei whakakaha, hei whakawhāiti rānei i te aronga ki taua mea. (i) Ko Roimata taku ingoa. (ii) Ko rāua ngā mea tika hei kawe i tērā mahi. (iii) Kei te pai ngā kaimahi - me pana kē ko te rangatira.

2. [Tāne] Ka whakawhāiti i te aronga ki tētahi mea kua tohua whānuitia i mua tata atu. (i) Tērā anō tētahi pā nui onamata, ko Maungawhau (W 121). (ii) Ngā ingoa o ngā waka nei nā, ko Te Arawa . . . ko Tokomaru, ko Matawhaorua (W 121). (iii) Koinei te karakia e kīia nei ko te tuora.

3. [Tāne] Ka whai mai i ngā tūkapi takirua me ngā tūkapi takitini ina āta whakaingoatia ngā mea e tohua rā ki aua tūkapi. (i) Kei te pēhea kōrua ko Tia? (ii) Me kōrero kē koe ki a Tame rātou ko Hine, ko Rangi. (iii) Ka haere tonu atu māua ko taku ora (W 121).

4. [Tāne] Ka whakamahia hei tohu i tētahi wāhi. (i) Kātahi ka ui atu te kuia raka ki a ia, 'E hika, ko hea koe?' (Wh 3, 2) (ii) Ko hea tō ara i haere ai koe? (NM3 254, 14) (iii) Ko hea te wāhi pai hei haere māku ki te kato pikopiko? (KK 21) (iv) Ko konei tātou whai ngoi ai ki te patu i te kino, ki te whakatū i te tika (KtKM 1, 3).

kō1

1. [Tāne] ing. He taputapu roa hei wāwāhi i te oneone, he koi te pito ngau ki te oneone, he hamaruru hei takahi atu mā te waewae kei runga paku atu i te koinga; he rākau whawhai anō hoki i ōna wā.

2. ‑ia tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kari i te whenua, ka ono kai rānei ki te kō. Kātahi ka kōia te māra (W 120).

kō2

[Tāne] ing. He kupu whakamihi ki te kōtiro. Tino pai tō mahi ki te whai i ngā ara o te mātauranga, e kō!

kō3

[Tāne] wāhi. Tētahi wāhi kāore e tino pātata ana, engari kāore e tino tawhiti ana i te kaikōrero rāua ko te kaiwhakarongo. Kawea atu ā tātou tēpu ki kō, e tama mā.

kō4

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tangi (o te manu). Ka rite ki te kōpara e kō nei i te ata (W 121).  korihi

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] Ka whakaputa i tētahi oro kārangaranga nui. He tangata araara, kua kō ōna rongo ki te tī, ki te tā.  haruru (1)

3. [Rūaumoko] ing. Te tangi tīori, te tangi kaha o te reo. Ānō te kō e pā ana ki tētahi pari, nā ka whakahokia mai (W 121). {hō}

4. [Tāwhirimātea] ing, āhua. Te hau takiwā e rere noa ana i te mata o te whenua. E koiri ana ngā rākau i te kō.  hau1 (1)

kō5

‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke whakararo mai i tētahi wāhi teitei atu. E kō ana rā hoki te taratahi a Tāwhaki, ka tau ki raro (W 121).  heke (1)
