He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ihu

1. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhi i te kanohi e kōture mai ana i runga ake i te waha, kei reira ngā pongāihu e whakahā ai tētahi hanga, e rongo ai rānei i te kakara, i te haunga. (i) Engari ka tuohu anō kia hongi te ihu ki te ihu (IwiT 47:5). (ii) Ahakoa pēwhea te ngau a te puruhi, kei te takaokeoke au nei, kei te ngongoro ia ō rātou ihu (KRK 196). {kanekane}

ihu oneone [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He kupu whakarite mō te tangata pukumahi. Hei aha te pai āhua, rapua he ihu oneone hei tāne māu.  ringa (ringa raupā)

ihu parehe [Tāne] ing. He ihu pāraharaha te kaka. He momo whakaheke te ihu parehe i tēnā whānau. {(ihu pātiki)}

ihu pātiki [Tāne] He ihu pāraharaha te kaka. E ai ki taku māmā i taka mai au ki taku ihu tonu i taku whānautanga mai, koinei au e ihu pātiki nei.  (ihu parehe)

ihu pongare [Tāne] He ihu whānui, he pāraharaha te kaka. I tō rātou hapū, he āhuatanga heke iho tērā te ihu pongare mai rā anō.

puta te ihu [Tāne] kīw. Ka puta, ka ora (te tangata) i tētahi uauatanga pērā i te mate whawhati tata, i te whakataetae, i te whakamātautau rānei. (i) Kāti ko te mate kohi tētahi tino mate e huna nei i a tāua i te iwi Māori, nō reira whakapoua te kaha ki te whakatutuki i ngā tohutohu kia puta ai te ihu o te iwi ki te ao mārama (TT 12, wh12). (ii) Ka rere ki Rānana, ki Ingarangi ki te tono moni punga kia puta ai te ihu o te kamupene (TTR 2, 85). (iii) Nei noa te wawata, kia puta te ihu o papai rāua ko tōtika, ā, kia tapoko kau ko kikino rāua ko kotiti (HM 8, 1).

2. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhanga whakamua o te waka. Kia tika te ihu o te waka ki te putanga ake o te rā (B 70).  tauihu (1)
