He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ariā

1. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He kare ā-roto hākirikiri, he whakaaro hākirikiri rānei ka toko ake i roto i te tangata. (i) Heoi te mihi nei mō te ariā aroha nei ki a koe (W 15). (ii) I te ao pūtaiao ka takoto he ariā, kātahi ka āta whakamātauria kia kitea mēnā e pono ana, kāore rānei.

2. [Tāne] Te rerekētanga ka puta ake i te mahinga o tētahi mahi, i te pānga rānei o tētahi āhuatanga. (i) Kei te rangahau ia i ngā kōrero e whakaata mai ana i te ariā o aua kaupapa ki te Māori. (ii) Ka rere te ariā o te mate ki ngā waewae (W 15).  hua1 (8)

3. [Whiro] Te wairua o tētahi mea (pērā i te makawe o tētahi tangata) kua riro mai ki te ringa o tētahi atu, ko taua mea rā te huarahi atu ki te tangata e hiahiatia ana kia whakamatea, kia ahatia atu rānei. (i) Ko tēnei karakia mō te tangohanga o te ariā o te tangata e hiahiatia ana kia whakamatea (W 15). (ii) He māturunga toto te ariā o te hau o Māui.

4. [Tūmatauenga] Tētahi mea ko ia te whakatinanatanga o tētahi atua, o tētahi tangata whai mana rānei. (i) He atua a Moekahu, he kurī tōna ariā (W 15). (ii) He taio makawe te ariā o Te Iho-o-te-rangi. {ārika}

5. [Tāne] He tā karamū ka whakamahia hei whakamāmā, hei whakakore atu rānei i ngā taumahatanga o te tapu i runga i te taua.

āria

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He wai hōhonu e rere ana i waenga matatāhuna. He āria kei te whanga, he rerenga aua, he wāhi hirou pipi.

2. [Tangaroa] He wāhi wātea i waenganui i ētahi toka awa, toka moana rānei. I ū mai te waka i te āria, kia kore ai e raru i te toka.

3. [Tangaroa] He takotoranga wai ka kitea i te onepū ina timu te tai. Mā tai aronui rawa e kī ai te āria i te wai.

4. [Tangaroa] He whanga, he hōpua hōhonu rānei i te awa. Ka peke i te tahataha, ka tau atu ki te āria.

5. [Tangaroa] He horanga wai i te moana tata ki uta, he pai mō te tuku kupenga. E huri ana te hunga nei i te āria me te tō haere i te kupenga.
