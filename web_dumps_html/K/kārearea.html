He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kārearea

1. [Tāne] ing. He manu noho pae maunga, he huruhuru pango whai tāhei ura, kiwikiwi rānei kei te tuarā, he kahotea whai tāeka pango kei raro, he huruhuru ura kei ngā papa, he kiri kōwhai e taiāwhio ana i ngā karu parauri, he ngutu pango, he kōwhai ngā waewae. Falco novaeseelandiae. {kāeaea, kāiaia, kaiawa, kāieie, kakarapiti, kārewarewa, kāuaua, tāwaka}

2. [Tāne] He momo tā whakairo.

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. Rite ki te rau rākau ora, ki te pātītī ora rānei te tae. Kia whāranua te kikorangi me te kōwhai, ka puta ko te kārearea.  kākāriki (3)
