He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōingo

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka kaha rongo i te hiahia ki tētahi tangata, ki tētahi mea rānei. (i) E kōingo tonu ana ki te wā kāinga i mahue atu rā i ngā tau ki muri. (ii) Takoto, e hine, i roto i te kāinga i kōingotia e tō ngākau (TT 57, wh402). (iii) Nō taua wā ka waihangatia e ia āna waiata e rua hei whakaatu i tana kōingo ki a Hauāuru (TTR 2, 93). {āwhiti, āwhitu, ingo1, kōhau1 (2), komaingo, konau, kōningo, kōnohi, kōnohinohi, kōnonohi, kūata, kuatau, kūwata, mānakonako, mōhukihuki, muri2 (muri aroha), murimuri (murimuri aroha), pūkōnohi, pūkōnohinohi, takuate, tāwhai, watawata1, whakangākau, whēnako2, whēnakonako2}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka rongo i te pōuri nui i te rironga o tētahi mea, pērā i te matenga o te tangata e aroha nuitia ana. E kōingo ana tōku ngākau ki tōku hoa ka riro (W 128). {harapuka, hea3 (1), huamo, kōnohi, kōnohinohi, kōnonohi, māpuna2 (4), mōteatea, muri2 (3), murimuri, takuate}

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka roa tonu te tangata e āwangawanga ana, e āwhiti ana, e matangurunguru ana rānei, me te kitea anō pea o tēnei āhua ōna e ētahi atu. E kōingo ana te tamaiti i te mea kua ngaro tana pōro.  whakakunāwheke
