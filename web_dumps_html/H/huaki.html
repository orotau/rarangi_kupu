He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



huaki

1. ‑na ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka rere whakamua ki te patu (i te hoariri). Nō te taenga atu o te whai a Tūtetawhā me tana ope ki reira, kātahi ka huakina mai e te matua nui a Waikato (IwiT 29:20). {amo, āpiti, āpititū, ngau1 (5), pā2 (3), pāha, taiapu, tau8, tomo, tuki, whakaeke, whakatorotoro}

2. ‑na ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka mahi kia tuwhera mai, kia puare mai tētahi mea. (i) Ka pātōtō anō, kātahi a Pare ka matike ki te huaki i te tatau. Ehara. Kāore he tangata i reira (Wh 6, 6). (ii) Ka huri ngā wāhine ki te kōhue i ngā kōura, ki te huaki i ngā kina me ngā pāua kia noho reri ai mō te rā nui āpōpō (Wh 24, 9). {pahera, pawhera, whakapuare, whakatuhera, whakatuwhera}

3. ‑na ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka tango ake i te uhi, i te mea rānei e noho mai ana ki runga, kia mārama ai te kitea atu (o tētahi mea e takoto mai ana i raro). Ka tahuri ki te huaki i te hāngī, ā, ka rere te kakara.  hura1 (2)

4. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka taea te kuhu atu, te kite atu i roto, e wātea ana, arā, kāore e kati ana. (i) Waiho ngā tatau kia huaki kia uru mai ai te hehengi. (ii) Hei te iwa ngā toa ka huaki.  tuwhera

5. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] Kua mārama haere te rangi i te ata, engari kāore anō kia piki te rā ki runga ake i te pae. Ka huaki te ata, ka haere ngā mahi whakamahara ki ngā hōia i hinga i tāwāhi.  ao1 (5)
