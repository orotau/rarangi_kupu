He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

mahue

1. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] maho, ing, mahp, āhua. Kua waiho (tētahi mea) ki muri i te wehenga atu; kua whakarerea atu (tētahi mea) mō ake tonu atu. (i) I mahue ngā mātaitai a te wahine ki reira, mō te hokinga mai rā anō ka uta ai (N 167). (ii) I te whāwhai ki te haere, ka mahue tana pāhi ki te kāinga. (iii) Kātahi ka pupū ake te aroha i a ia, ka hoki ōna whakaaro ki a Nihomakuru i mahue atu rā i a ia ki Titirangi (KRK 19). (iv) He whenua mahue, ka tipuria e te māheuheu. {mangamutu, tāhapa, toreke}

2. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka waiho ki te taha, ka unuhia rānei (o te kākahu, o te taonga, o te aha atu). (i) Ko te whanake a Paoa, e kore e mahue (W 165). (ii) Ka mahue te taiaha, ka mau ki te meremere (W 165). (iii) Tēnā! Mahuetia ngā kākahu! (W 165) (iv) Mahuetia ōna hoa ki tō rātou waka (W 165).

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kāore i whiwhi, i whai wāhi atu, nā te wareware rānei, he mea āta kape rānei ki waho. (i) Kīhai i mahue te rapu i ngā mea katoa e ora ai rānei ia, e ngāwari haere ai rānei te mamae (KM 1, 8). (ii) Ka mahue te kōrero hāngai mai ki taku kanohi. {awere, ngere, rere}

4. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Kua whakarērea ki muri (o te wā). Nā tō rātou kaha tātou i tiaki i ngā whawhai nui o te ao i ngā tau tata kua mahue ake nei (H 17).  hipa (2)
