He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



makere

1. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] maho, mahp, āhua. Ka taka, ka tukua rānei kia taka i tētahi wāhi teitei atu. (i) He mea nui rawa taua taonga, ko taua taonga he wati, i makere ki te takiwā o Te Haukē nei i te ata o te Hatarei nei (HTK 1, 28). (ii) Kua makere ngā here, māhorahora ana te noho. (iii) E kore e roa kua makere ngā kākahu mākū, kua mau ngā tarau, ngā hāte maroke (Wh 6, 8).

2. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke whakararo mai i tētahi wāhi teitei atu. (i) Ka makere i ngā hōiho, ka tukuna e rāua ki te inu i te puna i reira (Wh 49, 5). (ii) Ahakoa kua tae rawa ia ki te wā e tika ana kia makere mai ia i taua tūranga nō te kaumātuatanga ōna, erangi, kua whakahoua anō (TTR 3, 17).  heke (1)

3. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Kāore e tutuki pai, e eke rānei ki te taumata e tika ana, e kimihia ana. Ko taku hiahia kia kaua ia e hoki anō ki ana mahi hē, te āhua nei kua makere taua moemoeā ōku.  paheke (2)

4. ‑tanga [Tāne] Kua riro te mauri ora, kua kore i ora. Ina makere au, ka tukuna aku whēkau kia ora ai ko tētahi atu.  mate (1)

5. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakaatu mai (tētahi wairua, tētahi āhuatanga) i a ia i tētahi whakakitenga.  whakaaranga

mākere

[Tāne] ing, āhua. Te wāhanga whakarunga o te tinana tangata, te wāhanga whakamua, whakarunga rānei o te tinana kararehe, kei reira ngā roro, ngā karu, te waha, ngā taringa me te ihu. Kua ānini noa taku mākere i te hoihoi o ngā puoro.  upoko
