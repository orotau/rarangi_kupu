kōhimuhimu

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka kōrero me te waiho mā te hā anake e kawe ngā kupu, kaua te reo, kia kore ai e rangona whānuitia te kōrero. Ka haere atu a Taana ki te kōhimuhimu atu ki tōna taringa, 'Kāore koe i te Mema i nāianei.' Kātahi anō ia ka mōhio kua hinga ia (PH 1, 14). {kōhamuhamu, kohimu, kōhumuhumu, kōkihi2, kōmuhu, kōmuhumuhu, kotete, kowhete, kōwhetewhete, whetewhete}

2. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka kōrero kia mārū, kia hākirikiri te reo, tērā tonu pea he whakaputa whakahē te mahi. (i) Pare: I rongo au i a Pīkari e kōhimuhimu ana mōu. Rangi: Hei aha māku! (KK 76) (ii) Nā ngā raruraru i pā i te wā o te hurahanga, me ngā kōhimuhimu whakahē a ētahi o ngā kaumātua o Waikato, ka kīia aua mea rā, he tohu nō te aituā i tūpono i muri iho (TTR 3, 114). {hāmeme, kōhumuhumu, kōmuhu, kōmuhumuhu, kowhete, kōwhetewhete, ngunguru, nguru, rea2, whakatanguru}
