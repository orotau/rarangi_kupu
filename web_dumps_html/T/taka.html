He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



taka1

‑hanga ‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke mai i tētahi wāhi o runga ake ki tētahi wāhi o raro atu, i te mea kua kore e puritia, e tautokona rānei ki taua wāhi teitei rā. (i) Ka taka atu te niu, ka tau atu ki tana mātenga, ka ngaro te ao mārama i a ia. (ii) Ka taka i a ia te oko, pakaru rikiriki ana. {ānewa, marere, ngahoro, pore3, taumarere}

taka2

1. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke haere i tētahi mata me te hurihuri anō, pērā i te rere o te pōro i te papa. Ka makere tētahi o ngā wīra o te motokā, ka taka atu ki te ururua i te taha o te rori.  pīrori2 (1)

2. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka whai haere i tētahi ara porohita, taiāwhio noa i tētahi hanga e tū ana ki waenganui pū. He roa tonu tana pōtaka e taka ana me te pīoioi anō, kātahi ka hemo.  huri1 (5)

3. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka huri kē te ahunga ki wāhi kē. Kua mutu te hui, kua taka te aroaro ki te wā kāinga.

4. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka haere mā muri, ka hipa mā muri. He rāngai maomao ka taka i tua o Nukutaurua, e kore a muri e hokia.

5. ‑nga [Ranginui] Ka tae mai, ka eke (tētahi wā), ka hipa rānei (o te wā). (i) He rā e taka e kai ai ia i ana tūtae. (ii) Ka taka ngā tau, ka pakari haere ngā whakaaro.

6. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka haere noa i te mata o te whenua, ka maewa noa me te kore e herea e tētahi mea, e tētahi tangata rānei. Kātahi ka kitea te rangapū tangata e taka ana mai i raro i ngā rākau (W 366).

7. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka karapotia (te pā, te ope) i runga i te whai kia horo, kia hinga. Kia rongo atu au i te rongo putakari, i te rongo parekura, i te rongo pā taka a Te Whatanui (W 366).

8. [Tangaroa] ing. He tauira whakairo he tōrino te āhua. Ka whai haere ana matimati i te hanga o ngā taka i ngā pokohiwi me ngā turi o te tipuna whakairo.

9. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] tūkē. Kei ngā taha katoa, e huri noa ana (i tētahi mea). Kātahi te hokowhitu rā ka whakataka i taua whare, ā, taka noa (W 366).

taka3

1. takā ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia rite tētahi mea mō tētahi kaupapa. (i) Ki te haere ahau ki te taka i te wāhi mō koutou ka hoki mai anō ahau, ka tangohia koutou ki ahau (Ha 2, 3). (ii) Ko ngā wāhine ki te taka kai mā mātau (HP 272).  whakarite (5)

2. ‑ia ‑nga [Tāne] Ka hāpai, ka poipoi (i te whakaaro kia mahia tētahi mahi). (i) Ka taka a Whakatau i te mahara kia haere ia ki te takitaki i te mate o tōna tuakana (W 366). (ii) Kua nui noa ngā rā, ngā wiki rānei, e taka whakaaro ana, e mahia ana ngā tikanga mō taua rā (WM 10, 23). (iii) Tēnei anō ētahi iwi te taka mahara nei, kia ahu ake anō i a rātou anō he tikanga mō rātou (KtKM 7, 6).

3. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka hāpaitia, ka poipoia (te whakaaro kia mahia tētahi mahi). (i) Ka taka te whakaaro i a Tupeteka kia hangā he whare hei kōhuru (Wh 3, 1). (ii) Ka taka te whakaaro i te kararehe nei me tūkino e ia tōna rangatira (Ko 56, wh9). (iii) Ka taka te mahara i ngā tāngata i whānau ki konei kia kaua e waiho mā tērā tangata o wāhi kē e takahi a runga o te tihi teitei o Nui Tīreni nei (HTK 2, 42).

taka4

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Ētahi mea kua whakawhāitihia mai ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Me huihui ā tātou kūmara kia kotahi te taka (W 366).  haupū (3)

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He huinga tāngata ka whakakotahi i runga i tētahi kaupapa. Kei te tokomaha haere te taka e whawhai ana kia hurihia te ture mō te hunga pāria e te mate kino kei te hiahia whakamate i a rātou anō.  rōpū (1)

whare taka [Tāne] Te whare huihui matua o te papa kāinga. (i) Ākuanei koe ka haere ki te whare taka (W 366). (ii) Nā, ka haere ngā tuākana ki ngā whare taka o taua kāinga (Wh 4, 8). {tako2 (whare tako)}

3. ‑nga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua noho puke iti mai ki tētahi wāhi. Ka mahue te whakairi i ngā tāora - ka waiho kē kia taka noa ki te papa.  haupū (2)

4. ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia whāiti mai ētahi mea ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Ka takaia e Tāwhaki ngā paru o te kāinga o Whaitiri kia ātaahua (W 366).  ahuahu1 (1)

taka5

[Tāne] ing. He whāriki kōkau hei hora ki te papa.  kaitaka1 (2)

takā1

[Tāne] ing. Te kahokaho e uhi ana i te taha whakawaho o te wāhi e tūtaki ai te rauawa ki te tinana o te waka. He takā tō waho, he paewai tō roto (W 366).  henga (1)

takā2

1. ‑ngia ‑hia ‑nga [Tangaroa] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka here i te aho ki te matau hī ika. Kātahi a Māui ka takā i tana matau (W 366).

2. [Tāne] ing. He aho hei takai i te kiko o tētahi mea (pērā i te toki) ki te kakau. Ka whakawhenahia ngā takā o te toki ki te mātiti.
