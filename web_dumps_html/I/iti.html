He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



iti1

‑nga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore i rahi te hanga, ina whakaritea ki ētahi atu mea; arā tōna kupu tauaro, ko te 'nui'. He manu iti te pihipihi, he manu nui te toroa. {hangariki, hauiti, huariki, kauriki, kaurikiriki, kōmehomeho, konaho, koroiti, mahake, maiti, mākari, matariki, mero, meroiti, meromero, meromeroiti, meroriki, meroririki, mōkitokito, momipū, moroiti, mororiki, muimui, nahonaho, namunamu, nepi, nepinepi, niki, niniki, ninipi, nipi, nohinohi, noke2, nonohi, ngorangora, ngote2, ngotengote, paku2, pakupaku2, pinepine, pohuka, pōkeke, pōnaho, poniponi, pota, puheto, puwheto, riha, riki1 (1), ririki, rōriki, tātāriki, tōriki, tōrikiriki, tōririki, tuaiti, tūpakipaki, whengowhengo, whetau, wheto}

iti2

‑nga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e whai take ana, kāore e whai kiko ana. He take iti noa iho tēnei! Kāore ōna kiko!  hauwarea (1)
