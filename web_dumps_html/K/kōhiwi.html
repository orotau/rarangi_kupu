He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōhiwi

1. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhanga mārō katoa, pāpango katoa o roto rawa o te kahiwi o te rākau. He rākau tawhito, e mau ana te taitea i waho rā, e tū te kōhiwi (W 125).  taikākā

2. [Tāne] Te wāhanga matua o te tinana o te waka, arā, te wāhanga ka aukahatia atu ngā rauawa ki runga. Kāore anō te kōhiwi kia rauawatia.  takere (2)

3. [Tāne] Te tinana o te rākau. Ka tapahia ngā peka katoa kia noho mai ko te kōhiwi anake. {ariaringa, kahiwi, kōiwi, tīwai1 (2)}

4. [Tāne] Te huinga kōiwi e tautoko ana, e tiaki ana i ngā whēkau, i ngā uaua, i ngā wāhi kikokiko o te tinana. I te kōrerotia te kōhiwi i kite rātou i te whare pupuru taonga o Te Upoko-o-te-ika.  anga2 (3)

5. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. Te tinana o te tangata kua mate. Patua iho aua mano, waiho iho ngā kōhiwi kia paea ana e te tai ki uta (W 125).  tūpāpaku (1)

6. [Tāne] ing. He tangata e noho wātea ana i te atua, i te wairua rānei, nāna ia i kōwhiri hei huarahi, hei reo, hei ringaringa, hei tinana mōna. E noho kōhiwi noa iho ana te tangata, kāore he mahara (W 125).  kaupapa (8)
