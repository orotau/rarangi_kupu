He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōpae

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Ka takoto whakapae mai, arā, ko te taha roa e anganui mai ana. E takoto kōpae ana i mua i te ahi.  hīpae

2. [Tāne] āhua. E noho mai ana te kūwaha ki te taha. Ko tōna whare, he whare kōpae (W 135).

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. Rite ki te porohita te hanga. He hanga kōpae te nuinga o ngā pereti me ngā oko. {porotaka, porotātaka, porotiti}

whare kōpae [Tāne] ing. He whare porohita te hanga, he pou kei waenganui. Ko tōna whare, he whare kōpae, ko tō Tinirau he whare paikea (N 30).

kōpae tutu [Tāne] He kete porohita, hanga kupenga nei te āhua mō ngā hua o te tutu.

4. [Rongo] hou. He taputapu kirihou he porohita te hanga, he tino angiangi, 12 henimita iti ake rānei te whitianga, whakamahia ai hei pupuri i te puoro, i te kōrero, i te whakaahua, i te aha atu rānei hei whakatangi, hei whakaata ā tōna wā. (i) Ko te pai o te kōpae puoro, he māmā noa te tīpoka i ngā waiata kāore e pai ki a koe. (ii) E rua tekau ēnā kōnae kōrero i uru atu ki te kōpae kotahi. (iii) Ka utaina atu ngā whakaahua o te mārena ki te kōpae, ka haria ki te toa tā whakaahua.

5. [Tāne] He rua i te whenua, i te rākau, he hanga tamomo rānei ka hangaia e te manu hei whānau i ana hua, hei kāinga mō ana pīpī; he wāhi i te whenua ka whakapaipaitia e te kararehe pērā i te poaka, hei kāinga mō ana punua. Ka rauwiria e te kererū he kōetoeto hei kōpae mōna.  kōhanga (1)

6. [Tāne] Ngā rau e karapoti ana i te hāngī kia kore ai ngā kai e taka atu i ngā kōhatu. Te haerenga atu o ngā wāhine ki te muru toetoe hei kōpae kai (W 135). {koronae, koropae, kōtonu}
