He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kohukohu1

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E totohu whakaroto ana te mata. Ko ngā wāhi kohukohu i te onepū e tohu ana he toheroa kei reira. {kohu2, kokohu, pakonga, whāruarua}

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He mate e kukuti ai, e mamae ai ngā uaua o tētahi wāhanga o te tinana. Ko te kore tote tētahi pūtake nui o te kohukohu o ngā waewae. {hakoko, kuiki, parerori, uauawhiti, uhu2}

kohukohu2

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He pūkohu wai tai nui ka tipu i tahatai, i te moana, i runga toka rānei i tua atu o tai aronui. Ka murua ngā rimurimu me ngā kohukohu i tōna tinana (W 126).  rimurimu

2. [Tāne] Ētahi momo pūkohu i whakamahia i ngā rā o mua hei takai, hei momi ake rānei i tētahi momo wai. (i) Arā ētahi karakia i whai wāhi atu ai te kūmara maoa, he mea takai ki te kohukohu i mua i te tunuhanga. (ii) E rua ngā kūmara, he mea takai a waho ki te kohukohu (W 126).

3. [Tāne] He rākau e 9 mita te tupu. Pittosporum tenuifolium.  kohuhu

4. [Tāne] He rākau e 3-4 mita te tupu, he rau iti manawa te hanga, he kahotea, he porotea rānei ngā pua ririki. Pittosporum obcordatum.

5. ‑a ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Whiro] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka whiu i te kupu kino, i te kupu whakahāwea, hei whakaatu i tō riri ki tētahi mea. (i) Ko te 'purari paka' he kohukohu nā te Pākehā (KRK 101). (ii) He rite te 'pokotiwha' ki te 'pōkokōhua' - e rua, e rua, e kohukohu ana i te tangata, i tētahi atu tūāhua rānei.  kanga (2)

6. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Whiro] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka karanga i te kino kia tau ki runga i te tangata, ki tētahi mea rānei. Nā tana kino ki ngā mahi a ērā tāngata ka tahuri ki te kohukohu i a rātou.  kanga (1)
