He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngunguru

1. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tāwhi tonu te aurere, te tangi o te mapu, te horu rānei, arā kāore e tino kaha te whakaputa. (i) Kua rongo mai ki te reo, ka ngunguru te koroheke nei (W 236). (ii) Ka pā te patu, ka ngunguru ake me he kumukumu (NM3 224, 7).  nguru (1)

2. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka whakaputa i te hā kia roa tonu, kia rangona tonutia, hei tohu i te ngenge, i te hōhā, i te pōuri, i te tānga o te ihu, i te aha atu. Ā, nō ka kite a Ihu i a ia e tangi ana, i ngā Hūrai hoki i haere tahi me ia e tangi ana, ka ngunguru ia, arā tōna wairua, ka kōingo (PT Hoa 11.33).  mapu (3)

3. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka kōrero kia mārū, kia hākirikiri te reo, tērā tonu pea he whakaputa whakahē te mahi. Ka ngunguru te tūroro, ka whakatata atu tana whānau ki te whakarongo ki ana kupu.  kōhimuhimu (2)

4. ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp. Ka haruru, ka tangi mārū. (i) Taku aroha rā ki ngā tai e ngunguru ē, ē, rā; he rite mai ki ahau e ngunguru noa nei ē, ē, rā ī (T 1, 28). (ii) Kāore i roa ka rongo mātau i te pūru e ngunguru haere ana mai (PH 6, 6). {hanguru, māorooro, marū, nguru, puoro, whakarūrū, wheoro1}

5. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He karakia e pā ana ki te hono i te tāne me te wahine. Ka roa ka mutu hoki tōna ngunguru (W 236). {nguru}
