He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hou1

‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kātahi tonu ka puta mai, ka mahia mai, ka riro mai rānei. (i) Heoi kia ora koutou i roto i ngā rā o te tau hou (PH 3, 6). (ii) Kātahi anō ka tae mai i te ringa tonu o te tangata nāna i hanga. He mea hou katoa, ko te utu he iti rawa (WMA 5, 2). (iii) Rekareka ana a kuia ki tana waka pīataata hou. {hōu, mohou}

hou2

[Tāne] ing. Ngā huruhuru roroa i te timu o te manu. I hukia mai rā taku hou kōtuku (W 62).  hikumaro (2)

hou3

‑a ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka takatakai (ki te miro, ki te taura, ki te aha atu) kia mau pai, kia tina; ka takatakai tahi i ētahi mea kia mau tahi ai.  here (1)

hou4

1. ‑a ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka haere ki roto. Ka tangi te pere kātahi ka hou atu ngā tamariki ki te akomanga.  uru2 (1)

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka ū tonu ki tētahi mahi, ki tētahi whakaaro rānei, ahakoa e uaua ana, e whakahētia ana rānei e ētahi atu. Ka hou ngā mata ki te moe, ahakoa kua hipa noa atu i te hāora e tika ana mō te maranga.  tohe (1)

hou5

1. ‑nga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Te iti o te pāmahana, ina whakaritea ki ētahi atu mea; te mahana kore. He pōtae, he kāmeta te haere i ngā rangi hou.  māeke

2. ‑nga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Tino kore nei e pai ki te titiro atu, ki te rongo atu, ki te whāwhā atu; tino kore nei e pārekareka. Te hou hoki o te kānga piro ki taku ihu.  houhou3 (2)

hou6

1. ‑a ‑hia ‑tia ‑hanga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka karakia i te tangata kua tutuki i a ia ngā whakamātautau a te tohunga e rite ai ia, ka tāpae hoki i ētahi makawe i te tūāhu ki tōna atua. Houa he pō au (W 63).

2. ‑hanga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakaū, ka tuku mā te karakia. Ko tō koutou mana ehara i te mea whānako, he mea hou ki runga ki ō koutou tīpuna (W 63).

3. [Tāne] ing. He karakia e whakaritea ai tētahi tangata mō tētahi kaupapa. Ka kitea tētahi tangata mōhio, kaha, ka houa taua tangata ki te hou nō Tū (W 63).

hou7

‑nga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Kāore i tata, arā, ka roa te haere e tae atu ai. Kāore he wai o konei, māu hoki e titiro kei hou noa atu te wai (W 63).  tawhiti1 (1)

hou8

[Tāwhirimātea] ing. He tōiriiritanga ka rere i te hau takiwā, i tētahi atu hanga rānei, ka rangona e te taringa; he mea ka rangona e te taringa. Ko te hou o te waiata a tuawahine e mataaho mai ana ki ngā taringa (W 63).  oro2

hōu

‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kātahi tonu ka puta mai, ka mahia mai, ka riro mai rānei.  hou1
