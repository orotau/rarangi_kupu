He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ao1

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Te aorangi e noho nei tātou te tangata, koia e noho tuatoru mai ana i te rā; ngā whenua me ngā tāngata katoa e noho ana ki tēnei aorangi. (i) Ko te māharahara nui, kei tae te mate urutā nei ki ngā tōpito katoa o te ao. (ii) Haere koutou ki a Tūmatauenga, i mate mai nei koutou ki te pae o te pakanga ki te Tiamana, i mea nei kia riro i a rātau te ao katoa, ka mea ai i ngā iwi hai pononga takatakahi mā rātau (HP 65).

ao tukupū [Ranginui] hou. Ngā wāhi katoa, ngā kokonga katoa o tuarangi, tae atu ki ngā whetū, ngā aorangi me ērā atu hanga katoa e noho mai ana ki roto, taka mai ki a Papatūānuku me te takiwā huri i a ia. Kei te tino whakaae ngā tohunga pūtaiao ki tā te iwi Māori, i tīmata mai te ao tukupū i te kore.

2. ‑nga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Te wā mai i te whitinga o te rā, ā, tō noa. He oho pō, he moe ao te paihamu.  awatea (2)

3. [Ranginui] ing, āhua. He huinga tōtā ka kitea e te karu, e rere ana i te rangi. (i) He ao te rangi ka ūhia, he huruhuru te manu ka rere (HP 47). (ii) E ai ki te kōrero i tapaina ai tēnei whenua ko Aotearoa, i te mea he ao tea roa e tākawe mai ana i runga i a ia.  kapua1

4. [Tāne] ing. He hanga koropuku ka tupu tika mai i te kakau, i te pito rānei o te kakau (o te tipu), ka huri hei pua, hei putiputi, hei rau, hei pihi hou rānei.

5. ‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua mārama haere te rangi i te ata, engari kāore anō kia piki te rā ki runga ake i te pae. Ka ao, ka ao, ka awatea (Ha 3, 1). {haeata, hāpai, hāpara1 (2), hī1 (4), hiko, huaki, ihi1 (3), kapuranga, māhina, ngahae, pūao, tākiri}

ao ake [Ranginui] tūkē. I te rā ka whai iho.  aoake (1)

6. ‑nga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E kaha ana te tuku aho, te whakaata aho rānei. He whetū ao tēnei ka tata mai (W 11).  pūaho (2)

ao2

1. ‑tia ‑hia ‑tanga ‑hanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka koko ki ngā ringaringa tonu. Me ao ake te oneone nā kia rongo ai ō ringaringa i te mahana o te onematua.

2. ‑tia ‑hia ‑whia ‑tanga ‑hanga [Rongo] Ka tango kia nui tonu. Ahakoa aohia, e haupū tonu ana te mātaitai.

ao3

‑tanga ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa (te kurī) i tētahi oro kaha, āhua rite ki te pakō, ki te pakē rānei. Nō te moemoetanga, ka ao mai a Irawaru (W 11).  auau3
