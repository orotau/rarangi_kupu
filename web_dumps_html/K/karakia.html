karakia

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahw, mahp, āhua. He kohinga kupu ka whakahuatia (me te haere tahi anō pea o ētahi atu tikanga) i runga i te whakapono, i te tūmanako rānei ka whakamanatia mai, ka arohaina mai e te taha wairua, e te wāhi ngaro rānei. (i) Te karakia kia mao te ua, te karakia kia whatitiri, kia rū te whenua, kia maroke ngā rākau, kia maroke te wai, kia ngaru te moana, kia rokia te moana (T 2, 57). (ii) E kī ana a Kemureti ki te kore he kai, kua riro mā tētahi o ngā tohunga e karakia, kua rere mai he kai ki runga ki te waka (HP 9).

2. [Tāne] ing. Ngā karakia o tētahi whakapono. I mua i te tukunga o te tūpāpaku ki te rua ka haere te karakia whakamutunga a te Hāhi Mihingare.

whare karakia [Tāne] He whare nō tētahi hāhi hei haerenga atu mā āna tangata ki te karakia, ki te mahi i ētahi mahi tapu. (i) Ka iriiria au ki te whare karakia Mihingare i Nūhaka (HP 17). (ii) Nā tōna hāhi Mōmona a ia i karakia. Kikī ana te whare karakia, puhake ana ngā tāngata ki roto i te hōro (Wh 44, 2).
