He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



karu1

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Ngā wāhanga e rua i te kanohi e taea ai te kite. (i) Kāore e taea e au te ika nei te tuaki. Kei te titiro mai hoki ngā karu (KK 35). (ii) Kua nenewha ngā karu i te kaha hiamoe. {kaikamo, kamo, kanohi1 (2), konohi, mata1 (3), nohi, pī3 (2), pūkanohi, pūkonohi, whakangita2, whatu1 (4)}

karu whakatata [Tāne] ing. hou. He taputapu e māmā ake ai te kite i ngā mea noho tawhiti, pēnei i te whetū. Ahakoa e whakanamunamu mai rā i te pae, ina mātakihia ki te karu whakatata, ka pōhehe koe kua tata tonu mai.  (karu whātata)

karu whārahi [Tāne] hou. He taputapu e kitea ai ngā mea tino moroiti nei te hanga. Mā te karu whārahi ka kitea ngā āhuatanga mōkitokito o te pūtau kararehe me te pūtau tipu.

karu whātata [Tāne] hou. He taputapu e māmā ake ai te kite i ngā mea noho tawhiti, pēnei i te whetū. (i) Ina titiro koe ki te marama mā te karu whātata, ānō nei kei mua tonu i tō kanohi. (ii) Mā te karu whātata rawa e āta kitea ai tō rātou waka i te tahatū o te rangi. {(karu whakatata)}

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te wāhanga whakarunga o te tinana tangata, te wāhanga whakamua, whakarunga rānei o te tinana kararehe, kei reira ngā roro, ngā karu, te waha, ngā taringa me te ihu. Whiua ana te kapa ki te rangi, ka tau ko te karu ki runga ko te hiku ki raro.  upoko

karu2

1. [Rongo] ing. Te wāhi kōpūtoitoi, te wāhi kākoa o roto o ētahi huawhenua pērā i te hue, i te kamokamo pakari, e takoto ai ngā kākano. He karaka te karu o te kamokamo pakari.  kakaru

2. [Tūmatauenga] He taputapu whai koromāhanga hei hopu manu. Kāore i mau he manu tā rāua karu.  māhanga2 (1)

karu kiekie [Tūmatauenga] He taputapu whai koromāhanga hei hopu kererū.

karu māhanga [Tūmatauenga] He taputapu whai koromāhanga hei hopu manu. E kore e mau he manu i tēnā karu māhanga.  māhanga2 (1)
