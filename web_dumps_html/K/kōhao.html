He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōhao

[Ranginui] ing, āhua. He āputa ka tīmata ake i tētahi taha o tētahi mea, ka puta ake anō i tērā taha, he wāhi areare rānei i tētahi hanga, i tētahi mata. (i) Kotahi te kōhao o te ngira e kuhuna ai te miro mā, te miro pango, te miro whero. I muri, kia mau ki te aroha, te ture, me te whakapono (NP 1507). (ii) Tapahia mai kia rua ngā kōhao ki te pepa - koinei te wāhi mō ō karu. (iii) Hāua atu ana te pōro, ehara, kotahi atu ki te kōhao. {kōputa, koroputa, kōwhao, puare, puta1 (2)} rua2 (1)

kōhao hiko [Ranginui] ing. He kōhao e purua atu ai te puru hiko hei hono atu i te taputapu hiko ki te puna hiko. (i) Waiho ngā pana o ngā kōhao hiko kia weto. (ii) Me uhi ngā kōhao hiko kia kore ai e taea e ngā nohinohi.
