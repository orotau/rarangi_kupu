He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



Rongo

1. [Rongo] ing. He tama rongonui nā Ranginui rāua ko Papatūānuku, he atua; nāna ka puta te āio ki te whenua, ko te rongomau ki waenga i te tāngata; he maha atu ōna ingoa hei tohu i ōna rohenga mana. (i) Ko ngā tohu nui o Rongo kitea ai i roto i te wharenui, arā, ko te rangimārie, ko te manaaki i te manuhiri. (ii) Nā Rongomaraeroa i ako te hanga whare ki te ao.

2. [Ranginui] hou. Te aorangi noho tuaono mai i te rā. Ko Rongo te aorangi o te whānau e mōhiotia ana mō ōna tarawhiti.  Pareārau (2)

rongo

1. rangona rongona ‑hia ‑tia rongonga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka hopu te taringa (i te oro). (i) Ina rongo koutou i te tangi a te pere ahi, taki puta ki waho. (ii) Kāore te kuia rā i te rongo, nā tōna turi.

2. rangona rongona ‑hia ‑tia rongonga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka noho mōhio te tinana, te wairua (ki te āhua o tētahi mea kua pā ki te kiri, ki te ngākau rānei). (i) Ka rongo i te makariri o te hau tonga i tōna taenga mai. (ii) He tokomaha ngā Māori kua rongo i te mamae i runga i te nama. E kī ana te whakataukī Pākehā, 'Pai atu te haere matekai ki te moe, i te maranga taimaha i te nama' (Pi 32, wh3).

3. rangona rongona ‑tia ‑hia rongonga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka hopu te ihu (i te kakara, i te haunga rānei o tētahi mea kua tae atu ki a ia). (i) Kua rongo i te kakara o ngā kai, kua waiwai te waha. (ii) Kei te rongo au i te haunga kurī i roto nei. {hongi, hongihongi, pihongi, whakamono}

4. rangona ‑tia rongonga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka hopu te arero (i te hā, i te tāwara o tētahi mea) ka pā atu ki a ia. Ka kai a Kae, ka rongo i te reka (W 346).

5. [Rongo] ing. He kōrero whaitake nō nā tata nei i pā ai, kātahi anō rānei ka puta whānui. (i) I Kirikiriroa mātau ka pā mai te rongo kua mate te koroua rā. (ii) Kua hau ngā rongo mō tērā wahine kai tangata ki ngā tōpito katoa o te motu. (iii) Ka puta te rongo kua whakapaea te pā, ka maunu mai te ope taua a Ngāti Te Ata, Ngāti Tīpā me Ngāti Tāhinga (IwiT 47:2).

6. [Rongo] He kupu ka tae ake ki te taringa e whakaatu ana i tētahi āhuatanga kua pā. (i) Ka tae atu te rongo o taua raruraru ki Pōneke, kātahi ka huihui ngā rangatira, kia kōrero ai rātou mō taua hē (Ha 2, 2). (ii) Ka pā mai te rongo kua mate a Kehu, ka waea haere au ki ana hoa tata. {kōrero}

7. [Rongo] Te rangimārie ka mau ina mutu te pakanga, te riri. Ka whawhai tonu, ā nāwai, nāwai, ā ka tuku te rau o te patu, ka mau te rongo (TTR 1, 297).  maungārongo

rongo ā-marae [Rongo] Te rongo nā te tāne i mau ai.

rongo ā-whare [Rongo] Te rongo nā te wahine i mau ai.

rongo taketake [Rongo] He rongo pūmau. Mauria mai ko te rongo ā-whare, ko te rongo taketake, ki mua ki te atua . . . ka whakaoti te riri (W 346). {rokihau}

rongo moana [Tangaroa] He ingoa whānui mō ngā ika tai tino nui.  ika1 (ika moana)
