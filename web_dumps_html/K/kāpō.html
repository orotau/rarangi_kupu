He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kapo

1. ‑a ‑hia ‑nga ‑hanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka tere tonu te rere o te ringa o te aha atu rānei, ki te tango i tētahi mea. (i) Ka whai a Hari i a ia i runga hōiho, te taenga ake ka kapohia tōna pōtae, ka tahuti (WMA 2, 32). (ii) Kia kapo kau noa ake ko te mahara, e koro ē (HM 10, 4). {hiko, hopu, kōhaki, kōrari, kōwhaki}

2. ‑nga ‑hanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tuku, ka whakaata rānei i te aho kaha tonu, otirā he poto, he ohotata, he taratahi te āhua o te whakaputa. Tērā te uira kapo taratahi ana (W 389).  hiko (1)

3. [Ranginui] ing. He aho kanapa ka hiko i te rangi i te marangai; arā tana hoa puta ngātahi, ko te whatitiri. Te whatitiri, te kapo, te hukarere (W 96).  uira (3)

kāpō

‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e kite, i te hē, i te mate rānei o ngā karu. I te tīmatanga o te pakanga kāore ia i whakaaetia kia haere ki te mura o te ahi nā te kāpō o tōna whatu mauī (TTR 4, 12). {kēkerepō, kerepō, matakene, matakerepō, matamorari, matamoraru, matapō, papi, pārewha, pohe, popohe, pura}
