He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kuku

1. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kati ki waenganui i te mati me te kōnui, ki ngā kauae rānei (o te kararehe, o te taputapu rānei), ki ētahi mata noho tauaro rānei. Kāore au e hiahia kia kukua taku waewae e te pāpaka - koinei au e mau hū nei i roto i te wai.  kini (1)

2. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka kukume i ētahi mea, i ngā taha rānei o tētahi mea kia kiato. Kukua te waha o te kete kia kore ai ngā kai o roto e makere mai. {tāroi}

3. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka kati (i tētahi mea) kia kore ai e noho whewhera tētahi wāhanga ōna, kia huirua rānei. Ka kukua te ringaringa, ka motokia ake ki tōna ihu (W 155).

4. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka pēhi (i te hā) ki te uma. Ka kuku te tamaiti i tana manawa kia puta rawa ia i te arapoka. {kumu}

5. [Tangaroa] ing. He hanga e rite ana ki ngā matihao o mua o te kōura, o te pāpaka. (i) Kei te huhuti ake i ētahi o ngā huruhuru o ana pewa ki te kuku, kia pai ake ai te āhua. (ii) Ka tīkina tana kuku hei tango ake i te ngita i tana tapuwae.

kuku o te manawa [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He kupu e tohu ana i te tangata e aroha nuitia ana. Kua whakaarorangi noa ake tōna ngākau ki te ātaahuatanga o Te Whatuiāpiti, te kuku o tōna manawa (W 155).  ate1 (tau o te ate)

6. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai whai kokopi rapa te hanga, 12 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki taihua, he pukepoto a waho, he pango, he mā rānei a roto. Mytilus edulis. {kūtai, pōrohe, toretore2 (2), torewai, toritori2}

kuku mau toka [Tangaroa] He mātaitai whai kokopi rapa te hanga, e 4 henemita te tupu, he tini te piri toka ki taihua, he anga kōwhai, he ōriwa rānei, e 6 ngā rara kei tētahi pito, 14 kei tētahi, he māwhero a roto. Modiolarca impacta.

kuku taurei [Tangaroa] He momo kuku, he mā te kai o roto.

7. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai whai kokopi rapa te hanga, e 20 henemita te tupu, he piri toka ki tai takoto haere ake, he anga kānapanapa, he ngutu kākāriki. Perna canaliculus. {kūtai, pipi, pōrohe}

8. [Tāne] He mate e tohua ana e te ngau kino o te puku, he kakā, he pupuhi rānei nō te kōpiro, he mau rānei nō te hau ki reira. He tangitangi tonu te pēpē ka pāpāria e te kuku.  haku1 (2)

9. [Tūmatauenga] Te mataku, te wehi. Nā tana kuku ki ngā aitanga a punga i kore ai ia e tomo i te wao.  wanawana (3)

10. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He moemoeā kino e mataku ai te tangata, e hē katoa ai tana wairua. Ka moe koe, ā, e haere mai ana te wairua o tētahi tangata ki te patu i a koe, he kuku tēnā (W 155).  moepapa

11. [Tangaroa] He tauira whakairo.

kukū1

[Tāne] ing. He oro e rite ana ki tētahi mea e wakua ana e tētahi atu mea. Ka rongo a Kupe i te kukū o te takere o te waka (W 153).

kukū2

‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore i waiwai, e āhua pē ana, e āhua totoka ana. Ka patua te hīnau, ka tātari, kātahi ka pokepoke ki te wai; ka karanga mai tētahi tangata, 'Kia iti te wai kia kukū ai' (W 153). {pupuru2}

kūkū

[Tāne] ing. He manu noho wao, noho ngahere. Hemiphaga novaeseelandiae.  kererū
