He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kopapa

[Tangaroa] ing. He kahawai kōpīpī, arā, kāore anō i pakari. Arripis trutta.  puāwai (3), tāhuri

kōpapa

1. [Tāne] ing. He waka iti, kāore ōna rauawa, kāore ōna whakairo. Ka taka a Koroki ki runga ki te waka kōpapa, ka hoe ki Waikato (W 136). {koki2 (3), kōnia, kōpiri, kōrea, korehe, kōreti, porea, reti2 (2)}

2. [Tāne] He momo waka ka hangā ki runga reti hei kawe i te tangata, i te taputapu, i te aha atu, ka tōia haerehia e tētahi atu waka, e te kararehe, e te tangata tonu rānei. Ngahau ana te rere a te kōpapa ki te take o te puke, ko te tō anō ki runga kē te mahi hōhā.  kōneke (1)

3. [Papatūānuku] He hanga roa, he papatahi, he rahirahi, he mātotoru rānei, ka kania mai i te rākau. He mataī ngā kōpapa o te whare.  papa1 (3)

kōpāpā

1. ‑kanga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tino piri, ka apiapi te noho tahi, te tū tahi rānei. Ko Te Tapuwae tonu te urupā rongonui o roto o Ruātoki. Tae rawa mai ki te tau 1955, kua kōpāpā rawa hai tanu tūpāpaku (TTR 1, 238).  kōpipiri (1)

2. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He rākau ka whakamaua atu ki te pito whakarunga o te tumu, o te tuke kia pai ai te whakairi i te taputapu hopu manu nei ki te rākau.  kōrera
