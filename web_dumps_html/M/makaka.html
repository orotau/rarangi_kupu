He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



makaka

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. E kopa ana, e takawiri ana, kāore e rere tōtika ana. Ka makaka te pihi o te nanenane i te tukinga ki te pakitara.  kōnukenuke

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] E tōrino ana te āhua o te tupu (o ngā makawe). He rite ō makawe makaka ki te rimurimu.  māwhai

mākaka1

1. [Tāne] ing. He rākau iti e 2 mita te tupu, he whīwhiwhi te tupu o ngā tātā, he rau tino ririki, he mā ngā pua ririki, he hua parauri ririki e rere ana i ngā tātā. Plagianthus divaricatus.  runa

2. [Tāne] He huruwhenua piki rākau. Lygodium articulatum.  mangemange

3. [Tāne] He rākau iti e 2 ki te 5 mita te tupu, he rau pinerua torutoru noa, he rārā papatahi whai kōwakawaka, he pūkanohi e rere ana i ngā rārā, ranea ana te tupu o ngā pua, he pākākano roa tonu. Carmichaelia australis.  tainoka (1)

4. [Tāne] He rākau 12 mita te tupu, he rau nunui pinerua te tupu o ngā tawheraiti, he tawheraiti nui tonu kei te kāmata, he niho ririki kei ngā tapa, he taratara, he pūhui roa te tupu o ngā pua, he whero ngā hua. Caldcluvia rosaefolia.  makamaka (3)

5. [Haumia] Te aka whenua o te rarauhe; he kai. Me kuru te mākaka kia hemo ai te kākoa, kātahi anō ka kai.  aruhe

mākaka2

[Tūmatauenga] ing. He karakia whakatapu tūpāpaku, kia kore ai e kainga.
