He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



Māori

‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. He tangata whenua nō Aotearoa, tērā tonu ka hoki ōna whakapapa ki tētahi o ngā tūpuna o runga i ngā waka i heke mai i te hekenga nui. (i) Kei te tohe tonu rātou me takoto kē ki roto i te whare karakia Māori, nō te mea he Māori ia, ā, he mema nō te Hāhi Māori (HP 311). (ii) Ko ngā taonga ātaahua a te iwi Māori kei roto i ō tātou wharepuni (Wh 26, 5). (iii) Ahakoa tonu, ko te tikanga kē ia kia mau tonu tātou ki ngā tikanga Māori.

māori1

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, mahp. E kitea nuitia ana, e puta ake ana i ia rā, i ia rā, kāore i tauhou. Ko te kai māori a tētahi iwi, he kai tino rerekē pea ki tētahi atu iwi.

wai māori [Tangaroa] ing. He wai pērā i ngā wai o te kōawa, o te awa, o te puna, ehara i te wai tai. (i) He wai māori ngā wai o Taupō-nui-a-Tia me Rotorua-nui-a-Kahu. (ii) I taua wāhi e whakaranu mai rā te wai tai ki te wai māori, ka kitea atu te mahi a te aua e ngote ana i te wai tai (KRK 16).

kaipuke māori [Tāne] He kaipuke kāore he pūkaha, he rā tōna, he roa, he teitei ngā niao, he hōhonu te riu. Mā te kaipuke mamaha anake ka taea paitia ngā kai o ēnei awa, kua kitea hoki i ngā wā kua pahure e kore e āta oti tēnei mahi i ngā kaipuke māori e rere nei, nō te mea, e kaha ana te hau o te taituauru (KtKM 1, 2).

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, mahp. I tupu mai, i hua mai, i takea mai rānei i Aotearoa; i tupu mai, i hua mai, i takea mai rānei i tētahi atu whenua kua tohua. (i) He rākau māori te tōtara me te akeake, he rākau rāwaho te purukamu me te whiro. (ii) Ko te moa, he manu māori rongonui kua ngaro i te mata o te whenua. (iii) He kararehe māori te koara me te warapī nō Ahitereiria.

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] I takea urutapu mai i te ao tūroa, kāore i waihangatia, i kaha raweketia rānei e te ringa tangata kia rerekē katoa te hanga. (i) He rawa māori ngā kora o nehe pērā i te hau kapuni me te hinu. (ii) He matū māori te whītau, engari anō te kirihou.

4. ‑tia [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] tūkē, āhua. Kāore he here, he aukati e whakararu ana i te haere, kāore rānei he tikanga, he whāinga e ārahi ana i te haere, e rere kau noa ana. (i) Kua puta ia ki waho i te pito o te rua rā, kua ora ia, kua haere māori noa iho i te koraha (W 179). (ii) Ka haere mātou i te huarahi, ā ka ngaro taua huarahi, kātahi mātou ka haere māori noa atu i roto i te ngahere (W 179). (iii) Me peka māori noa atu, hei aha te waea atu i te tuatahi.

māori2

‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, mahp, tūkē. E māmā ana te kitea atu o te tikanga (o te kōrero). (i) He kupu māori tonu, kāore i rehurehu, kāore rānei i arero rua. (ii) Me kōrero māori atu au, kia āta mōhio ai koe ki taua whenua (W 179).  mārama (3)
