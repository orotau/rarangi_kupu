He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



korokoro1

1. [Tāne] ing. Te pito whakarunga o te ara e heke ai te kai ki te puku, e rere ai te hau ki ngā pūkahukahu. (i) I te kaha tonu o te wera, ka tau te rūhā ki runga ki te tinana, ka tae mai te maroke ki te korokoro (Wh 43, 6). (ii) Tata auē ahau i te tino mamae o taku korokoro, nā, ka homaingia he aihikirīmi hei kai māku (Wh 24, 8). {kakī1, tākakī}

2. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 45-75 henemita te tupu. Geotria australis.  piharau

korokoro2

‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore i tānekaha, i mārō (o ngā momo here katoa). Korokoro noa ngā tau o te kākahu (W 143).  hangoro

korokoro3

1. [Tāne] ing. Tētahi o ngā wāhanga roroa e rima e hono ana ki te kapu o te ringa tangata. Ka kuhuna atu te rīngi ki te mānawa o te ringa mauī, arā, ki te korokoro mārena.  mati (2)

2. [Tāne] Tētahi o ngā wāhanga e rima i te pito o te waewae. Ahakoa mau tōkena, ka mate tonu aku korokoro i te māngiongio ka takurua ana.  mati (1)
