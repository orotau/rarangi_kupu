He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kaka1

1. [Tāne] ing. He makawe kotahi, he huruhuru kotahi; he whenu angiangi kotahi nei, nō te tipu, nō te papanga rānei. (i) Mai i te kaka kotahi nei i mōhio ai ngā tohunga pūtaiao ko ia te tangata nāna i tūkino te kōtiro. (ii) Ka kīia ngā kaka o te harakeke kua oti te hāro, he muka, he whītau. {weu}

2. [Tāne] Te wāhanga roroa o te tipu e piki whakarunga ana mai i te papa, e tautoko ana i ērā atu wāhi ōna; te wāhanga o te tipu e mau mai ai ngā rau, ngā pua, ngā hua rānei. Kōmukua ngā rau i ngā kaka o ngā putiputi, ka rau atu ai ki te ipu.  tātā2 (1)

3. [Tangaroa] Ngā rārangi matua i te tānga moko. Me whai koe i ngā kaka kia tika ai te mahi.

4. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. Te pae (o ētahi puke, o ētahi maunga rānei), he roa, he whāiti. Kei runga au kei te kaka e tiro iho ana ki ngā mānia e hōrapa ana i raro.  kahiwi (2)

5. [Tāne] ing. Te taura e here ana i te tangata ki ōna tūpuna. Kāore i huna te kaka tino tangata (W 91).  kāwai1 (1)

kaka2

‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tere tonu, ka kaha tonu, ka pokapoka rānei te whewhera me te kokopi o te manawa. Kaka ana te manawa i te pānga o te ringa o tuahangata ki a ia.  kapakapa (1)

kaka4

[Tāne] ing, āhua. He ingoa whānui mō ngā uwhi tinana ka whakamaua e te tangata. Kuhuna ō kaka pai, kua haere tātau ki te karakia.  kākahu (1)

kakā

1. ‑nga [Rūaumoko] āhua, ing, mahp. E tino kaha ana te wera, kua hahana, kua whero (o te ahi, o te toka, o te maitai rānei). (i) Āpōpō, kia pēnā ngā kiri o ōu tungāne; hihī ana i ngā kōwhatu kakā o Waikororā (N 71). (ii) Ko te kī a Whaea kia kaua te tamaiti e whakapā atu ki te horehore kakā, kei wera tōna ringa. {ratarata}

2. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tuku i te aho āhua ngoikore me te mahana anō. E kakā tonu ana tō hikareti, e hoa! Kei wera i a koe te whare!  ura (3)

3. ‑nga [Whiro] Ka pūwhero, ka pupuhi, ka mamae anō pea (tētahi wāhi o te tinana) i te mea kua pāngia e tētahi mate, kua whara rānei. (i) E tangitangi ana te tamaiti nohinohi i te mea kua kakā tōna taringa. (ii) Kua kakā te kōnui, me te panapana anō. {kārawarawa}

kiriuhi ua kakā [Whiro] ing. He mate kino e kakā ai ngā kiriuhi o te ua me te roro i te pokenga e tētahi momo huakita, e tētahi momo huaketo rānei. Me haere tika ki te rata, ki te hōhipera rānei, mēnā ka kitea ngā tohu kiriuhi ua kakā.

kāka

[Tāne] ing. He manu nui tonu noho wairepo. Botaurus poiciloptilus.  hūrepo

kākā

[Tāne] ing. He manu noho wao, he kiwikiwi te upoko, he tāhei kura kei te ua, he ōriwa parauri a runga, he uma kiwikiwi parauri, he puku whero, he tou whero, he karu parauri, he pango ngā ngutu me ngā waewae o tō Te Ika-a-Māui, he kiwikiwi ō tō Te Waipounamu. Nestor meridionalis. {kōrī, kōriwhai, kura1 (11), perehere} tātāpopo

kākā nihoriki [Tāne] He momo kākā whai ngutu poto. {motaraua, nihoriki}
