He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



moe

1. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka kopi ngā karu, ka whakatā te tinana me te hinengaro me te waiho i te ao tūroa kia tārewa ana. (i) Takoto kau atu ki tana moenga, ka moe. (ii) He manu moe awatea te ruru. (iii) E mātaki ana i te pouaka whakaata, ka warea e te moe. {kaikaru}

moe whakatorohū [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He moe tūrama, kāore e au ana. He moe whakatorohū tana moe i te hokihoki tonu o ana whakaaro ki te aituā i mate nei tana hoa, kia ora ko ia.  āuta (moe āuta)

2. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka kati i ngā karu. Moe atu ana aku kanohi ki a Tūkōrehu, ki te hunga nāna i takitaki taku mate, ka ea Waipoua (NM2 131, 41). {kemo, kimo, whakamoe}

3. ‑nga [Tāne] Kua riro te mauri ora, kua kore i ora. Ko ngā tāngata o taua pā rā, moe tonu, kīhai i ora tētahi (W 204).  mate (1)

4. ‑a ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka piri te tāne ki te wahine hei hoa pūmau, i ngā wā o mua, i runga anō pea i te whakaae o te whānau, o te hapū, o te iwi. (i) Ka moe a Kakati i a Ururangi, hei tūhonotanga tērā mō ngā whakapapa o Tainui ki a Kurahaupō. (ii) E moe i te tangata ringa raupā (Wh 31, 34). (iii) Ka moe a Te Heuheu i tētahi atu wahine, ka pōuri a Rangiaho, ka tārona i a ia (NM4 358, Kwm). {hono1 (2), noho} mārena (1)

5. ‑a ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka whakatō i te ira tangata e puta ai he uri ā tōna wā. Mā Kahutia-te-rangi, te tangata i moea ki runga ki te takapau wharanui (W 204).  ai2 (3)

6. ‑a ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka kite (i te tangata, i te aha atu) i roto i te moemoeā. Ka moea iho e Waitiri ka kīia mai e te wahine o raro, o te rēinga, 'Aua rā te mea ka mate nā koe' (W 204). {tahakura, whakarehu1, whakarika, whakaririka}

moe tahurangi [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka moemoeā e noho tahi ana ki tētahi hoa kei wāhi kē. (i) Ka moe tahurangi au mō māua ko tētahi o aku hoa kei tāwāhi e haere ana. (ii) Oho ake ki te ao, he moe tahurangi noa, e haere tonu ana koe.

7. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He whakaaro, he āhua, he ariā ka whakaaturia ki te hinengaro i te wā e moe ana. He moe pō nāku i konei tonu koe (W 204).  moemoeā

moe tahakura [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] He moemoeā e kitea ai tētahi kua mate. E te moe tahakura i a Te Kōtuku rā, oho ake ki te ao tirotiro kau au, he ao mānu rawa i kitea iho ai (NM4, 360, 33).  tahakura (1)
