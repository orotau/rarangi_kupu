He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōiwi

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Ngā hanga mārō kōmā te tae kei te tinana, e tautoko ana i ngā wāhanga kikokiko pērā i ngā uaua, e tiaki ana anō hoki i ngā whēkau. (i) Ka kīia te katoa o ngā kōiwi o te tinana he anga. (ii) Ko te kōauau he mea hanga mai i te kōiwi, i te rākau hoki (Wh 41, 9). {iwi, poroiwi, wheua}

2. [Tūmatauenga] Te tinana o te tangata kua mate. I te wā o te rewharewha nui ka nehua ngātahitia ngā kōiwi maha ki te rua nui tonu.  tūpāpaku (1)

3. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te tangata ake, tōna kotahi - taha kikokiko, taha wairua, taha tuakiri. (i) Hoki mai anō ko tō kōiwi (W 129). (ii) Nō whea e hoki ake tō kōiwi ora ki te ao? (W 129)  whaiaro (1)

kōiwi kūrapa [Whiro] He tangata karo mahi, he māngere. Te kōiwi kūrapa! (W 129)  kaipaoe (2)

4. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Te taha kiko kore o te tangata e mau ana ngā kare ā-roto, te taha e kore e mate ahakoa mate atu te tinana. Ka mutu te tuku, ka tangi katoa ngā tāngata, roa noa atu e takoto ana, me te tangi tonu ngā tāngata, ka hoki mai tōna kōiwi. E 20 meneti pea ka karanga mai ki tōna kōkā, 'Kāti te tangi, e kore rawa koe e wareware i a au', ka mutu te tangi, kātahi ka hemo rawa tana kōtiro (Wa 1, 7).  wairua (1)

5. [Tāne] Te tinana o te rākau.  kōhiwi (3)

6. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Ngā tāngata, ngā kararehe, ngā aha atu ka heke mai i tētahi tupuna, i tētahi atua rānei. Kua noho kōiwi kore, koirā i noho tōpū tonu ai ana pānga whenua.  aitanga (1)
