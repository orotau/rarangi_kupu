He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



āniwaniwa

1. [Ranginui] ing. He piko ānau he maha ōna tae ka kitea e tū mai ana i te rangi ina kōuaua, ina ua. Ka tāwhana mai te āniwaniwa i te rangi, ko ōna tae he whero, he karaka, he kōwhai, he kākāriki, he pukepoto, he waiporoporo, he tawa - kānapa ana! {āheahea, atuapiko, atuatāpiko, atuatoro, Kahukura, kairangi1 (1), kōpere, Ōuenuku, pāhoka, pāhokahoka, rore1 (7), tāwhana, tāwhanawhana, Tūāwhiorangi, Uenuku}

2. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] āhua, ing, mahp. E pōuriuri ana, pērā i ngā kapua i te pānga mai o te āwhā, perā i te wai hōhonu rānei. E āniwaniwa mai rā te rangi, taihoa ka ua tātā.

3. [Tangaroa] ing. He wai hōhonu. He āta rere te wai i ngā āniwaniwa o te awa.  rire (1)

4. [Ranginui] He porohita hīnātore e karapoti ana i te marama, i te rā, i tētahi atu aorangi, whetū rānei. Ātaahua ana tērā te āniwaniwa e horopaki ana i a Māhina.  amaia
