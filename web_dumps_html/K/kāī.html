He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kai1

1. ‑nga ‑ngia ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia pau katoa, kia ngaro katoa. (i) Kātahi ka kainga te pā e te ahi (W 85). (ii) Ko te tohu o te pakiaka pēpē, he rākau mate kua puare kē a roto, kua kainga e te pirau (Wh 45, 2). (iii) Kaitoa kia kainga ia e te whakamā. {hohoni, honi, iki, none, ngonge, pongakawa, whakahemo, whakapau}

2. ‑nga ‑ngia ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka hoatu ki te waha, ka horomi; tērā tonu ka ngaungaua i mua i te horomitanga. (i) Kainga ō huawhenua kia pakari ai koe. (ii) Kia tae mai tā tātou manuhiri kua kai tātou. {haupa, kame, kami, kamu, kome, komi, ngonge, tame, whākoma}

kai angaanga [Whiro] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka kanga i te tangata. Ka tukutuku te ia ki Tarahanga, ki te kai angaanga i a Ngāti Pou (NM3 280, 25).  (kai upoko)

kai hāporo [Whiro] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka tere tonu te kai me te kore e whakaaro ki ētahi atu. Kaua e kai hāporo i ngā wāina rā, kei ngau tō puku.  kaihoro

kai paipa [Whiro] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tahu i te hikareti, i te paipa rānei, ka momi i te auahi. Kaua e kai paipa, kei pā he mate kino ki ō pūkahukahu. {(kai tōrori), moki3, mokimoki2, momi}

kai parapara [Whiro] ing. He hara e kainga ai te kai i tīkina atu i te whenua tapu.

kai tōrori [Whiro] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tahu i te hikareti, i te paipa rānei, ka momi i te auahi.  (kai paipa)

kai upoko [Whiro] Ka kanga i te tangata. Ka whakaari atu anō i tana mere me te kai upoko atu anō ki te hoariri. {(kai angaanga)}

kai whiore [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. Te ai tahi a tētahi tokorua e tata rawa ana te whanaungatanga, pērā i te pāpā rāua ko tana tamāhine. E whakapaetia ana nā te kai whiore i roto i taua whānau i raru ai ngā tamariki.  ngau1 (ngau whiore)

3. ‑nga ‑ngia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka inu i tētahi momo inu, hāunga anō te wai māori. (i) E kai waiū ana te tamaiti (W 86). (ii) Me mutu te kai waipiro.

4. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. He hanga whai painga ka kainga, ka inumia rānei e te tangata, e te kararehe, ka pau rānei i te tipu e ora ai ia. (i) Ko te kīnaki mō tērā kai he miraka, he huka. (ii) Kaua rawa e whāngaingia ngā tamariki tāne, kōtiro rānei ki te waipiro. Kāhore he pai o tērā kai (WM 9, 8). {aotea, kame, kamukamu, kome, komi, oranga, pararē1, poa1 (1), poumatua, tame, tami, tamitami, tātua (tātua o Hikakawa), tawhi1, wene1 (2)}

Anā tō kai [Tāne] kīw. He kupu ka whiua e tētahi ki te tangata tonu e kōrero atu ana ia, hei tohu kua tau atu ki runga i taua tangata tētahi āhuatanga kino e tika ana kia tau atu ki a ia, arā, nāna tonu i kimi tana mate. Anā tō kai! Ka kīia atu kia whakamutua tēnā mahi weriweri, kāore tonu koe e whakarongo. {āe1 (Āe, āe), koe4 (E koe (E koe e koe)), nge (E nge.)} kaitoa2

kai a te ahi [Whiro] kīa, ing. He kōrero whakakino, whakaiti i tētahi atu. (i) Kai a te ahi! He pai ake te kurī i a koe! (ii) Kāore au i te paku aroha atu ki tēnā kai a te ahi. {(kai a te kurī), kaiatahi, kaiatehi}

kai a te kurī [Whiro] He kōrero whakakino, whakaiti i tētahi atu. (i) Kātahi te kōrero kia whakaputaina e koe i te tangihanga o te tangata. Kai a te kurī! (ii) Kei hoki mai tēnā kai a te kurī ki konei.  (kai a te ahi)

kai kōtore [Papatūānuku] ing. He hākari hei whakanui i te moe a ētahi rangatira. Nō te 19 o Hune o te tau 1943 i moe ai a Pirimi i a Mairatea Pitt-Pōrutu o Te Āti Awa i te whare karakia o Rangiātea i Ōtaki, ā, horahia ana te kai kōtore i runga i te marae o Raukawa (TTR 4, 181). {umu (umu kōtore)}

kai tapairu [Tūmatauenga] He kai tapu hei kai mā ngā ariki o te kāwai mātāmua anahe. Kaitoa, nāna i kai te kai tapairu (W 382).

kai wētā [Tāne] He rākau e 6 mita te tupu. Melicytus lanceolatus.  māhoe (māhoe wao)

kai wētā [Tāne] He rākau 10 mita te tupu. Carpodetus serratus.  putaputawētā (1)

kai whakapaepae [Rongo] ing, āhua. He koha ka hoatu hei whakamihi i te tangata nāna tētahi koha mai ki a koe. He kōura, he tipa, he tio tā rātou kai whakapaepae hei utu i ngā manaakitanga a te iwi kāinga.  kaihaukai (2)

pari kai [Papatūānuku] ing. He maha, he rahi hoki (o te kai). (i) Ko ngā pū pea a Te Whiti i karangatia kia hunā te pari kai, kia ngaro (W 267). (ii) Tēnā te pari kai kei ōku mātua, tē hikoi atu (W 267).

tāngā kai [Tāne] He pūkoro kei te korokoro o te manu hei pupuri i ana kai, ka paku nakunakua i reira i mua o te hekenga ki te puku.  tāngaengae (2)

kai2

1. [Tāne] ing. Ētahi mea maha tonu. Takoto ana te kai nei a te kūkupa (W 86).

2. [Tāne] He mea e tino kaha ana, e ranea ana te tupu, te hua mai rānei i tētahi wāhi. (i) Kua kite ia i te whenua, tōna kai, he pounamu, he moa (W 86). (ii) He wāina te kai a Te Wairau.

3. [Tāne] He taonga, he āhuatanga, he aha atu rānei kei tēnei ao e mōhio nei tātou; koia anō te kupu ka whakamahia hei tohu i tērā kāore e taea ana e te kaikōrero te āta whakaingoa, hei aha noa iho rānei te whakaingoa. (i) Te kai i pura ai te kanohi o Tahuaroa (W 86). (ii) Heoi anō te kai e pahure i ō tātau nei huinga maha, i ō te iwi Māori, he pūhaehae, he tautohetohe mātauranga (TT 2, wh50).  mea1 (1)

4. [Ranginui] āhua. Nui te hua, nui te kai (o te tau). (i) He tau kai tēnei, e kī ana ngā rua, ngā pātaka. (ii) Mehemea he tau kai, kāore e nui te moni ka hoki mai i aku huawhenua.

kai3

1. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka āta mahi tētahi mea i te mahi e tika ana kia tutuki i a ia. (i) E whiti, e te rā, e kai ki taku kiri (W 86). (ii) I te ata ka kōkiri, e kai ana te patu (W 86). (iii) E kore e mau i a koe, he wae kai pakiaka (W 86). (iv) E kai ana te mīhini horoi maitai - kia puta mai ngā pereti, kua pīataata katoa.

2. ‑nga ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka puta rawa ki tētahi wāhi, ka tau rawa ki tētahi wāhi. (i) Kai ana ki te rae, te one Hākere (W 86). (ii) Omanga a tima mai nōu, e Kāwana, kai tonu ki te rae o Te Matau rā, ia, ē (W 86).  tae1 (1)

kai-

[Tāne] pīa. E tohu ana i te tangata, i te hanga rānei ko ia kei te kawe i te mahi e whakaaturia ana i te wāhanga tuarua o te kupu. (i) Nō te kaikaranga te reo tuatahi ka rangona ka whakaeke ana he ope i te marae. (ii) I te roa o ngā kakau o ngā kaikōrero, ka hipa noa atu ngā mihimihi i te wā i whakaritea. (iii) E whitu ngā kaitākaro ki te tīma poitarawhiti. (iv) E kīia ana e te Pākehā he puruhi te kaitītaritari i te mate kino nei, i te pūponika (TT 1, 3).

kāī1

[Tāne] ing. He māhuri kahikatea. Dacrycarpus dacrydioides.

kāī2

[Tāne] ing. He rākau e 25 mita te tupu. Prumnopitys taxifolia.  mataī
