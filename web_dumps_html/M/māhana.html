He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mahana

1. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E pai ana te wera, arā, kāore i pāwera, i pōkākā, kāore hoki i makariri. (i) Me horoi te tūroro ki te wai mahana ia rā ia rā, kauaka ki te wai makariri (T 1, 43). (ii) Mā te mahana o te rā me te heke o te ua e whakakorikori te tipu o ngā kai (Wh 54, 6). {pūāhuru, pūmahana, pūwera, werawera}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kua mahia ki te papanga pupuri i te mahana o te tinana (o te kākahu me ētahi momo uhi). (i) Kua tae atu hoki he kākahu mahana mō te ope (Pi 26, wh8). (ii) Ko aku hoa kei te moe tonu i roto i ō rātau moenga mahana (Wh 5, 3).

māhana

[Tāne] tkp. He kupu e tohu ana mā tētahi tangata i tua atu i te kaikōrero me te kaiwhakarongo te mea, te mahi, te aha atu e kōrerotia ana. (i) Kāore hoki i kitea he wahine māhana (W 162). (ii) Mehemea e rua ō tīkiti me hoatu koe i tētahi māhana anō.  māna
