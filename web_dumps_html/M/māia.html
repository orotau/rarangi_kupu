He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



māia

1. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp. E ū tonu ana ki tētahi mahi uaua, ki tētahi mahi mōrearea me te kore e mataku, ahakoa rānei te mataku; e manawanui ana, e manawa kai tūtae ana. (i) Ka whakawhiwhia a ia ki te tohu Rīpeka mō te Toa mō āna mahi māia i te mura o te ahi (TTR 3, 27). (ii) He rawe tāna tū mai i mua i te tini ki te whakahē i a rātou, he tangata māia. {aumangea, marohi, mārohirohi2 (2), mōrohirohi, niwha, ngana, pākaha, taitoa, toa1 (5)}

2. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te ū tonu ki tētahi mahi uaua, ki tētahi mahi mōrearea me te kore e mataku, ahakoa rānei te mataku. Ka hau ngā rongo o Te Keepa i te pakanga nei nā runga i tana māia me tana kakama (TTR 1, 271). {hautoa, hautū, niwha, para3, tara1 (5), toa1 (3)}

3. [Tūmatauenga] He tangata māia kua tino eke i a ia ngā mahi a Tūmatauenga. Na, whakahaua iho e ia ngā māia o tōna whenua . . . kia uru mai hei tangata mō tōna ope (KtKM 6, 12).  toa1 (2)

4. [Tangaroa] He hanga weriweri, he hanga whakamataku, he kai tangata, he wā anō e noho kaitiaki ai, he wai hōhonu tōna kāinga. Kātahi ka āta mātakitakitia te māia e huna nei i te tangata (W 166).  taniwha (1)
