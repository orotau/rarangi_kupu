He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kaho1

1. [Tāne] ing. He kahokaho takoto huapae i runga i ngā heke o te whare hei takotoranga mō ngā apa rauwhare. Kāore te whare i tau i te tāhuhu anake, nā ngā heke me ngā kaho, me ngā tara, kātahi ka tau (Wa 2, 34).

kaho pātū [Tāne] Ngā kaho e pātata ana ki te pakitara o te whare.

2. [Tāne] He toko rere whakapae ka whakamaua ki ngā pou o te taiapa, hei piringa mō ngā wana. (i) E whakawhirinaki ana ia ki te kaho whakarunga o te mahau. (ii) He maitai te kaho o te arapiki. {huahua3, rōau, tangotango}

3. [Tāne] He rākau pūhou, e pakari ana te tupu engari kāore anō i pakeke noa, i nunui. Kia mau atu au te kaho tū ki te wao (W 83).  māhuri (1)

kaho2

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. Āhua whero nei te tae. Kia kaho haere ngā rōpere, ka katohia, ka tukuna ki ngā toa.  pūwhero (1)

2. [Tangaroa] ing. He mea e teatea ana, e hāmā ana te tae. Kāore e tere wera te kaho i ngā hihi o te rā, engari anō te taonga uriuri.

kāho

[Tāne] ing. reo kē. He ipu porotaka āhua nui tonu ka hangā ki te rākau, ki te maitai rānei, ka puku mai a waenganui o ngā taha, he putunga kai, he putunga inu, he putunga aha atu rānei. (i) Nō mua i te riri i Ōrākau, ka tukuna e Tāraia he kāho paura mā Rewi Maniapoto (TTR 1, 156). (ii) I te taenga atu ki te hōtēra, anā kē te nui o ngā kāho pia i hokona mai e rāua (TTR 5, 247).
