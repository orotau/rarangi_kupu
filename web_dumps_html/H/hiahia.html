He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hiahia

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka manako te ngākau ki tētahi mea, ki te mahi rānei i tētahi mahi. (i) Kāore au e hiahia ki tēnā kai - he hinuhinu rawa. (ii) Kei te hiahia hoki au ki te moe - kua pau taku hau. {āmene, aro1 (3), atawhai, hōkaka, huene, hurikiko, kaiwhiri1, kanehe, kōpana, koro1, koroingo, korou1, kuika, mamina, matainaina, matawara, mina, minaka, minamina, monoa, monowa, pīrangi, popore, pore1, tameme, tāpara1, tōmina, tuhira, tukorou, turou2, tutoko, uara, wara, warawara, wawara, whakakoro}

2. ‑tia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka mōhio te ngākau, te tinana rānei me whai rawa i tētahi mea, i tētahi mahi e tutuki ai tētahi kaupapa. E hiahiatia ana he pūweru mahana mō te haere ki Ruapehu.
