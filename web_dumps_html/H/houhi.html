He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



houhi

1. [Tāne] ing. He rākau 12 mita te tupu, he rau hohoko whai niho koi roa, he mā, he kakara ngā pua, i ōna wā anō ka ngahoro te nuinga o ngā rau. Hoheria populnea. {hoihere, houhere1 (1), (houhi ongaonga), houī, ongaonga1 (2), whauahi, whauwhi, wheuhi}

houhi ongaonga [Tāne] He rākau 12 mita te tupu. Hoheria populnea.  houhi (1)

2. [Tāne] He rākau 10 mita te tupu, he rau hohoko whai niho tōpuku, he pua mā, whetū te hanga. Hoheria glabrata. {hoihere, houhere1 (2), houī, whauwhau, whauwhi}

3. [Tāne] He rākau e 6 mita te tupu, he rau hohoko whai niho tōpuku, he huruhuru whetū te hanga kei ngā tātā, he mā ngā pua. Hoheria lyallii. {hoihere, houhere1 (3), houī, whauhi, whauwhi}

4. [Tāne] He rākau 10 mita te tupu, he rau hohoko, he kōratarata, he whāiti, he niho roa tonu kei ngā rau, he mā ngā pua whetū te hanga. Hoheria angustifolia. {hoihere, houhere1 (4), houī, hūngere, whauhi, whauwhi}

5. [Tāne] He rākau 15 mita te tupu, he rau hohoko whānui te take, whāiti te kāmata, he niho pūhuki kei te tapa o ngā rau, he pua karera pōī roa te tipu, he kahotea ngā hua. Plagianthus regius. {(houhi ongaonga), houī, mānatu, manaui, puruhi2, whauwhi}

houhi ongaonga [Tāne] He rākau 15 mita te tupu. Plagianthus regius.  houhi (5)
