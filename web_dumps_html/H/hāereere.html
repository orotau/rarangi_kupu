He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hāereere

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka haere noa me te kore e āta whakaaro e haere ana ki hea, he aha rānei te take i haere ai. Ka kitea te kōtiro wairangi e hāereere kau ana i te tāone. {aewa, amio, ānau1 (6), atiti, ātiu, ātiutiu1, hākoke, kaea, kaeha, kaewa, kāewaewa, kaihanu, kaihora, kōkewa, konewha, kōroiroi, kōtītiti, maewa, makihoi, ngau2, pakoke (pakoke haere), paoe, paoke, paowe, paro1, poipoi, pōrangi, raumaewa, tahawhenua, taiāmiki, takiwhenua, tangaere, tihoi1 (3), tīpao, tipi (tipi haere), tiu, tīwhao, whakakaewa, whakatairangi, whakatihake}

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka haere i tētahi haere pārekareka noa nei kia mahea ai te wairua. Ka rite ngā mea katoa, ka haere māua ko Ira ki runga i taku motukā, ki te hāereere mā māua (HP 257).
