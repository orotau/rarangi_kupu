He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kakano

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Te āhua o te takoto mai o ngā kaka ki te rākau, o ngā aho ki te papanga, te āhua rānei o te noho tahi mai o ngā hanga iti e kitea atu ai he tauira i roto i te kōhatu, i tētahi atu hanga rānei. (i) He mingimingi te kakano o te mānuka nei. (ii) Ka rawe hoki te kakano o te hiraka ki taku kiri. (iii) He tāekaeka te kakano o te tōtōweka.

2. [Tāne] Te taura e here ana i te tangata ki ōna tūpuna. E kore e hekeheke, he kakano rangatira (W 94).  kāwai1 (1)

3. [Haumia] He hua iti whai kākano (pērā i ērā ka kainga e te manu). Ina kai kakano te manu, ka riro ko ia te kaitītaritari i ngā kākano o taua rākau ki wīwī, ki wāwā.

4. [Ranginui] Te āhua o tētahi mea i te awatea, e whitu o ēnei e kitea ana i te āniwaniwa. He kaho, he pango ngā kakano o te piupiu.  tae2 (1)

kākano

[Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. Te wāhanga whakaputa uri o te tipu whai pua tērā ka whanake hei tipu hou; i te nuinga o te wā he iti, he mārō anō. Ka tere pihi ake ngā kākano ki te mahana te oneone, ki te hoatu anō he wai. {huri2, kano, pua1 (3), purapura}

kākano whakauru [Tūmatauenga] ing. He tangata nō ngā iwi e rua, arā nō ngā iwi e rua ōna whakapapa. He kākano whakauru taku whaea, nō Ngāti Whare rāua ko Ngāi Te Ōtawa.  taharua
