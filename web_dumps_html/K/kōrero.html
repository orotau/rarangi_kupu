He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōrero

1. ‑tia ‑hia ‑ngia ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa whakaaro ki tētahi atu, ka tohutohu i tētahi atu. (i) I te ata, ka kōrero mai aku mātua ki a au, 'Kaua anō koe e huri ki te aru i a Uenuku' (Wh 15, 6). (ii) Ka kōrerotia atu rātou - kāore tonu e whakarongo. He upoko mārō hoki. {kī1 (1), mea1 (4)}

kōrero pukapuka [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka titiro ki ngā kupu, ngā tohu rānei o tētahi tānga kōrero, ka mārama anō ki tāu i kite ai. Kia roa ia e kōrero pukapuka ana, ka rotua e te moe.  pānui (3)

2. ‑tia ‑hia ‑ngia ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa whakaaro kua āta whakakaupapahia, ki te tokomaha. Kei te haere mai tētahi wahine ki tō mātou kura ki te kōrero ki a mātou mō ngā taonga puoro.

3. ‑tia ‑hia ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakamahi i te reo kia puta ai he kupu e pā ana ki tētahi mea, ka whakamōhio. (i) Kua kōrerotia ake nei ā rātou pakanga ko Wairangi mā ki ngā tāngata whenua o tēnei motu (IwiT 26:1). (ii) Mā wai te kuia e kōrero kua mate tana hoa? {kī1 (2)}

4. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakamahi i te reo kia puta ai he kupu. (i) Me nohopuku ngā ākonga ina kōrero ana te pouako. (ii) He tokomaha tonu ngā mātua Māori i mutu ai tā rātau kōrero Māori ki ā rātau tamariki i te kāinga, i reira ka raru nui te reo Māori (RK 3). {hamumu, kī1 (6), kīkī, korokī, kupu}

kōrero rākau [Tāne] Ka whakaputa whakaaro i te wā tonu e toko ake ai te whakaaro; ka kōrero ki a koe anō. Kaua e aro mai ki a au, kei te kōrero rākau noa iho au. {motatau, murakehu, pātīhau, pūrākau (kōrero pūrākau)}

5. [Tāne] ing. Te whakawhitinga whakaaro ōpaki (a te tokorua, neke atu), he mea kawe ā-waha. Ko tā māua kōrero i te waea i nakua nei, mō tana kōtiro e haere nei ki te kura tuarua i tēnei tau.

6. [Tūmatauenga] He whakaaro ka whakahuatia, ka whakapuakina e te waha tangata. Ka puta te kōrero a Pourua i reira: 'Ka poroa a uta, ka poroa a tai; ko te iti o Pourua, ko te nui o Rauru' (B 98).  kī1 (7)

7. [Rongo] He kupu ka tae ake ki te taringa e whakaatu ana i tētahi āhuatanga kua pā. E umere ana te iwi i te taenga ake o te kōrero kua mutu te pakanga.  rongo (6)

kōrero ahiahi [Tāne] He kōrero ka tāraia e te hinengaro, kāore ōna mana, kāore ōna kiko.  takuahi (kōrero takuahi)
