He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōmore

1. [Tāne] ing. He hanga whakapaipai mō te kawititanga o te ringa, mō te whatīanga raparapa rānei, i whiria ki te harakeke, ki te aha atu, he huruhuru manu, he kota rānei ka tautau mai; he moko e huri ana i te kawititanga o te ringa, i te whatīanga raparapa rānei. (i) Kia kumu tōu ringa kei ngahoro ōu kōmore (W 132). (ii) Ko te kōmore e whakarākai ana i tana whatīanga raparapa, he mea whiri ki te whītau.  tauri (tauri kōmore)

2. [Tangaroa] He mātaitai rei te hanga, e 4 henemita te tupu, he noho ki te takere o tai hōhonu, he mā, he kiwikiwi te anga. Antalis nana. {pipi (pipi kōmore), pipi (pipi taiari)}

3. [Tāne] He pūtake kōekoeko tipu torotika ki roto i te oneone, koia nei te pū e tipu ai ngā piakaaka.  more (1)
