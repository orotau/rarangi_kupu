He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kuhu

1. ‑na ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia uru atu, kia noho atu rānei tētahi mea ki roto i tētahi atu hanga. (i) Me kuhu tō ringa ki roto i te paipa, ka tango mai i te mea e aukati ana i te rere o te wai. (ii) Kotahi te kōhao o te ngira e kuhuna ai te miro mā, te miro pango, me te miro whero (NP 1507). (iii) Hei ētahi wā anō hoki kua kuhuna he kōrero hei whakakata i ngā kaiwhakarongo (PR 64). {kokomo1, kōkuhu, komo, puru1 (4), whakauru}

2. ‑na ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] Ka whai kia kore tētahi mea e kitea. Ka kuhuna ana hū hou ki raro i te moenga kia kore ai tana hoa e pātai mai e hia te utu.  huna (1)

3. ‑na ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka puru atu i ngā ringa, i ngā waewae rānei (ki te kākahu, ki ngā hū rānei), kia mau ai. (i) Kuhuna tō koti - kei te makariri a waho. (ii) Ka kuhu te koroua i tana tarau, ka whakamau ai i te tātua. (iii) Me kuhu ō hū ki te hiahia tākaro koe ki waho. {komo} whakamau (3)

4. ‑na ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka haere ki roto. Ka kuhu atu ia, kite tonu atu i ngā whakaahua o ana tūpuna e iri mai rā i te tuarongo.  uru2 (1)

5. [Tāne] ing. He rūma, he whare, he whakaruruhau rānei ki reira tunua ai te kai. Kua rerekē katoa ngā kuhu o ēnei rā - kua waiho mā te hiko, mā te kapuni e tunu ngā kai - hei aha te ahi.  kāuta
