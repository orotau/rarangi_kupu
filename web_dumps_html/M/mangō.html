He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mangō

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He whānau ika tai he roa ngā tinana, he tutū tētahi o ōna urutira, he ika orotā, he ika tukituki, he ika whakamataku. (i) Ko te ururoa arā ko te mangō he toa ki te riri (KRK 87). (ii) Kua mōhio rātou ko te wāhi tēnā o te moana e muia nuitia ana e tētahi tū mangō nanakia rawa, ā e kore anō hoki rātou e ora i taua taniwha horo tangata (WM 10, 26). {makō}

mangō au pounamu [Tangaroa] He momo mangō neke atu i te 2 mita te tupu. Prionace glauca.  (mangō pounamu)

mangō hapū [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1.2 mita te tupu. Squalus spp.  (mangō tara)

mangō ihunui [Tangaroa] He momo mangō neke atu i te 2 mita te tupu. Notorhyncus cepedianus.  tuatini (2)

mangō makomako [Tangaroa] He momo mangō neke atu i te 3 mita te tupu. Isurus glaucus.  mako1 (1)

mangō matawhā [Tangaroa] He mangō e 3 mita te tipu, he kiri rauwhero, he mā te puku, he mimira ngā niho o runga, he koi, he pūhihi, he tūtika ngā niho o raro, he tōkari kei mua i te hiku. Carcharinus brachyurus. {horopekapeka, matawhā, ngengero (mau ngengero), ngengero, ngerungeru, tuatini}

mangō pare [Tangaroa] He momo mangō e 3 mita te tupu, he kiwikiwi a runga, he kōwhai teatea a raro, ko ana karu kōwhai parauri kei ngā pito e rua o tana upoko whakapae, he mangō kai tangata. Sphyrna zygaena. {kakere}

mangō pekapeka [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1 mita te tupu, he hāurauri ngā whakairoiro i tana kiri parauri, he āhua pāraharaha tana tinana, ko ana kai kei te takere o te moana. Cephaloscyllium isabella. {pekapeka}

mangō pekepeke [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1.2 mita te tupu. Squalus spp.  (mangō tara)

mangō pounamu [Tangaroa] He momo mangō neke atu i te 2 mita te tupu. Prionace glauca. {(mangō au pounamu), matawhā, pounamu (taha pounamu), pounamu}

mangō ripi [Tangaroa] He momo mangō ka mōhiotia i te rite o te roa o tana hiku ki tana upoko me tana tinana, he kahurangi kiwikiwi a runga, he mā a raro, he iti tana waha me ana niho. Alopias supercilliosus.

mangō taniwha [Tangaroa] He momo mangō e 5 mita neke atu te tupu, he kiwikiwi a runga, he mā a raro, he pūtaratara kei ngā tapa o ana niho tapatoru te hanga, he mangō orotā, he toa ki te riri, he mangō tukituki, he mangō horo tangata. Carcharodon carcharias. {(mangō tuatini), (mangō ururoa), taniwha (makō taniwha), ururoa}

mangō tara [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1.2 mita te tupu, he tara kotahi kei mua i ōna urutira e rua, he kiwikiwi te tae; ko tētahi momo anō kei te taha tonga o Te Moana-o-Raukawa, he kiwikiwi parauri, he kōtingotingo mā nui anō ōna. Squalus spp. {huarau, kāraerae, koinga, (mangō hapū), (mangō pekepeke), okeoke, pepeke, pioka, pioke, pīokeoke}

mangō tuatini [Tangaroa] He momo mangō e 5 mita neke atu te tupu. Carcharodon carcharias.  (mangō taniwha)

mangō ururoa [Tangaroa] He momo mangō e 5 mita neke atu te tupu. Carcharodon carcharias.  (mangō taniwha)

2. [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1 mita te tupu, he kōtingotingo māwhe kei te tuarā o tana tinana kiwikiwi, he iti, he pūhuki ana niho. Mustelus lenticulatus. {kapetā, makō, mangā}
