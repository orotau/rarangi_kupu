He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



niwha

1. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp. E ū tonu ana ki tētahi mahi uaua, ki tētahi mahi mōrearea me te kore e mataku, ahakoa rānei te mataku; e manawanui ana, e manawa kai tūtae ana. He tamaiti niwha, kāore e tuohu noa ina rokohanga he raruraru.  māia (1)

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] E taikaha ana, e kairiri ana te āhua o te kawe (a te tangata, a te kararehe rānei) i a ia, tana āhua, tana mahi rānei. E tika ana anō kia niwha te whāereere ina whakatata atu koe ki ana kūao.  uraura (2)

3. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] E kaikā ana ki te rīriri, ki te whawhai me te whakatumatuma anō. He tamaiti niwha, he tere tū ngā pihi. {horepatatai, horetātā, horetītī, muha, tainanakia}

4. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te ū tonu ki tētahi mahi uaua, ki tētahi mahi mōrearea me te kore e mataku, ahakoa rānei te mataku. Ka maumaharatia te niwha o Kaho i te kēmu - ahakoa ko ia te mea tino iti rawa, kāore i wehi.  māia (2)

5. [Tūmatauenga] Te riri tino kino, e kore e taea te pupuri. I te whakatūtū tonu te taua toto a Tūhoe, ka wehe a Te Purewa me tōna kotahi. Ahu tonu atu ia ki Te Pūaha-o-Kahu, ko tōna niwha e kawe ana i a ia (TTR 1, 259).  nguha (4)

6. [Tāne] He tara koi ka hoki paku ake i te koinga o te matau, o te tao, o te aha atu e uaua ai te tango mai ina titi ki tētahi mea. I mea ia ki te peke i te taiapa ka mau tana tarau i ngā niwha o te taiapa.  kāniwha (1)
