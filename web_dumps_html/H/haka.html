He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



haka1

1. hakā ‑ina ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka whakaputa (ā-kapa nei) i ngā whakaaro ki tētahi kaupapa me te haere anō o ngā ringa, te pūkana anō o ngā karu, te whātero anō o te arero, te takahi anō o te waewae, ki te whakatinana i ngā kupu e whakapuakina rā. (i) Kātahi a ia ka hoki ki te taha whakararo o te awa, i reira nei ngā taua me ā rātau pū e huihui ana; kātahi rātau ka haka, ā, ka puhipuhi i ā rātau pū (TTR 1, 292). (ii) Kātahi ka hakā e rātou, koia tēnei tā rātou haka (W 31).

2. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He mahi whakangahau, he mahi whakawehiwehi, he mahi whakaihiihi, e tū mai ai te tangata, tētahi kapa rānei ki te whakaputa i ō rātou whakaaro ki tētahi kaupapa. Ka haere ngā ringa, ka pūkana ngā karu, ka whātero te arero, ka horu te waha, ka takahi anō te waewae, ki te whakatinana i ngā kupu e whakapuakina rā. (i) E tū ana te haka, he tohu whenua rangatira te haka (W 159). (ii) Me āta takahi te waewae, kei kahakina tā tātou haka. (iii) Ka mutu te haka o te iwi whenua kei runga ko te ope manuwhiri, ā, uru katoa atu ana rātou ki ngā kapa o te haka, kia haruru ai te tioro o te taringa i te haka (Wh 2, 6).

haka a Tānerore [Tāne] Te kārohirohi o te hau takiwā i te kaha o te wera. Me te mea tērā ko te haka a Tānerore, e wiriwiri nei i te raumati (PH 3, 2).

haka2

‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahw, mahp. E hē ana te āhua, te hanga rānei (o te tinana), arā, kāore i rite ki te āhua, ki te hanga e tika ana. Ka hia ngā tamariki haka i whānau mai i muri i te tukutanga i te pahū karihi, ko ētahi kāore he waewae, ko ētahi kāore he ringa, he mati rānei. {ngunu1 (4)}
