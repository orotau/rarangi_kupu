He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hoki1

1. ‑a ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka haere (tētahi tangata, tētahi hanga rānei) ki te wāhi, ki te kaupapa, ki te āhuatanga rānei i reira ia i mua. (i) Ka hoki ia ki te wā kāinga ki te kite i tana kuia. (ii) Kua hoki anō ia ki ana mahi petipeti. (iii) Ka aroha anō a Kiri - kua hoki mai tana mate pukupuku. (iv) Ka hoki ngā mahara o Pita ki ērā kai, ngā parāoa i tunua e tōna whaea, me ngā tāmure nunui nā rātau i hī mai i te moana (Wh 49, 5). (v) Mawhiti te weka i te māhanga, e hokia anō? (Pi 56, wh8) (vi) Taihoa anō hokia ake ai ngā kōrero mō Hoturoa mā (IwiT 4:8).

2. [Tāne] ing. He karakia whakaora, arā, mō te haumanu tūroro, mō te māra kōaro, me ētahi mea. Takina te hoki kia ora ai tā tāua tūroro nei.

hoki2

1. [Tāne] tūkē. E tohu ana ko te mea e whakahuatia ana i mua tonu, i ngā taha e rua rānei o te kupu nei, he tāpiritanga ki te whakaaro kua whakahuatia i mua tata atu. (i) Tētahi tikanga, he kai tapu mō te tūātanga tamariki, mō te hahunga tūpāpaku hoki (NM4 303, Kwh). (ii) Nā T. W. Downes i kohikohi ngā kupu, i tuku hoki ki a Te Pēhi rāua ko Te Mete (NM4 302, Kwh). (iii) Kei te tata tonu rā te kupu ki a koe, kei roto i tōu waha, kei roto hoki i tōu ngākau (PT Tiu 30.14).

2. [Tāne] thu. He kupu whakakaha ake, whakaū i tētahi whakaaro kātahi tonu nei ka puta. (i) Tō mōhio hoki! (ii) A: Mōhio koe, he tino matapiko a Rangi. K: Āe rā hoki! (iii) A: Ko wai ia nei tana ingoa? K: Aua hoki! (iv) A: Ko wai tērā e hāparangi mai rā, ko Kiri? K: Ko wai atu hoki!

hoki3

[Tangaroa] ing. He ika tai e 60-120 henemita te tipu, he nui ōna karu me tōna waha, he tinana kōekoeko tōna, he koi te hiku, he kānapanapa a runga, he hiriwa ngā kaokao me te puku. Macruronus novaezelandiae.
