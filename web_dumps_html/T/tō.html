He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



tō1

1. ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakapau kaha ki te haukume (i tētahi mea) kia whai haere i a koe; ka haukume kia roa. (i) I te ata pō tonu, ka maranga a Tāwhaki ki te whakaoho i ōna hoa, kia haere rātou ki te tō i te hīnaki ki uta (Wh 1, 4). (ii) Heoi anō tāku nā pātai āianei, ko te āhua o te tō rānei, kāore rānei e tō, arā, ko te whakahua tika ko Whangārei, ko Whāngārei kē rānei (H 33). (iii) Kāore e tōia roatia te kōrero, kei te rere te wā. {hautō, kukume, kume, kumekume, tōtō2}

tō ā-papa [Ranginui] ing. hou. He tōpana e kumea whakarotohia ai tētahi hanga whai papatipu ki te pūtahi o Papatūānuku, ki te pūtahi rānei o tētahi atu ao tuarangi. (i) Ki te kore he tō ā-papa, kua kore tātou e tū noa ki te mata o Papatūānuku, kua rere kau ki tuarangi. (ii) He tō ā-papa anō tō te marama, engari ko tētahi hauono noa iho o te kaha o tō Papatūānuku, i te mea he iti noa ake te marama i a Papatūānuku. {kume (kume ā-papa), (tō whakaroto)}

tō whakaroto [Ranginui] hou. He tōpana e kumea whakarotohia ai tētahi hanga whai papatipu ki te pūtahi o Papatūānuku, ki te pūtahi rānei o tētahi atu ao tuarangi. Nā te tō whakaroto o Tamanuiterā i pūmau ai te amio a tana whānau i a ia.  (tō ā-papa)

2. ‑ia ‑nga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kukume (i te tatau, i te matapihi) hei huaki, hei kati rānei i a ia. (i) Tōia ake te tatau kia tuwhera (W 428). (ii) Ka tōia te papa o te mataaho.

3. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka kawe whakapae i te taiaha ki te ringa kaha, ko te rau ki mua, ko te upoko ki muri.

tō marino [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E tau pai ana (tētahi horanga wai), kāore e ngarungaru ana i te hau. Ka haere te kōkopu ki ngā tāheke whānau ai, kāore e noho ki ngā wāhi tō marino (W 428).  marino (1)

tō2

‑nga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka heke atu (te rā) ki raro o te tūāpae. (i) Ka tō te rā, ka pōuriuri, taihoa ka pō. (ii) E hika mā, kei te raumati a konei, hei te whitu ka tō te rā (Pi 55, wh2). {ahuahu3, hekeheke, kengo, nenewha, newha, opu, pō, rehurehu, toene1, torengi, torongi, towene, wharara}

tō3

1. ‑nga ‑kanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka noho mai he kukune ki te kōpū. Ka tō pōriro au (W 428).  hapū (1)

2. ‑nga ‑kanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka hono te tātea tāne ki te hua wahine i roto i te whare tangata, kia kukune mai he tamaiti hou. Ka moe a Tāwhaki, ā, tō noa tā rāua tamaiti (W 428).

tō4

‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka pani (i tētahi wāhi o te tangata) ki te hinu, ki te kōkōwai, ki tētahi atu hanga rānei, hei whakatutuki i te tapu, hei whakaihi rānei i te tangata ki tētahi mahi motuhake. Ka tōia te kiri ki te kōkōwai (W 428).  whakawahi (1)

tō5

[Tāne] ing. Te wāhanga roroa o te tipu e piki whakarunga ana mai i te papa, e tautoko ana i ērā atu wāhi ōna; te wāhanga o te tipu e mau mai ai ngā rau, ngā pua, ngā hua rānei. E ai ki te kōrero, ki te toua atu te tō o te rōhi ki te wai korohū, ka roa ake e ora ana i te ipu.  tātā2 (1)

tō6

[Tāne] pmu. Te kupu māmā mō te 'tāu' me te 'tōu', heoi anō, e taea ana te whakamahi i ngā wāhi anake ka whai i muri tonu mai te mea e whakaingoatia ana. (i) Kei te makariri tonu a waho - kei hea kē tō pōtae? (ii) E haria ana tō take ki te hui a te komiti matua.  tāu, tōu

tō7

[Tāne] ptm. Ka noho te kupu nei ki mua i ngā tūkapi mō te tokorua, te tokotoru neke atu, i tētahi ingoa, i tētahi tūingoa, i tētahi kīanga rānei, e tohu ana nōna taua mea. (i) He rawe tō rātou pāpā - he tangata ngahau. (ii) Kei te mākū tonu tōku koti - haria tō Hine.

tō8

[Tūmatauenga] ing. reo kē. He taputapu tahu kai ka whakawerahia ki te hiko, ki te kapuni, ki te waro, ki te wahie rānei. Heoi anō tāu he whakakā i te tō, he whakanoho atu i te kōhua, kāore e roa kua maoa te kai.  umu (2)
