Māngere

[Ranginui] ing. Te marama tuatoru o te tau. Kāore e matomato te tipu o ngā tini a Tāne i te Māngere i te mea he makariri rawa.  Hereturikōkā

māngere

‑tanga [Rongo] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e kori ake te tinana ki te mahi, ki te aha. Kua kitea nuitia ko ngā iwi māngere e whiwhi ana rātou ki te utu o te māngere, arā, he mate (KRK 63). {ānau1 (2), hākiki, hakorea, hakurara, hakurea, huhure, kutekute, maeko1, māhonge, māikoiko, makihoi, mākoko, makuku, ngehe, pakihore, pakukore, paraheahea, toupiore, tūkeke, tūmaeo, waimori, whakatoreke, whakatōrekereke}

māngere hōnia [Rongo] Tino kino te māngere. He kai anake te mahi ka pahure i tēnā tangata māngere hōnia.
