He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mata1

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te taha whakamua o te upoko, mai i te kauae ki te rae. Ka huna i tana mata ki ngā panekoti o tana whaea i te kaha whakamā.  kanohi1 (1)

2. [Tāne] ing. Te papanga whakarunga rawa, te taha whakawaho rānei (o tētahi mea). (i) Ka nui te kōhatu i te mata o te whenua nei. (ii) Ka takoto mai te hinu ki te mata o te wai, ki reira whakararu ai i ngā manu tai. (iii) E ono ngā mata o te mataono tau, he rerekē te tau i tēnā mata, i tēnā. {kahu}

3. [Tāne] Ngā wāhanga e rua i te kanohi e taea ai te kite. Ko aku mata anake i whakamau ki waho rā ei, e kore koe e kitea i te rehu waitai ei (PH 5, 20).  karu1 (1)

mata hewa [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore he tukemata; he tangata kāore ōna tukemata. (i) He mea āta tuhi atu ngā pewa o kui mata hewa. (ii) I wera ngā tukemata o te mata hewa rā i te ahi.

mata tangata [Tangaroa] ing. He mātaitai pōtaka pūhuki te hanga. Turbo smaragdus.  pūpū2 (1)

4. [Tūmatauenga] Te wāhi tino koi, tino whāiti o tētahi taputapu pērā i te māripi, i te kāheru. He mahi nui tāu ki te keri i te rua ki te pūhuki te mata o tō kāheru. {niao, niho}

5. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. Te wāhi o te kupenga kua whatua, te wāhi e mau ai he ika. He kahawai tērā i mau ki te mata o te kupenga. {papa1 (12), raumata, tākekenga}

6. [Papatūānuku] ing. He pito whenua teitei e toro atu ana i te tahatika ki te moana, ki te roto rānei. Ngā mata tāhuna i waho Tauranga.  mātārae (1)

7. [Ranginui] Te wāhi o te rorohiko, o te pouaka whakaata, o te aha atu e puta ai, e kitea ai ngā āhua, ngā kupu. (i) E hura ō mata ki te mata o te rorohiko ka toro i ngā taonga o te motu (HM 11, 3). (ii) Kua kotahi atu te kurī heahea ki te whawhai i te ngeru kua puta ake ki te mata o te pouaka whakaata.

mata2

1. ‑nga [Haumia] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Kāore i tunua, kāore rānei i māoa. (i) Ka tae ki te aroaro o tōna matua, ka whakaruakina te kiwi me ngā mātaitai i kainga matatia rā e ia (N 67). (ii) Kei te rere tonu te toto i ngā mīti o te hāngī, kei te mata tonu. {kaimata, kōmata, mangungu, ota, tōngāngā, whakarae2, wheo2}

2. ‑nga [Haumia] Kāore anō i maoa, kāore anō pea i reka (o te huarākau). (i) Kua ngau taku puku i te kai paramu mata. (ii) I kainga matatia te karaka, mea kau ake, kua ngau te puku (KK 147). {hangongi, kaimata, kaiota1, kānewha2 (2), ota, torouka1}

mata3

1. [Tāne] ing. He waka atua, arā, he huarahi e whakawhitiwhiti kōrero ai te tangata ki tōna atua, te atua rānei ki te tangata.

2. [Tāne] He momo karakia. He mata tawhito te karakia a Tamure, hei rongoā mōna (W 186).

mata rākau [Tūmatauenga] He karakia ka whakahaeretia mō ngā rākau whawhai i mua i te pakanga. {hoa3 (hoa rākau), kī1 (kī tao), waniwani}

mata rere puku [Whiro] He momo mākutu.

3. [Tāne] He waiata e whakakite ana i ētahi āhuatanga kei te tū mai. Ko te mata tēnei mō te matenga o Pukerangiora (W 186).

matamata aitū [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata ka kite i tētahi tohu, i tētahi hanga e mōhiotia ai he aha ka pā āpōpō, ka whakamārama rānei i ngā tohu ka puta, ka kōrero ki ngā atua kia mōhio ai he aha ka pā āpōpō. Ki te kino te tapu o te matamata aitū, kua kahupō ia, kua kore e kite i ngā tohu a ngā atua.  matakite (1)

mata4

[Tāne] ing. He harakeke tino roa āhua māwhero rauwhero nei te tae i te mea he whero ngā iaia o ngā rito, he whero ngā tapa me te tuaka, he harakeke whakapaipai. Phormium tenax.

matā

1. [Rūaumoko] ing, āhua. He toka puia pango, he mōhinuhinu, he mārō, whakamahia nuitia ai i ngā rā o mua hei tapahi, hei ripiripi.  tūhua

matā kautete [Tūmatauenga] ing. He taputapu haehae i mahia ki te niho mangō, ki te pāra matā rānei i herea ki tētahi kakau.  mira

matā paretao [Rūaumoko] ing, āhua. He toka puia pango, he mōhinuhinu, he mārō, whakamahia nuitia ai i ngā rā o mua hei tapahi, hei ripiripi.  tūhua

matā tūhua [Rūaumoko] He toka puia pango, he mōhinuhinu, he mārō, whakamahia nuitia ai i ngā rā o mua hei tapahi, hei ripiripi. Kātahi ka haehaea ki te matā whaiapu, ki te matā tūhua (W 185).  tūhua

2. [Papatūānuku] ing. He konganuku taumaha he kiwikiwi kahurangi te tae, he kōngohe, he māngohe, he āhuatanga tāoke tōna. (Tohu pūtaiao: Pb) I whakamahia nuitia te matā i mua hei hanga kōrere wai, i whakaurua anō ki te peita me te penehīni. {konumatā}

3. [Tūmatauenga] He anga maitai ka pūhia mai i te pū, he rango te hanga, he koi tētahi pito. I taua wā, ka pakū anō ana pū, ka kite atu anō au i te taunga o ngā matā i mua tonu i taku upoko, nā te tutū hoki o te puehu (HP 185). {hāmanu, hāmoamoa, pokepoke2}

4. [Tāne] He pepeke 10 mirimita te roa, kei raro kōhatu i te takutai tana kāinga, he pango, he pīataata te tinana, he kuku kei te tou, kāore ōna parihau. Anisolabus littorea.

matā kai kutu [Tūmatauenga] He tangata māia kua tino eke i a ia ngā mahi a Tūmatauenga. Ka aroha rā ngā matā kai kutu i hinga i te ngutu o te pū.  toa1 (2)

māta

[Tūmatauenga] kīa. E tohu ana me tūpato te tangata i ana kōrero, i ana mahi rānei. (i) Māta kohukohu mai koe i konā! (KK 110) (ii) Ei, māta takahi tō waewae. Kei whakaahuatia tāua e te kāmera rā.

mātā1

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Ētahi mea kua whakawhāitihia mai ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Kua oti te māra te ngaki, inā kē te nui o te mātā tarutaru kua hua ake.  haupū (3)

2. [Papatūānuku] He hanga papatahi e hora ana ki tētahi mata, e takoto ana rānei i waenganui i ētahi mea e rua. Ina titiro koe ki te pari, ka kitea atu ngā mātā kōhatu, kirikiri kua takoto mai i ngā tau tini whāioio.  papanga (1)

mātā2

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. He pito whenua noho mākū tonu, he tāpokopoko, he hōhonu, me te tipuria e te raupō, e te harakeke, e te aha atu. Kāore e mutu te tangi mai a te poraka i te mātā. {mātātā1, mato}

2. [Tāne] He rākau e 2 mita te tupu. Rhabdothamnus solandri.  kaikaiatua1

mātā3

1. [Tāne] ing. He manu noho pātītī taranui, noho māheuheu, noho raupō me ērā momo wāhi. Bowdleria punctata.  mātātā2 (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika tai e 30-50 henemita te tipu. Caprodon longimanus.  maomao2 (2)

mātā4

whare mātā [Tāne] ing. He whare e whakaritea ai, e whakaputua ai ngā taputapu mahi manu, mahi kiore, me ētahi atu taputapu e pā ana ki te ngahere. Ko te whare mātā, he whare whāpiko rau huka (W 185).
