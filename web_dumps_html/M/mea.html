He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mea1

1. [Tāne] ing. He taonga, he āhuatanga, he aha atu rānei kei tēnei ao e mōhio nei tātou; koia anō te kupu ka whakamahia hei tohu i tērā kāore e taea ana e te kaikōrero te āta whakaingoa, hei aha noa iho rānei te whakaingoa. (i) Me āta heri atu ngā mea katoa e tika ana mō te noho ki rō ngahere. (ii) Ko tēnei mea te pūhaehae, he āhuatanga nō tāua, nō te tangata. (iii) He aha te mea rā? (iv) Tēnā homai te mea nā e kare. {hanga, kai2 (3), taru2}

2. [Tūmatauenga] Te hanga tonu nāna i pā ai tētahi āhuatanga, te hanga rānei e whakamārama ana he aha i pā ai tētahi āhuatanga. (i) He wahine te mea i pura ai te kanohi o Tahuaroa (W 199). (ii) He wahine āhua pai tōna kanohi, ko te mea tērā i moe ai a Paoa i a ia (W 199).  take (3)

i te mea [Tāne] hono. Hei tīmata i tētahi kōrero e tohu ana i te take i mahia ai tētahi mahi, i pērā ai rānei tētahi āhuatanga. Kāore i whakaaetia taku uru atu ki te mātakitaki i te pikitia, i te mea he tamariki rawa au.  (nā te mea)

nā te mea [Tāne] Hei tīmata i tētahi kōrero e tohu ana i te take i mahia ai tētahi mahi, i pērā ai rānei tētahi āhuatanga. He makariri katoa a roto nei, nā te mea i wareware a Hēmi ki te tahu i te ahi. {(i te mea), (nō te mea), (tā te mea)}

nō te mea [Tāne] Hei tīmata i tētahi kōrero e tohu ana i te take i mahia ai tētahi mahi, i pērā ai rānei tētahi āhuatanga. Kāre aku moni, nō te mea i pau katoa ki te whakaea i te nama mō te hiko.  (nā te mea)

tā te mea [Tāne] Hei tīmata i tētahi kōrero e tohu ana i te take i mahia ai tētahi mahi, i pērā ai rānei tētahi āhuatanga. Ka tupu te whakaaro i a Tāwhirimātea kia tahuri mai ia ki te whawhai ki ōna tuākana mō te wehewehenga i ō rātou mātua, tā te mea kāore ia i whakaae kia wehea rāua (N 2).  (nā te mea)

3. ‑tia meinga ‑tanga ‑nga [Tāne] mahw. Ka mahi (i tētahi mahi).  He wā anō ka tū hei whakakapi mō tētahi tūmahi whāiti ake te tikanga. (i) Kaua e meatia, kaua rānei e tukua kia meatia tētahi mahi, e poke ai ngā wai o roto o ngā awa rerenga wai (Wa 2, 7). (ii) Me mea ngā pouaka nā ki runga i te tēpu. (iii) Ko au tēnei kua meinga nei e ngā taitama hai kāpene mō rātau (HP 151).

4. ‑tia meinga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa whakaaro ki tētahi atu, ka tohutohu i tētahi atu. (i) Ka takoto te koroua rā ka mea mai, 'E taku tamaiti, haramai rā' (Wh 40, 2). (ii) I mea mai ia māna e hoko ngā tīkiti.  kōrero (1)

5. ‑tia ‑hia meinga ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka hihiri ake, ka toko ake he āhuatanga (i tua atu i te kare ā-roto) i te hinengaro, i te ngākau. E mea ana a Hine he tangata purotu a Para.  whakaaro (5)

6. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] mahp. E tohu ana i te takanga o te wā. (i) Me te rere haere o te waka rā, a Te Arawa, mea, mea, ā, ka taka ki waho o Maketū (N 64). (ii) Ka pao mai he pīpī. Ka mea, ka turuki, ka mea, ka pakari (KK 141). (iii) Ka mea ā, ka timu anō te tai.

mea ake [Ranginui] tūkē. I tētahi wā poto i muri mai; ā tōna wā tūtata (ka puta tētahi āhuatanga). (i) E kōrero ana taua tangata mea ake ka pau ngā mīti . . . engari te parāoa me ētahi atu kai, e kore e pau wawe (WMA 6,18). (ii) He ritenga tawhito ia nā ngā Māori, mea ake ka mahue, pērā me ētahi atu ritenga tawhito kua mahue (WM 10,9).  taihoa (1)

mea kau ake [Ranginui] wā. Kāore noa iho i roa i muri mai; i tētahi wā tino poto i muri mai. (i) Kainga ana e te kurī te paitini, mea kau ake kua hukahuka te waha. (ii) Ka raua atu te kānga ki te kōhua wera, mea kau ake kua pahupahū. {taro1 (taro kau iho)}

mea2

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E rite ana ki te toto hauora te tae.  whero1 (1)

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui] Āhua whero nei te tae. Ka huki kei waho te ata ura, te ata mea (W 200).  pūwhero (1)
