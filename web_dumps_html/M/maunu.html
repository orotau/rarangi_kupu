He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



maunu

1. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tangohia mai, ka unuhia mai (i te tātua, i te pūkoro, i te aha atu rānei). (i) Maunu te parāoa, kua motu te upoko (W 197). (ii) Ka maunu i a ia te kauae o tana tupuna e huna ana i roto i tana uma (W 197).

2. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka makere atu, ka taka atu i te mea kua tangatanga, kua motu rānei tētahi momo here. (i) Te ākinga atu a tōna ringaringa i te toki ki te tapahi i te rākau, nā, kua maunu atu te rino i te kakau, pono tonu atu ki tōna hoa, ā mate iho (Ha 2, 8). (ii) Kei te wehi ahau kei maunu atu te taonga nei i a tātou (TT 61, wh454).

3. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka unuhia (te kākahu). (i) Ka maunu ngā kākahu, ka rere ki te wai (W 197). (ii) Ka eke a Kāwana i te hōiho, ka maunu ngā pōtae o te iwi katoa, hipi hipi hurētia ana e rātou a te Kāwana (PM 2, 5). {marere}

4. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka puta mai ki waho. (i) Ka maunu te taniwha i te rua (WM 10, 17). (ii) Kua maunu mai te Hauhau ki waho rawa mai o tō rātou pā (WMA 16, 3).

5. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka whakarere atu i tētahi wāhi, ka haere ki wāhi kē. (i) I muri iho o te pakanga kua kōrerotia ake nei ka maunu te ope a Waikato me Ngāti Hauā ka ahu whakatetonga (IwiT 54:4). (ii) Taka ana ki a Hoani Wiremu Hipango, māna te haere ki rāwāhi, hei whakatutuki i ngā tūmanakoranga o te iwi. Ka maunu atu a Hoani Wiremu Hipango . . . mā runga kaipuke (Wh 16, 1). {tāwhai}

6. ‑tanga [Tangaroa] Ka tīmata te ahu whakawaho (o te tai). Ka maunu te mata o te tai ka whakaterea te waka. {ketu}

māunu1

‑hia ‑tia ‑tanga [Tangaroa] ing, mahw, mahp, āhua. He kai ka whakamaua ki te matau, ka hoatu rānei ki te māhanga, ki tētahi atu hanga hopu ika, hopu kararehe rānei. (i) Kāore i tua atu i te noke hei māunu mō te tuna. (ii) Kia tūpato i a koe ka māunu i ngā matau, kei tū tō ringa. (iii) He paihamu tā rāua māunu mō te hīnaki. {mōunu, poa1 (2), poapoa}
