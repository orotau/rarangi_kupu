He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



manga

1. [Tāne] ing. He toronga roroa e tipu mai ana i te kahiwi o te rākau. Ahakoa te whatiwhati mai o ngā manga o te kauri, e tū mārō tonu ana te kahiwi.  peka (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He rerenga wai ka whati mai i te awa matua me te whai i ara kē.  kāpeka (2)

3. [Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. He rerenga wai, ahakoa nui, ahakoa iti, ahakoa māori, ahakoa karia rānei e te tangata. Kāore au e mōhio mēnā he awa māori taua manga, he mea āta kari rānei e te ringa tangata.  kōawa (1)

4. [Tangaroa] He rerenga wai iti ka karia e te tangata. Ka karia he manga hei rerenga atu mō te wai, kia kore ai te whenua e tāpokopoko.  awakari

5. [Papatūānuku] ing. He rerenga wai māori tino iti e rere ana ki te awa, ki te kōawa, ki te roto, ki te moana rānei.  hawai (2)

6. [Tūmatauenga] He taputapu whai koromāhanga hei hopu manu. Kia mōmona te manu kua haere atu ōna tāngata mōhio ki te whakatikatika i ngā manga.  māhanga2 (1)

7. [Rongo] He huawhenua he kākāriki ngā rau. Ko ngā manga i hokona e rātou, he pūhā, he kāpeti, he rētihi, he pōhata.

mangā

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika tai e 60-90 henemita te tipu, he pukepoto a runga, he hiriwa a raro, he ika kātoretore, he pango te urutira, he roa te kauae, he nui, he tino koi ngā niho. Thyrsites atun. {makā}

2. [Tangaroa] He momo mangō 1 mita te tupu. Mustelus lenticulatus.  mangō (2)

mānga

1. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Rongo] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whāngai (i te pēpē) ki te kai mārō kua ngaungaua kia ngāwari. Mehemea kāore e ora tō pēpē i te waiū anahe engari kāore anō i whai niho, me mānga.

2. [Rongo] ing. Ngā kai ka toe mai ina mutu te kai. Ka kite i te mānga aruhe e takoto ana, ka noho, ka kai (W 177).
