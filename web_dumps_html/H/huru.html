He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



huru1

1. [Tāne] ing. Te huinga weu e tipu ana i te upoko o te tangata. Waiho mā te hunga taitamariki, mā te huru pango e kawe ngā tūru nei ki waho.  makawe (1)

2. [Tāne] He hanga papatahi ka tipu mai i te kiri o te manu, he tātā piako āhua mārō kei waenganui, he weu e rere ana i ngā taha e rua. Ka hoka te huru manu (W 72).  huruhuru (3)

3. [Tāne] He rākau iti, he rākau hūtoitoi e manomano ana, e apiapi ana te tupu tahi. E tupuria ana taua mānia e te huru rākau (W 72).  ururua (1)

4. [Tāne] He kahu kiri kurī, he mā te tae. Ngā huru, ngā kākahu onamata o ngā rangatira (W 72).  (huru kurī)

huru kurī [Tāne] He kahu kiri kurī, he mā te tae. {huru1 (4)}

huru2

‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahw. He karakia mō te riri, mō te rākau whawhai. Ka tū te tangata ki te riri kātahi ka hurua te maro (W 72). {hurupiki}

huru3

1. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing. Te tukunga o te aho ngoikore me te mahana anō. Tōna māhunga pākākā, ko te huru ki te rangi te rite.  hana (4)

2. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tuku i te aho āhua ngoikore me te mahana anō.  ura (3)

3. ‑nga [Ranginui] Ka ara ake (o te rā). Kāore anō i huru te rā, kua takahi te tira i te huarahi.

huru4

1. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka ngākau kawa, kāore e pai ngā whakaaro (ki tētahi tangata, ki tētahi mea rānei). Kei noho ngātahi tāua; manako mai koe, huru atu au (W 72).  matakawa

2. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Kāore e whakaae ki te tono, ki te hiahia rānei o tētahi atu, kāore rānei e whakaae kia uru mai tētahi mea kāore e pai ki a koe. Kua rite tonu taku whakapati i a ia kia haere mai ki konei mahi ai, engari ka huru tonu ia.  kape1 (2)
