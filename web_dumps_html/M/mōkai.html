He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mōkai

1. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He tangata kore mana ka mauheretia hei kaimahi mā tētahi atu. Ahakoa he aha te hiahia o tana rangatira, me mahi te mōkai ki te whakangata, kāore he tirotiro, kāore he aha.  pononga2 (1)

2. [Tūmatauenga] He tangata ka hopukina, ka puritia e ōna hoariri i te wā o te pakanga. Ka herea ngā ringa me ngā waewae o ngā mōkai kia kore ai e oma.  herehere (1)

3. [Tāne] He kararehe, he manu, he aha atu rānei ka puritia e te tangata hei hoa mōna. (i) Ko aua pōtiki, he tohorā rata, he mōkai nāna e rua tahi (HP 11). (ii) I a rāua e haere ana, ka whai tahi te kurī mōkai a Tamatekapua rāua ko te teina i ngā tokorua nei (Wh 3, 3).

4. [Tāne] ing. He kākā ka ākona ki te karehā kia pai ai te onga i ētahi atu kākā. Ko te pōkai kākā e onga ana ki te mōkai i runga i te tūtū (FL 201). {maimoa, moutī, pērua, tīmori, tionga, tīrore, whakahope, whakakōpē}

5. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua. Whakahōhā, whakariri i tētahi atu. Mōkai taku taokete ui makihoi.

6. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e āta whakaaro, kāore rānei e mātau ki te mahi tika. Mōkai te ngākau i whakapono ai ki ngā kī taurangi a tērā nanakia.  heahea2 (1)
