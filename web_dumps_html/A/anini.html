He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



anini

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka rongo i ngā mea katoa e hurihuri haere ana me te hiahia hinga anō.  ānini (1)

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te aho ka kārohirohi mai i te hau takiwā i te kaha o te wera.  ārohirohi (4)

ānini

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka rongo i ngā mea katoa e hurihuri haere ana me te hiahia hinga anō. E takahurihuri ana ngā tamariki kia ānini rawa rātou. {āmai1, āmiomio, ānewanewa, ānina, āninanina, anini, aniroro, ārohirohi, kehu, pōānini, pōātinitini, pōrewarewa, pōrewharewha, pōtaitaka, rori1 (2), rorohī, rorohuri, takaāmiomio, takaānini, takarangi1}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka rongo roa (te māhunga) i te mamae āhua kaha nei. Kua ānini noa tana māhunga, he roa rawa nōna e titiro ana ki tana rorohiko. {ānina, āninanina, ngāruru, pāhoahoa}

3. [Tāwhirimātea] ing. Tā te tinana, tā te hinengaro rānei i rongo ai i te pānga atu o tētahi āhuatanga, o tētahi mea rānei ki a ia. Kei te rongo au i te ānini kakati i taku waewae, me te mea nei kua tū i te taraongaonga.

4. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te aho ka kārohirohi mai i te hau takiwā i te kaha o te wera. Mā te ānini o te rorerore e tohu kua wera, kua rite mō te tunu i ngā kai.  ārohirohi (4)
