He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



Koro

[Ranginui] ing. Te pō tuarima o te marama. He rā pai tēnei, me tōna pō he pai. He pai mō te ono kai, me te hī ika. E ngaio ana te ika.  Okoro

koro1

‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka manako te ngākau ki tētahi mea, ki te mahi rānei i tētahi mahi. Kua tekau tau e koroa ana he wharekai hou mō tō tātou marae, kua ea tērā wawata i te rā nei.  hiahia (1)

koro2

1. [Tāne] ing. He aho ka herea kia āhua porohita mai te hanga, kia mahuhu anō te pona, ina kumea, kua kikī haere. Kei te hanga i ngā koro o te rore (W 143).  kaha2 (3)

2. [Papatūānuku] He wāhi whānui i te takutai e toro whakaroto ai te moana. Hoe atu ana te iwi ki te tirotiro i te kaipuke kua ū mai ki te koro.  whanga (1)

3. [Tāne] He aka piri rākau. Metrosideros perforata.  akatea (2)

koro3

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. He tāne taipakeke kua roa ngā rā ki te ao.  Whakamahia nuitia ai ina hāngai pū te kōrero ki te tangata. (i) Takoto mai rā, e koro ē! (NM2 144, 12) (ii) Ko te karangatanga mai a te koro rā — 'He kōhuru rā pea tā kōrua i au?' (WMA 3, 77)  koroua (1)

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. Te tāne nāna i puta ai tētahi tamaiti ki te ao, nāna rānei ia i whakatipu. (i) Ko Tamatea te koro o Kahungunu. (ii) Ngā turipona o tō koutou koro (W 143).  matua (1)

3. [Tāne] ing. He kupu whakamihi ki te tāne taipakeke; he kupu whakamihi ki te koroua. (i) E koro, Taupopoki, kia ora tonu koe, koutou ko ō tamariki, ko ō mokopuna, nā koutou nā i whakanui tātau i te aroaro o ngā iwi nui o te ao (KRK 31). (ii) Kua tau tēnā, e koro. Ko tātou katoa me haere (Wh 8, 1). (iii) He pai ki te rongo atu i tō reo, e koro.

koro4

[Tāne] tkp. Te kupu ka whakamahia e te kaikōrero hei tohu i te tokorua e kōrero atu ana ia, i te tangata rānei e kōrero atu ana ia me tētahi atu hanga kei a ia, e tata ana rānei ki a ia. Mā koro ētahi, mā mōu ētahi (W 143).  kōrua1
