He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kāinga

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Te wāhi nō reira mai te tangata, kei reira anō ētahi mea e arohaina ana e ia. Kei te hokihoki tonu ia ki te kāinga ki ngā tangihanga, ki ngā hui ā-iwi, ki ngā aha noa iho. {hau1 (hau kāinga), (kāinga tupu), toi1 (toi whenua), ūkaipō}

kāinga tupu [Papatūānuku] Te wāhi nō reira mai te tangata, kei reira anō ētahi mea e arohaina ana e ia. E hoki ana ki tōku kāinga tupu, ki te wāhi i whakatupuria ai, ki ōku hoa tākaro i ngā tau maha ka huri (KRK 127).  kāinga (1)

2. [Papatūānuku] Te wāhi e noho ai te tangata. (i) Kei te haere mātou ki te kāinga o taku tuakana mō te Kirihimete, i te mea he whare rahi tōna, he pai anō a waho mō ngā tamariki. (ii) E ai ki a rātou mā ko te kāinga noho o Whaitiri ko Raparapa-te-uira (H 30). {(kāinga noho), wāhi2 (wāhi noho)}

kāinga noho [Papatūānuku] Te wāhi e noho ai te tangata. Kei Pōneke tana kāinga noho, engari i ngā hararei katoa kua hoki ki te hau kāinga.  kāinga (2)

kāinga noho [Papatūānuku] Te tau o te whare, te ingoa o te huarahi, tae atu ki te ingoa o te tāone, o te takiwā rānei e noho ai te tangata. Tuhia mai koa tō kāinga noho, kia pai ai te tuku atu i ngā pitopito kōrero o te wā ki a koe. {wāhi2 (wāhi noho)}

3. [Papatūānuku] Te kāinga māori o te tipu, o te kararehe rānei i te taiao. Ko ngā maunga teitei te kāinga o te kea.  wāhi2 (wāhi noho)
