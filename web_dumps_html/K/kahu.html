He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kahu

1. [Tāne] ing. He ingoa whānui mō ngā momo kākahu tūturu o te Māori ka uhia ki ngā pakihiwi, he kākahu waho, he poto ētahi, he roa ētahi. (i) Ko te korowai, te tōpuni me te pākē, he momo kahu katoa. (ii) Arā ētahi kahu ko te ingoa tonu e whakaatu ana he mea hanga ki te aha, pērā i te kahu kiwi, i te kahu kekeno me te kahu kererū.

kahu kiwi [Tāne] He kahu ka uhia katoatia ki te huruhuru kiwi.

kahu kurī [Tāne] He ingoa whānui mō ngā kahu ka mahia ki te kiri kurī. He pūahi, he kahu waero ngā kahu kurī o tēnei kohinga.

kahu tāniko [Tāne] He kahu ka mahia ki te whītau tarapī, he tāniko tōna.

kahu waero [Tāne] He kahu ka mahia ki ngā huruhuru o te waero kurī.  waero (2)

2. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He ingoa whānui mō ngā uwhi tinana ka whakamaua e te tangata. He kirirua te kahu pai mō te ruku i ngā wai makariri.  kākahu (1)

kahu koka [Tāne] ing. He kahu kōkau ka hangaia ki ngā whā o te harakeke, o te kiekie rānei.

kahu mamae [Tāne] Te kākahu o tētahi tangata i patua kia mate, he mea tuku ki ana whanaunga kia kā tonu ai te riri, te mauāhara i roto i a rātou.

3. [Tāne] Te papanga whakarunga rawa, te taha whakawaho rānei (o tētahi mea). (i) Ka roa tonu te kawau e ruku ana, kātahi ka puta ake ki te kahu o te wai. (ii) He rā paihuarere, tīhore ana te kahu o te rangi.  mata1 (2)

4. [Papatūānuku] Te kiriuhi e kōpaki ana i te kukune. E mitimiti ana te hipi i te kahu e kōpaki ana i tana reme. {kahukahu, tewe, whakakahu}

5. [Papatūānuku] He wāhanga hou ka puta ake i te tipu, pērā i te ao, i te tā, i te rau rānei.  pihi1 (2)

6. [Tāne] Te kōhungahunga kua mate kē i te whānautanga mai. I tangihia te kahu, ka tukuna ki ōna mātua tīpuna.

7. ‑nga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka puta ake he tipu i te whenua, ka puta ake rānei he ao hou, he tā hou, he rau hou i te tipu.  pihi1 (1)

whare kahu [Tāne] ing. Te whare i āta whānau mai ai te tamaiti a te wahine rangatira. Ka whānau mai te tamaiti i te whare kahu, kātahi rāua ko tana whaea ka haere ki te whare kōhanga mō ētahi rā nei.

kāhu1

1. [Tāne] ing. He manu nui tonu noho mānia, he hāurauri a runga, he peapeau ngā pāpāringa me te kakī, he pākā, he parauri ngā tāekaeka o te poho heke iho ki te puku, ki ngā waewae, he karu kōwhai ōna. Circus approximans. {(kāhu komokomo), (kāhu maiepa), kērangi, manu (manu tāhae)} kāhu1 (1), kāhu1 (1)

kāhu komokomo [Tāne] He manu nui tonu noho mānia. Circus approximans.  kāhu1 (1)

kāhu kōrako [Tāne] He kāhu kua koroua, kua hina ngā huruhuru. Circus approximans.  kāhu1 (1)

kāhu maiepa [Tāne] He manu nui tonu noho mānia. Circus approximans.  kāhu1 (1)

kāhu pango [Tāne] He kāhu e ohi tonu ana, e hāurauri tonu ana te nuinga atu o ngā huruhuru. Circus approximans.

kāhu pōkere o te whenua [Tāne] He kāhu pīrere. Circus approximans.

2. [Tūmatauenga] He kupu whakarite mō te rangatira. He kāhu koe nō te iwi, he ngārahu taua anō hoki.  tētēkura (2)

kāhu kōrako [Tūmatauenga] He kupu whakarite mō te rangatira. Ko te motu nui tonu tērā i haere atu ki te tangihanga o te kāhu kōrako.  tētēkura (2)

3. [Tāne] He taonga tākaro rite ki te manu te hanga, he whai anga māmā ka uhia ki te raupō, ki te pepa, ki te aha atu, he aho roa tōna ka mau tētahi pito ki te ringa, ka tukua kia rere rā runga hau. Kei te ako tonu taku tama ki te turu i tana kāhu.  manu (manu tukutuku)

kāhu2

[Tangaroa] ing. He ika tai 160 henemita te tipu. Seriola lalandi.  haku2 (1)
