He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kara1

[Tūmatauenga] thu. He kupu whakamihi ki te tāne taipakeke, ki te tāne whai mana rānei. (i) E kara, e te Kāwana, kia rongo mai koe (KtKM 7, 18). (ii) E hoa mā, e tama mā, e kara mā, tēnā koutou (WM 9, 6).

kara2

1. [Tūmatauenga] ing. He taonga ka tukua, he kōrero ka whakapuakina, he waiata rānei ka waiatatia hei tohu ki ngā iwi e rata mai ana, e kimihia ana he āwhina i a rātou mō te haere ki tētahi pakanga. Ka whiua ngā kara ki Te Whānau-a-Apanui, rāua ko Te Whakatōhea. Ka tapaina te ingoa a taua patu ko Te Whiu, mō te whiunga o ngā kara (W 97).  tiwha (4)

2. [Tāne] He mahere huna, he tikanga huna. Kei te whakatakoto kara mō Te Horo kia patua (W 97).

kara3

1. [Ranginui] ing. reo kē. Te āhua o tētahi mea i te awatea, e whitu o ēnei e kitea ana i te āniwaniwa. He mā, he whero, he pango ngā kara o te haki o te Tino Rangatiratanga.  tae2 (1)

2. [Tāne] reo kē. He papanga kua tāia ki ōna anō tohu hei waitohu i te whenua, i te iwi, i te aha atu. Ko te nuinga ka hūtia ake ki te pou. Ka mauria mai e rātau te kara o Te Kīngi Māori ka titia ki te marae (H 39).  haki3

3. [Tāne] reo kē. Te wāhanga o te kākahu e huri ana i te kakī, ka taea te nuinga te whakapeke. He hutu Pākehā tōna. He hāte, he neketai pango, he mea poto, me tīpona tonu ki mua o te kara (HP 177).

kara4

[Tāne] ing. reo kē. He hanga ka whakamaua ki te kakī o te kararehe hei whakaita, hei whakamatua i a ia. (i) E mea ana au kia rongo ngā tāngata Māori o Heretaunga ki te tini o te hanga kei a au e tū ana. Ngā hānehi parau, me ngā kara hōiho (WMA 7, 7). (ii) Whakamaua atu te tohu rēhita a te kaunihera ki te kara o te kurī.

karā

[Rūaumoko] ing. He toka puia matarehu he uriuri te tae, he āhua rite ki te ōnewa. He kohurau, he karā, he ōnewa, he pounamu; koia tēnei ngā kōwhatu e mahia ana hei toki tārai waka, me ērā atu mahi whare (PH 4, 3).
