He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



a1

[Tāne] pmu. He kupu ka noho mai ki mua i te ingoa tangata, te ingoa wāhi me ngā tūkapi 'mea' me 'wai', ina tū ēnei hei tāhū mō tētahi rerenga, ina whakarārangitia rānei ēnei; ki mua i te ingoa tangata me te tūkapi (hāunga anō te 'ahau' me te 'awau') i muri i ngā pūtūmua 'i', 'ki', 'hei', 'kei'. (i) Kei te moe tonu a Hine. (ii) Kei a wai taku pukapuka? (iii) I reira a Hāmoa, a Rarotonga, a Whiti. (iv) Kei te kōrero au ki a Wira āpōpō. (v) Kei a ia tonu taku pōtae.

a2

[Tāne] pmu. E tohu ana i tētahi mea e whakahaeretia ana e tōna anō rangatira, hei tauira, ko te mahi, ko ngā mea e māmā ana ki te neke, te tamariki, te mokopuna, te tāne moe, te wahine moe, te pononga, te kōrero ā-waha, ngā mea i hangaia e te tangata, me te kai (engari kaua te wai māori e inumia ana me te rongoā). He pērā anō te tikanga o ngā kupu 'nā', 'tā', 'ā' me te 'mā', me ngā hononga o ēnei ki te '-ku', te '-u' me te '-na', hei tauira, 'nāku', 'tāu', 'māna', me te katoa o ngā pūriro o te rōpū-'a'. Ko ērā atu o ngā pūriro e kīia ana he rōpū-'o'. (i) Tokowhā ngā tamariki a Pare. (ii) Ngā Kurī-a-Whārei. (iii) Ka pai ngā kōrero a Tihi. (iv) Ehara nāu ēnā taputapu, nō reira āta tiakina.  o

a3

[Tāne] thu. He kupu ka whakahuatia kia aro mai te tangata e kōrero atu ana koe. (i) A! Kāti te turituri! (W 1) (ii) A, kua tae mai koe.  ā7

a4

[Tāne] thu. E whakataki ana i tētahi whakaaro e whakatakoto wero ana ki te kaiwhakarongo. (i) A tēnā, kei whea āu tūāhu? (W 1) (ii) Mehemea ko te kura te mahi pai, a tēnā me mahi tātou i taua mea (WM 9, 9). (iii) Ki te whakaae koe ki aku taki, a tēnā whakamāramatia mai tō māua whaea, tō māua pāpā, te tāne, me ngā tamariki a Whakaotirangi, kua hē hoki aku taki (WM 9, 5). (iv) A, hei konei whakarongo ake ai (W 1).

ā1

1. ‑ia ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga ‑kanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka akiaki i ētahi mea kia neke me te ū ki tētahi ahunga kua tohua. (i) He mana pio ngā ika i āia mai e te āmai i te takutai o te moana i Okarito (Wa 5, 7). (ii) Kua haere ki te ā mai i ngā kau ki te whare miraka. {uruhi, whiu1 (6)}

2. ‑ia ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga ‑kanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tikitiki haere i ētahi mea, ka waiho tahi ki tētahi wāhi; ka whakahuihui. Kua haere rātou ki te ā kōhatu papai mō te hāngī.  kohi1 (1)

ā2

[Tāne] ptm. Ka noho te kupu nei ki mua i ngā tūkapi mō te tokorua, te tokotoru neke atu, i tētahi ingoa, i tētahi tūingoa, i tētahi kīanga rānei, e tohu ana nāna aua mea. (i) I mauria e mātou ā mātou pounamu wai ārani. (ii) He hīanga ā Mere tamariki. (iii) Kaua e whakapono ki ā te rēwera kōrero. (iv) Me āta whakarongo ki ā te tangata e whakamātauria ana, āna whakautu. {wā2}

ā3

[Tāne] hono. He kupu hei hono i ētahi kōrero e rua, e tohu ana ko te mea tuarua ka whai mai i te mea tuatahi, ka haere tahi rānei ki te mea tuatahi. (i) Ka tae mātou ki te marae, ā, ka tū ka tatari. (ii) Ka kapakapa te manawa, ā, ka heke te werawera.

ā4

[Tāne] ptm. Mā tēnei punga ka honoa ētahi kupu e rua, ko te mea tuarua hei whakaahua i te mea tuatahi, te āhua o te mahinga mai, tana āhua tonu rānei. Mehemea he tūmahi hāngū te kupu tuatahi, tērā pea ka whakahāngūtia te kupu tuarua. Ka honoa te kupu tuarua me te punga nei ki te tohuhono, hei tauira, waiata ā-ringa; tikanga ā-iwi. (i) Ko tētahi wāhanga o te whakamātautau he whakamāori ā-waha, ko tētahi he whakamāori ā-tuhi. (ii) Kei mate ā-moa te kōkako.

ā5

[Tāne] ptm. E tohu ana kei te wā e heke mai ana tū ai te mahi kei muri e whakaingoatia ana. Ā te wiki e tū mai nei te hui. {hei1 (1)}

ā6

[Tāne] thu. He kupu whakautu e whakaatu ana i te whakaae. Mēnā e whakautu ana i te pātai whakakāhore, e whakaū ana i te whakaaro whakakāhore o roto. 'Kei te hoki mai rāua ā te ahiahi nei?' 'Ā. Hei te toru karaka ka tae mai.'  āe1 (1)

ā7

[Tāne] thu. He kupu ka whakahuatia kia aro mai te tangata e kōrero atu ana koe. (i) Ā e tino hari ana ahau mōu i tae mai. (ii) Ā e hoa, te āhua nei hore kē ēnā kōrero e tika. (iii) Ā! Waiho te naihi nā - ka motu koe. {a3}

ā8

[Tāne] ing. Te kōiwi kei te pakihiwi e tūhono ana i te pākau ki te whaturei.  I te nuinga o te wā ka honoa ki te kupu 'kakī'. Te ā o tōku kakī (W 1).  paemanu (1)
