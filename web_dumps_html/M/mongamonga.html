He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mongamonga

1. ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua tukitukia, kua wāwāhia kia moroiti ngā wāhanga, kia tino hē rānei te hanga. (i) Ā ka wāwāhia ana whakapakoko, mongamonga ana (PT Mik 1.7). (ii) Ka tukitukia koutou e ahau, mongamonga noa (PT Her 25.34). (iii) I hangā e te kiore tana kōhanga kia āhuru ai ia i te takurua . . . Ināianei, auē! Mongamonga noa taua kōhanga (TT 39, wh118). {hume, kōhungahunga, kōparu, kōrengarenga, mangungu, marū, nakunaku, ngatu, nguha, rengarenga}

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] Kua pōuri, kua rerekē rānei te tae o te kiri i te mea kua pākia e tētahi mea i pahū ai ngā iaia toto o raro iho (i te tinana), i kino ai rānei te kiko (o te huawhenua, huarākau). Kua mongamonga katoa tana tinana i te aituā waka, waimarie kāore i whati he kōiwi.  marū (1)

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Kua noho wehewehe (tētahi hanga mōwhakiwhaki) i te takanga, i te pātukitanga rānei ki tētahi mea. (i) Kātahi ka mongamonga ngātahi te rino, te uku, te parāhi, te hiriwa, te kōura (PT Ran 2.35). (ii) Kua kino tonu te mongamonga - e kore rawa e taea te whakakotahi anō ki te kāpia.  pakaru (1)

4. [Tāne] ing. He hanga ngohe, he hinuhinu anō, kei ngā arearenga o te wheua. Ngotehia te wheua nā kia puta mai ai te mongamonga. {roro, wai1 (wai mongamonga), wai1 (wai ponapona)}

5. [Tāne] Te taihemahema o te tāne, o te toa hei tuku tātea, hei mimi hoki. Kāore ētahi tāne e pai kia kitea ō rātou mongamonga i te wā e mimi ana.  ure
