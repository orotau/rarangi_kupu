He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hiki

1. hīkina ‑tia ‑na ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia neke whakarunga (tētahi mea). Tēnā hīkina ō waewae, e kare, kia pai ai taku tahitahi i te papa i raro i tō tūru. {ara2 (1), eke, hāpai, hī1 (2), huataki, kōranga, mairanga, riaki, riariaki, waha2, whakaara, whakaikeike, whakakaurera, whakatāiri}

2. ‑na ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka kawe haere (i te kōhungahunga) me te awhi anō. Ka hiki a Māmā i tana pēpi kia mutu ai te tangi. {morimori, rapoi, rapou, whakairo}

3. hīkina ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka hari ki wāhi kē noho mai ai. Nā wai aku pēke i hiki?  tango (2)

4. hīkina ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kawe haere (i tētahi mea) i ngā ringaringa. Ka hiki i tana pēpē huri i tana whare, kāore tonu e mutu te tangitangi a tēnā tamaiti.  hikihiki (1)

5. ‑tia ‑na ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] Ka whakatau ki te waiho (i tētahi take kua whakaritea) mō muri i te wā i whakaritea tuatahitia ai tū ai. (i) Ko ngā huihuinga tangata mō ngā take Māori, me hiki kia wātea rawa te mate nei (TT 43, wh188). (ii) Tiamana. Me hiki te Komiti i nāianei mō te 11 karaka i te ata o te Tūrei te 4/9/98 (PH 1, 19). {whakahoro}

6. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tupana te tinana i te tumeke, i te pānga o tētahi āhuatanga matawhawhati. Ka tika mai te manu ki te matapihi, ka hiki taku poho.  ohorere (1)

7. ‑nga ‑tanga [Whiro] Ka hē, ka mahue rānei he kōrero i te wā e karakia ana. He aitua tō tāua, i hiki taku karakia (W 49).

8. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] Ka piki, ka neke rānei ki runga. Kātahi ka rere katoa te kāhui ka hiki ake ki te takiwā (KRK 172).  ara2 (2)

9. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka rerekē te wāhi noho, ka haere atu i tētahi wāhi ki tētahi atu. Ko te whakatatūtanga, me hiki ngā kōiwi i tāpuketia ki reira ki wāhi kē (TTR 4, 19).  neke (1)

10. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka wehe mai, ka wehe atu rānei (i tētahi haere). Ao ake ka hiki mai ia i Heretaunga.  whakatika (3)

11. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tangaroa] ing, mahp, āhua. He karakia hei whakaara ake i tētahi mea i te wai. Kātahi ia ka hāpai ake i tana hiki ake mō tana ika kia maiangi ake (W 49).

12. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] He karakia kia heke tētahi iwi ki wāhi kē, hei whakakore rānei i te tapu i ngā ringaringa.

13. [Tāne] ing. Te hononga i waenganui i ngā wāhi e rua o tētahi whāriki. Tata kore ana ngā hiki e kitea, he tohunga nō te kairaranga ki tāna mahi.
