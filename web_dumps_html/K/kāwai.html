He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kāwai1

1. [Tāne] ing. Te taura e here ana i te tangata ki ōna tūpuna. He Māori tōna wahine, nō tētahi o ngā kāwai rangatira o Ngāti Tāwhaki (HP 259). {aho1 (4), hikahika, hiwi2 (3), kahou, kaka1 (5), kakano, kauhau, kauwhau, kāwei, takiaho, tātai1 (7)}

2. [Rongo] Ngā toronga o te hue, o tētahi atu tipu toro whenua rānei. Ka whāia haeretia a Rakawahakura, ka pipiri tonu, ka kuhu ki raro i ngā kāwai o te hue a Mahunui huna ai kia ora (NM4 348, Kwm). {kautawa, kāwei, kīwai, kīwei}

3. [Tangaroa] Ngā hanga e rite ana ki te ringa ka toro ake i te upoko o te wheke, o te ngū. Ko ētahi o ngā kāwai roa nei o taua taniwha e piri ana ki te toka, ko ētahi e awhi ana i te tinana o te wahine rā (WM 13, 23).  kawekawe (1)

4. [Tāne] Ngā puritanga ringa o te kete. Māu tētahi kāwai o te kete, ko tētahi ki a au.  kīwai (1)

5. [Tāwhirimātea] ing, āhua. He hau māriri, kāore e kaha te pupuhi. Ki te kaha ake i te kāwai, kua hoki tātou ki uta, kei kahakina noa tātou ki waho.  hengihengi (1)

kāwai2

[Tāne] ing. He kāhui manu.  Tērā pea e hāngai ana ki te pārera, te whio me te weiweia anake. Pakō ana te pū, whati ana te kāwai pārera ki te tī, ki te tā.
