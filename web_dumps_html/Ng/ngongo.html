He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngongo1

1. ‑hia ‑tia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka tō mai ki roto i te waha, mā te kukuti i ngā uaua o ngā ngutu me te arero, mā te whakahā noa iho rānei. (i) Hiakai katoa atu au i te rongo i a koe e ngongo nā i te ngako o ngā wheua poaka. (ii) Kia maroke, ka rewa ki runga te puehu o te whare, o te rūma rānei, ka ngongoa mai e te tangata ora e tū tata ana mai (TT 75, wh688). {momi, mote, ngote1}

2. ‑a ‑hia ‑tia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka pupuri i tētahi mea ki te waha, me te kukuti i ngā uaua o ngā ngutu, te arero me ngā pāpāringa, ka tō mai ai (i tētahi hanga) ki te waha. Ki te werohia e te waeroa te tūroro, na ka ngongo i te toto o te tūroro ki roto i a ia, ka noho te mate ki roto i taua waeroa (PH 2, 20).  ngote1 (1)

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka mamae te ngākau i te rironga o tētahi mea, i te pānga o tētahi āhuatanga kino, i te kore rānei o tētahi kaupapa e tutuki. Kāore i ora ake i te matenga o tana tamaiti, e ngongo tonu nei.  pōuri (3)

4. ‑tanga [Rongo] Kāore e kōrero, e hamumu te waha. He tamaiti ngongo - me uaua ka puta he kōrero i a ia.  wahangū (2)

5. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. Kāore e kakama ki te whakaputa whakaaro. He tangata ngongo hoki, koirā au i āhua tumeke ai i te nui me te hāngai o tana kōrero.  nguengue (2)

6. [Whiro] ing. He tangata kua pāngia e te mate. Ka roa te ngongo e whakatete ana kia noho tonu ia ki te kāinga, engari i te mutunga iho ka kawea ki te hōhipera.  tūroro1 (1)

7. [Whiro] ing, āhua. He tangata kua ngoikore, kua hauā rānei i tētahi mate, i tētahi aituā, tērā pea e riro ana mā tētahi atu e tiaki. I te whatinga o tana kakī, ka whakawhiwhia ki te penihana mō ngā ngongo.  tūroro1 (2)

8. [Tāne] ing. Te wai reka o roto i te pua. E kai ana ngā tūī i te ngongo kōrari.  waihonga

9. [Tāne] He hanga roa, he whāiti, he rite te hanga o te nuinga ki te rango engari he ipuipu a roto, ko tāna he kawe i te wai, i te hau, i te aha atu.  Whakamahia nuitia ai mō ngā hanga ka momi ake, ka tango i te wai, i te aha atu. (i) He ngongo i ngā ringaringa, he mea mahi ki te tūpākihi, he mea ore te uho o roto kia kore (W 234). (ii) Ko te ngongo e tango ana i ngā hūare kia kore ai te tangata e rāoa i te wā e mahia ana ōna niho.  pū2 (2)

10. [Tāne] Te wāhi koi i te ihu o te waka ka wāwāhi i te ngaru, ka haehae i te wai i te waka e haere ana.

ngongo2

[Tūmatauenga] ing. He tao ka whiua ki te ringa. (i) E kore e ngoto te pere, te ngongo, engari te tete (W 235). (ii) E kore te pourewa e taea ake e te ngongo, engari mā te pere (W 235).
