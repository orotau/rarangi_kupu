He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mahau

1. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He papa e toro whakawaho ana mai i te pakitara o te whare, he tuanui whakamarumaru tōna. Kei te mahau o te tipuna whare te tūpāpaku e takoto ana, kāre i roto. {haurangi, hōpua, kōihi2, kopa3 (1), opua, pikitara, rueke, tupehau, whakamahau}

2. [Tāne] He whare whai tuanui kāore i pērā rawa te teitei, he pakitara kore ngā taha katoa, ka whakatūria hei whakamarumaru, hei whakaruru. Ka whakatūria he mahau hei whakaruru mō te waka.

māhau

[Tāne] tkp. He kupu e tohu ana mā te kaiwhakarongo te mea, te mahi, te aha atu e kōrerotia ana. (i) Kia ngāwari tonu tō reo ina tono kai koe māhau, e moko. (ii) E hoa, e te ētita, māhau e whakamārama te takotoranga o ngā kupu (PH 4, 4).  māu
