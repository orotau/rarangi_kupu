He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kakī1

1. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhi o te tinana kei waenganui i ngā pakihiwi me te upoko. (i) Ka pakū te pū, ka mate taua poaka; werohia atu hoki te kakī kia puta ai ngā toto (Wh 1, 3). (ii) 'Tangohia mai te taura i taku kakī kia waiata au i taku waiata.' Koia nei tā Mokomoko i mea ai ki ana kaiwhakamate e takatū ana ki te tārona i a ia (TTR 1, 59). {porokakī, tākakī, ua1 (2)}

kakī mārō [Tūmatauenga] He tangata kāore e aro ake, e whakarongo ki ngā whakaaro, ki ngā tohutohu a tētahi atu, ka ū tonu ki tāna ahakoa pēhea. E kore rawa tērā kakī mārō e neke mai i tēnā whakaaro ōna. {maitai (taringa maitai), mārō (taringa mārō), mārō (upoko mārō), taringa (taringa kōhatu), taringa (taringa turi)}

kakī mārō [Tūmatauenga] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e neke, e aro ake, e whakarongo ki ngā whakaaro o tētahi atu, ka mau tonu ki tāna, ahakoa pēhea. E kore rawa e taea te huri ngā whakaaro o te hunga kakī mārō.  whakatete (1)

2. [Tāne] ing. Te pito whakarunga o te ara e heke ai te kai ki te puku, e rere ai te hau ki ngā pūkahukahu. Kāore ia e kaha ki te kai nā te mamae o tōna kakī.  korokoro1 (1)

kakī2

[Tāne] ing. He manu noho wai māori pāpaku, he pango a runga, he hāurauri a raro, he whero ngā karu, he ngutu pango roa ōna, he waewae whero tino roa. Himantopus novaezealandiae. {kanohi2, poaka1 (2), tōrea (tōrea pango), tūarahia}
