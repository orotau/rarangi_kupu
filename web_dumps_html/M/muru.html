He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



muru

1. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka waku ki te ringa, ki tētahi papanga ngohe rānei kia mā ai, kia maroke ai rānei. Ka noho te koroheke rā ki te muru i ana mētera, kia pīataata ai mō te rā o ngā hōia.  ūkui (2)

2. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka neke whakamua, whakamuri kia auau tonu (ki te mata o tētahi mea) me te paku pēhi atu anō. Ka muru a Hine i tana tinana mamae ki te hinu.  muku (2)

3. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tango, ka muku (i ētahi mea) kei te mata o tētahi mea. Ka murua atu ngā pūkohu e tipu ana i te raima.  kōmuku

4. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka hoatu i tētahi matū hinuhinu, hāpiapia rānei ki tētahi mata, ka tō haere, ka pana haere rānei kia whānui te hora. Murua he pata ki te uma o te heihei, ka rau atu ki te umu mō te hāora me te hāwhe.  pani2 (3)

5. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tāhae i ngā rawa, i ngā taonga a ētahi atu, me te tūkino anō i a rātou.  Whāia nuitia ai tēnei tikanga i te wā o te whawhai. Tuhituhia ki te pukapuka ngā kōrero katoa kia kite, kia rongo ai te Kāwana ki te take i murua ai te whare o Te Pōtete (KNT 1, 5). {hone, hūhunu1 (3), hunuhunu, kohi1 (3), kohikohi1, kōhunu, marure, mūrei, pāhua1, pāhuahua, pāhue, paru4, pārure, takahi, tūkuku, whakarekereke}

6. ‑a ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka mutu te riri, te whakamau (ki te tangata) mōna i hara ai, i mahi kino ai. (i) Murua ō mātou hara, me mātou hoki e muru nei i ō te hunga e hara ana ki a mātou (PT Mat 6.12). (ii) Ko te hara o te tangata nei, ko tana korenga e muru i te hara o tana hoa, i te mea i inoi ia ki te Atua kia murua atu tōna ake hara (Pi 6, wh2).
