He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



koka

1. [Tāne] ing. He kahu kōkau ka mahia ki ngā rau o te harakeke, o te tī, o te kiekie rānei, hei pare i te ua. Heoi anō ā rātou kākahu i tae ora mai ai rātou, he koka, he pākē (W 129).

2. ‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E rite ana ki te oneone, ki te wahie rānei te tae. E rua ngā kiore, he pūhina tētahi, he koka tētahi (W 129).  parauri (1)

kōkā1

[Papatūānuku] ing. Te wahine nāna i puta ai tētahi tamaiti ki te ao, nāna rānei ia i whakatipu. Ko Maria kē taku kōkā, kīhai ko te wahine i whakamamae ki te whakawhānau mai i a au.  whaea (1)

kōkā2

‑tanga [Rongo] āhua, ing, mahp. Kua rite mō te hauhake, mō te kai, kua pakari (o te hua whenua). Kua kōkātia ngā kūmara, kua maroke ngā rau (W 129).
