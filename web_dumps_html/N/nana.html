He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



nana

1. [Tāne] ing. He tāhei huruhuru poto e tipu ana i runga ake i te karu. Ka hī ngā nana, he mīharo nōna ki te nui o ngā moni i kohia.  tukemata

2. [Rūaumoko] He tānga moko kei waenga i ngā tukemata.

nanā

‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tohu whakaipoipo ki tētahi atu kia mōhio ai ia e hiahiatia ana ia, heoi, he mahi kirimoko, he hiahia kirimoko rānei. E kore ia e nanā kua mārena nei ia.  nangā

nāna

[Tāne] tkp. He kupu e tohu ana nā tētahi tangata i tua atu i te kaikōrero me te kaiwhakarongo te mea, te mahi, te aha atu e kōrerotia ana. (i) Nāna, nā Mere te kāheru nei. (ii) Nāna ēnei kupu i tuhi, kei te mōhio hoki au ki te āhua o tana tuhi kōrero. (iii) Nāna ngā tamariki nohopuku, nāku ngā mea hoihoi. (iv) Nāna i tū ai te kaupapa. {nāhana}

nānā

1. ‑ia ‑hia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka āta tiaki i runga i te aroha. Tino pai a Ani ki te nānā i ana tamariki, inā rā tō rātau koa me te pai o ō rātau āhua.  awhi (2)

2. ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka āta tiaki (i te tangata, i te kararehe) i a ia e māuiui ana, e hē ana rānei te wairua. He pai ia ki te nānā i te tūroro - he manawanui, he hūmārika hoki.  tapuhi (1)
