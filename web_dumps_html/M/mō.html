He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mō

1. [Tāne] ptm. E tohu ana i te tangata, i te aha atu rānei ka tau atu ki a ia ngā painga o tētahi mahi, o tētahi hanga rānei. (i) Kua hangaia he whare hou mō rātou. (ii) Anei he wai māori mōu. (iii) E mea ana ia ki te hoko koti mō tana punua kurī, kia kore ai ia e werohia e te māeke.

2. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te tangata, i te aha atu rānei ko ia te kaupapa, te aronga o tētahi kōrero, e pā ana ngā kōrero ki a ia. (i) Ko te kōrero mō Māui rāua ko Mahuika e whakaahua ana i te takenga mai o te ahi. (ii) Me tuhi pūrongo koe mō ngā āhuatanga i pā. (iii) Kua tāia he kupu mō te pānga o tētahi mate whawhati tata i Aotearoa hei tuku ki ngā kāinga katoa.

3. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te take i mahia ai tētahi mahi, i te take rānei i puta ai tētahi āhuatanga. (i) Ka kawe nei a Tinirau ki te rapu utu mō tana tamaiti (W 203). (ii) Ka whakamā noa mō tōna ringa i kapea e Paoa (W 203). (iii) E nui ana tōku koa mōu i tae mai ki te whakatuhera i tō mātau whare (PH 7, 8). (iv) I rīria a Wī e Timi Kara mōna i kohukohu i te Pāremata (KRK 100). (v) Me waiho te ingoa i a Paretūāhu, mō tana māra i tūāhua kautia (W 203).

4. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te wāhi e tika ana kia noho atu, kia takoto atu, kia kuhuna atu tētahi mea, tae atu ki ētahi atu āhuatanga e pēnei ana. (i) Anei te kete pai mō ō taewa. (ii) Ko te tēpu kē te wāhi tika mō ngā pukapuka. (iii) He aha mō runga i tō tōhi - he tīhi, he tiamu rānei? (iv) Te rerekē noa iho o te kākahu nei - ko hea te wāhi tika mō aku ringa?

5. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te mea e taea ana te hoko ki tētahi moni, i te utu rānei o tētahi mea. (i) E rua tāra te utu mō te miraka kotahi. (ii) Katoa ngā hanga i tēnei tēpu, e hokona ana mō te rima tāra.

6. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te roa e haere ana, e tū ana tētahi mea. (i) Waiho atu te kānga ki te kōawa mō te toru marama, neke atu, kātahi anō ka rite hei kai. (ii) Kei Te Āporo Nui ngā ākonga mō te rima rā. (iii) I whai wāhi atu ia ki te kēmu mō te hauwhā whakamutunga noa iho.

7. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te mahi ka tutuki pai ki tētahi mea, i tētahi mea, ka pahure rānei i tētahi tangata. (i) He pai tēnei harakeke mō te mahi taura. (ii) He pō pai tēnei mō te mahi tuna. (iii) Koinei ngā tino hū mō te haere i te ngahere. (iv) Kei a ia mō te mahi kai. (v) Mō te kōrero kanohi ki te kanohi, kāore ōku take. (vi) He mātotoru rawa tēnei paraikete mō te raumati.

8. [Tāne] E tohu ana i te wā e mahia ai tētahi mahi, e tū ai tētahi mea. (i) Kua pōuri rawa - waiho mō te ata. (ii) Mō āpōpō tāua haere ai ki te kato pikopiko. (iii) Mō te whitu karaka tū ai te karakia. (iv) Waiho mō raurangi.

mō te [Tāne] pma. E tohu ana kāore rawa (tētahi tangata) e mahi i tētahi mahi. (i) E kore au mō te haere ki reira - anuanu ana ki a au ngā wāhi pērā. (ii) Kāore au mō te huna i aku whakaaro. (iii) Engari, ahakoa te mīharo o Hikairo ki te whakaaro rangatira o Ngā Puhi, kāore ia mō te whakarere i tana iwi (TTR 1, 171).
