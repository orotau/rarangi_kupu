He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ongaonga1

1. [Tāne] ing. He rākau e 3 mita te tupu, he taratara kakati kei ngā wāhi katoa o ngā rau me ngā rārā, he poi iti runga kakau roa ngā pua, he parauri ngā hua poi te hanga. Urtica ferox. {pōngitangita, pūnitanita, pūngitangita, taraonga, taraongaonga}

2. [Tāne] He rākau 12 mita te tupu. Hoheria populnea.  houhi (1)

3. [Tāne] He pepeke moroiti e 2 mirimita te roa. Haria he rongoā kia kore ai tātou e kainga e te ongaonga. Austrosimulium spp.  namu

4. [Tangaroa] He tauira whakairo, ko ngā pākati he rite ki te niho koi.

5. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e tau te wairua nā te wehi, nā te māharahara rānei, ā, ka kitea tēnei i roto i te kawe a te tangata i a ia. Kei te ongaonga a ngē au ki te tū ki te kōrero (PTT).  āmaimai

ongaonga2

‑tia ‑hia ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka poapoa (i te manu) kia tata mai, kia pai ai te patu. Kia ongaongatia mai taua manu (W 240).  onga
