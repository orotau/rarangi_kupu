He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



manaaki

1. ‑tia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi i ngā mahi o ia rā, i ngā mahi auau rānei e mau tonu ai, e pai ake ai rānei te noho ora o tētahi mea. (i) Ko te whenua he taonga atua tēnā. Nā ngā atua o te ao Māori i hanga mō te tangata. Ko tā te tangata mahi he manaaki kau i te whenua (H 23). (ii) Mā te Wairua Tapu koutou me tō koutou iwi e manaaki, e arataki ā ngā rā e heke iho nei (TT 50, wh299). (iii) Kei te kaha te manaaki a ngā tuākana i ngā tēina i tō mātou kura.  tiaki (1)

2. ‑tia ‑hia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] Ka āwhina, ka tiaki i runga i te ngākau pai, i te aroha rānei. (i) E kore taua tangata e manaakitia e te iwi - he whānako, he mūrere hoki. (ii) He tangata manaaki Pākehā ia, i te pito taenga mai rā anō o te Pākehā ki uta nei tae noa ki tōna matenga (WM 8, 1). {atawhai}

3. ‑tia‑hia ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] Ka āta tiaki (i te tangata ka noho hei manuhiri māu), ka whakarato i a ia ki ngā mea e ora ai ia, e pai ai tana noho. (i) Ka mutu, ka kōrerorero mātau, kātahi ka karangatia ki te kai. Ka pai hoki tā rātau manaaki i a māua (HP 229). (ii) He kaha rātou ki te manaaki i te hunga tāpoi ka tau atu ki tō rātou tāone. {taurima, tuku1 (4), whakamanuhiri, whakauhi, whakauwhi}
