He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kāhui

1. [Tāne] ing. He huinga tāngata ka whakakotahi i runga i tētahi kaupapa. Me mihi nui atu rā ki a koutou katoa o runga o tēnei kāhui tirotiro i ngā āhuatanga o ngā taonga a ō tātou tīpuna (KRK 6).  rōpū (1)

kāhui whetū [Ranginui] He huinga whetū e noho tauira mai ana i te rangi, kua tapaina te tauira ki tētahi ingoa. He kāhui whetū a Matariki me Māhutonga.  tātai1 (tātai whetū)

2. [Tāne] He huinga manu e noho tahi ana, e rere tahi ana, he huinga kararehe rānei e noho tahi ana. (i) Ko te manu taki tērā e tū ana hei tūtei mō te kāhui. (ii) Nāwai ā, ka ngaro atu te kāhui hipi ki tua o te hiwi. {pā4, rāhui2, rāngai1 (2)}

3. [Tāne] He huinga pepeke mano tini e rere tahi ana. Ka raru tonu te tangata ka werowerohia e te kāhui pī. {pōī, rāpoi, rere}

4. [Tāne] He huinga mea e piri tata ana. Ko Ngā Kuki Airani, he kāhui moutere kei Te Moana-nui-a-Kiwa.  rāpoi (2)
