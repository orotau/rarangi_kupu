He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



oma

1. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tere te takahi a ngā waewae me te whakarere tahi i te whenua mō tētahi wā poto i ia takahanga. (i) Ka kite i tana pahi e haere iho ana, kātahi ka oma ki te whai. (ii) Kei mua ngā kurī e oma haere ana, kei muri ngā tāngata e whētōtō ana. {karehe, matiko, rere, taioma, tuoma, tūturi}

2. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka wehe atu i tētahi wāhi i runga i te whāwhai, he karo āhuatanga kino te kaupapa. Ka puta mai te koroua me te hāparangi o te waha, ka taki oma ngā tamariki haututū. {hora2, horo2, kairere1, papahoro, pūihi, pūrere1, rere, tahuti, tauwhati, tawhiti3, tūrere, whakaarorangi, whati}

3. ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] Ka motu iho i tētahi momo here, ka karo rānei i tētahi āhuatanga mōrearea. Mōhio rawa ake e tuwhera tonu ana te kēti, kua oma kē ngā hipi. {hōnea, hora2, kounu, mawhiti1 (4), mokohiti, mokowhiti, ora1 (3), paheno, pahure, pūrere1, puta1 (7), rere, tipa2 (2), tupa, whakahore}
