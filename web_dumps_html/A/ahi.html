He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ahi1

[Rūaumoko] ing, āhua. Te mura, te wera me te auahi anō pea ka puta ake ina kā tētahi mea. Tēnā tahuna te ahi kia mahana ai tō tātou whare. {hatete, kanaku, kāpura, kora, māpura, maute, ngiha, pāhunu}

ahi2

[Tāne] ing. Ina tū koia te kupu tuatahi o ētahi kupu pūhui, e tohu ana i tētahi momo karakia.

ahi horokaka [Tūmatauenga] He karakia ka whakahaeretia i te wehenga atu me te hokinga mai o te taua. Ko te ahi horokaka, he ahi tapu, hai whāngai i te atua, hai whakatapu i te taua (W 61). {(ahi tapu), horokaka2}

ahi huka [Tāne] He ahi hei tunu i te manu tuatahi ka patua e tētahi hunga patu manu, hei kai mā tō rātou tohunga ina mutu ana karakia whakarata i a Tāne, ina hikina te tapu i te taupuni me te ngahere kei reira rātou. I te wā e haere ai ngā karakia, ka whiua atu he rau huka ki te ahi.  (ahi mātini)

ahi kā huka [Tāne] He ahi hei tunu i te manu tuatahi ka patua e tētahi hunga patu manu, hei kai mā tō rātou tohunga ina mutu ana karakia whakarata i a Tāne, ina hikina te tapu i te taupuni me te ngahere kei reira rātou. I te wā e haere ai ngā karakia, ka whiua atu he rau huka ki te ahi.  (ahi mātini)

ahi kerakera [Tāne] He ahi e whakahaeretia ai ngā mahi hei whakakore atu i te tapu.

ahi mātini [Tāne] He ahi hei tunu i te manu tuatahi ka patua e tētahi hunga patu manu, hei kai mā tō rātou tohunga ina mutu ana karakia whakarata i a Tāne, ina hikina te tapu i te taupuni me te ngahere kei reira rātou. I te wā e haere ai ngā karakia, ka whiua atu he rau huka ki te ahi. {(ahi huka), (ahi kā huka), (ahi rau huka)}

ahi pāhikahika [Tāne] He ahi i tapu ai nā te mea i āta hikaia (arā, kāore i tīkina atu tōna mura i tētahi atu ahi) e rite ai ngā karakia, ngā tikanga mō te tūpāpaku. Ko te ahi parapara he ahi pāhikahika mō te tūpāpaku (W 247).

ahi parapara [Tāne] He karakia e hikina ai te tapu e pā ana ki te hunga ora me te tūpāpaku, ki ngā mahi tā moko me ngā tāngata i whai wāhi atu, ki te ika tuatahi ka mau i te tamatāne. Ka tīmata te tangata ki te mahi tuna, kātahi anō ia ka mahi i tērā mahi, ka kā te ahi parapara, mā ngā tāne anake e kai (W 262).

ahi rau huka [Tāne] He ahi hei tunu i te manu tuatahi ka patua e tētahi hunga patu manu, hei kai mā tō rātou tohunga ina mutu ana karakia whakarata i a Tāne, ina hikina te tapu i te taupuni me te ngahere kei reira rātou. I te wā e haere ai ngā karakia, ka whiua atu he rau huka ki te ahi.  (ahi mātini)

ahi rokia [Tāne] He karakia hei whakarata i a Tāne ka whakahaeretia e tēnā, e tēnā o tētahi hunga patu manu hei hiki i te tapu i te ngahere me ngā manu.  (ahi tute)

ahi tapu [Tūmatauenga] He karakia ka whakahaeretia i te wehenga atu me te hokinga mai o te taua.  (ahi horokaka)

ahi tute [Tāne] He karakia hei whakarata i a Tāne ka whakahaeretia e tēnā, e tēnā o tētahi hunga patu manu hei hiki i te tapu i te ngahere me ngā manu. {(ahi rokia)} ahi2 (1)
