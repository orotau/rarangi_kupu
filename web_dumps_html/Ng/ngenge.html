He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ngenge1

‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua pau te hau, kua pau te kaha, mā te whakangā rawa, mā te moe rawa e whai kaha anō ai. (i) Nō te toru karaka i te ata ka mutu tēnā kanikani. Kua ngenge katoa ngā waewae, kua hiamoe ngā kanohi (Wh 1, 2). (ii) Ko te pakanga ki ēnā hoariri, he pakanga mutunga kore. Ka ngenge koe, ka toa ērā (TT 76, wh704). (iii) Ka whakatā te wahine rā i tana ngenge (IwiT 8:4). {hūhē, kū1 (2), kuru3, mārohirohi1 (2), ngehe, ngehengehe, ngōio1, parohea, rohea, rore1 (6), rowhea, ruha, rūhā, ruharuha, ruwha, taiaroa, tawari, tīhohe, tūngarangara, tūrohi, waea2, whakakuru, whakakurutahi, whakangehengehe, wherū}

ngenge2

[Papatūānuku] ing. Te hanga hinuhinu kei te tinana tangata, tinana kararehe, he mā, he kahotea rānei te tae, kitea nuitia ai i raro tonu i te kiri, e kōpaki ana rānei i ngā whēkau. I whāngaia koe ki te ngenge o te tāmure o Whangapānui (W 233).  mōmona (4)
