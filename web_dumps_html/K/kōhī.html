He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kohi1

1. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka tikitiki haere i ētahi mea, ka waiho tahi ki tētahi wāhi; ka whakahuihui. E whā hāora rātou e kohi ana i ā rātou wahie. {ā1, āmene, āmi, āmine, amio, apo, apoapo, karahui, kauopeope, kohikohi1, rāpoi, rauhī, tāhora, taiope, tīkohi, tīoko, whakaemi, whakahiato, whakakakao, whakakao, whakakapiti, whākao, whakapeti, whakarāmemene, whakarauika, whakaropeti, whatiwhati, whiu1 (5)}

2. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakatakoto atu, ka whakanoho atu, ka whakaemi atu (i ētahi mea) ki roto i tētahi hanga. (i) Ko ia hoki hei kīngi ā ngā rā e heke mai nei, ina kohia atu tōna pāpā ki te urupā o ōna tūpuna, o tōna whaea hoki kua ngaro tata ake nei (Pi 41, wh14). (ii) Ina haere koe ki te whawhaki tāwhara, me tari he pīkini me tōna taupoki kia pai ai te kohi atu ki roto (Wh 43, 3).  rau3 (3)

3. ‑a ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tāhae i ngā rawa, i ngā taonga a ētahi atu, me te tūkino anō i a rātou. Ko te whenua e kore e riro, e tangohia, e tāhaetia, e kohia rānei (W 125).  muru (5)

kohi2

1. [Whiro] ing. He mate rere kino e tipu ai he kōpuku ki ngā pūkahukahu, i ōna wā anō ka toro ki ngā kōiwi, ki ngā tākihi me ētahi atu wāhi o te tinana. Tērā ka mate rawa te tangata i te mate nei, ki te kore e rongoātia. (i) He tino mate te kohi kei te Māori, kei te whakamamae kei te whakapōuri i a ia, kei te iki hoki i ōna whanaunga ki te pō (Pi 38, wh6). (ii) Kei roto te 'bacillus' o te kohi i te miraka a te kau pāngia e te mate kohi. Ko tētahi tēnei o ngā take tino kaha e pāngia ai te tangata e te kohi (TT 93, wh994). {mate (mate kohi)}

2. ‑a nga [Whiro] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia heke te tupu o te tinana, kia kore katoa atu te mōmona, kia iti haere anō te kiko. Kua kohi rawa te mate weriweri rā i tana tinana - he kiri, he poroiwi kau.

kohi3

[Tāne] ing. He rākau e 8 mita te tupu. Schefflera digitata.  patatē (2)

kohī

‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. He mate kōpiro e kaha ai, e tino waiwai ai te tiko. Kāore i tika tō tāmahana i te mīti nā - koinā pea i kohī ai koe.  tikotiko

kōhī1

[Papatūānuku] ing. He paru manauri, he takotoranga whakapango mō ētahi mahi o te whare pora. Ka harakukua ētahi wāhi o te harakeke, ka tukua ki te kōhī kia pango ai ngā wāhi i harakukua rā.

kōhī2

1. [Tāne] ing. Te huinga kōiwi e tautoko ana, e tiaki ana i ngā whēkau, i ngā uaua, i ngā wāhi kikokiko o te tinana. Tēnā ka riro i te riri nui, nāu e te atua, hei kōhurehure, tū ana ngā kōhī anake ka horo ngā kiko (W 125).  anga2 (3)

2. [Tūmatauenga] He kupu mō te tāne taiohi. Ka kī te puku o te kōhī rā, ka tū, ka haere.  tāhae (3)
