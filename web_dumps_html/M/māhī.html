He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mahi

1. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, mahw, āhua. Ka whakapau kaha ki tētahi āhuatanga kia puta ai he hua, ka kawe i tētahi āhuatanga ki tōna otinga. (i) Nō te wehenga atu i te kura ka mahi taku koroua i runga i te pāmu. (ii) Ka mahi ia kia kā te ahi ahakoa te mākū o te wahie. (iii) Ko ana hāora mahi, mai i te iwa ki te toru karaka i ia rā. (iv) Poto noa ngā karakia i mahia rā ki a Hotupuku (W 163). (v) Kua tūreiti koe - kua mahia kētia tā rāua waiata ā-ringa.

2. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whakakotahi i ētahi mea, ka whakarerekē rānei i ētahi rawa, i tētahi rawa rānei, kia oti mai ai tētahi mea kē atu. (i) Tēnā mahia mai he keke mā tātou, e kare. (ii) He mea mahi mai tana mahi toi ki te rākau, te hurumanu me te anga mātaitai. (iii) Ka mahia mai tana oko ki te uku. {hanga, hangahanga, raweke, waihanga, whaihanga, whakanao}

ka mahi . . . [Tūmatauenga] kīa. He kupu whakataki i te whakamihinga, i te whakahēnga rānei (o te mea ka whakahuatia i muri mai). (i) Ka mahi te tawa uho ki te riri (NP 1021). (ii) Ka mahi te take pakiranga (NP 1018). (iii) Ka mahi te āwhato hohoni paenga (NP 1008).

3. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te hua, ngā hua rānei ka puta i te whakapaunga kaha, i te hekenga o ngā werawera. Ka kite rā koutou i te tini o aku mahi (W 163). {hanga}

4. [Tāne] Te tino maha, te huhua (o tētahi mea).  Ka pēnei te whakatakoto: 'te mahi a te . . .' (i) I tua atu i ngā oneone parakiwai i haria mai e tēnei waipuke, i mauria mai te mahi a te wahie (HP 14). (ii) I taua wāhi e whakaranu mai rā te wai tai ki te wai māori, ka kitea atu te mahi a te aua e ngote ana i te wai tai (HP 16). {hua1 (7)}

māhī

‑anga ‑nga [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua kawa i te roa e noho noa ana; kua wāwāhia ngā matū (o tētahi kai) e te huakita, e te īhi, e tētahi atu momo moroiti rānei, e puta ai he waipiro, he waikawa rānei. Kua māhī te kānga wai, kua pai hei kai.  moī (1)
