He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

Huna

1. [Ranginui] ing. Te pō tuangahuru o te marama. He kino katoa tēnei rā mō ngā mahi katoa. {Arikimatanui, Arimatanui, Ariroa, Hune2, Maure}

2. [Ranginui] Te pō tuawaru o te marama. He rā pai tēnei, he āio, ka kaikai te ika. He rā pai mō te mahi kai moana. Kia tūpato te hunga hī moana.  Tamatea (Tamatea-āio)

3. [Ranginui] Te pō tekau mā tahi o te marama. He kino ki te hōtoke i ētahi wā, ki te raumati he pai. He pō āhua pai tonu mō te ono kai, me te hī ika.  Ari

huna

1. ‑ia ‑nga [Rūaumoko] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka whai kia kore tētahi mea e kitea. Kua hunaia te kī o te whare ki raro i te kōhatu i te taha mauī o te kūaha. {kuhu, tārehu, whakamoe, whakangaro, whakapeke}

2. ‑ia ‑tia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka patu, ka takakino (i tētahi mea) kia mate katoa atu, kia kore katoa atu. He taniwha te mea e huna nei i te tangata o tēnei huarahi (W 69).  whakangaro (2)

3. ‑nga [Rūaumoko] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whai kia kore e kitea. (i) Ka peke ki roto i te wai, ka huna ki raro i te kei o te waka (IwiT 9:14). (ii) I mua tata tonu i te haerenga, ka piki atu au ki runga i tētahi o ngā taraka, huna ai mei kore au e tae ki Itari. I te hiahia katoa au kia kite i Itari (HP 218). {piri, whakamoke, whakapeke, whakapupuni, whakatarapeke}

4. ‑nga [Rūaumoko] Kāore e kitea atu, nā te mea e noho ana i muri, i raro, i roto, i waenganui rānei i tētahi atu hanga. Te tini tāngata e huna nei ki roto i te arearenga o ngā poho o Rangi rāua ko Papa (W 69). {hunahuna, koropuku}

5. ‑tia [Rūaumoko] tūkē, āhua. Me te kore e whakaatu atu ki tētahi atu. (i) Ka rere huna atu te iwi rā kei kitea, kei patua kia mate. (ii) Kāore i roa rawa te haerenga atu o ngā kātipa e ono tekau ki te hopu i a Rua Tai Kēnana . . . ko ngā kōrero a ētahi, mōna i harihari waipiro ki Maungapōhatu, ka hokohoko huna (HP 242). {hū3 (4), momote, ngaro1 (10), puku2}
