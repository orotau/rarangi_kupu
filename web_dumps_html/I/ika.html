He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ika1

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He iti nā Tangaroa haere tai, haere wai māori, he pihapiha, he pakikau, he unahi ō te nuinga. Kua whakangaro ngā kai moana me ngā ika (TTR 1, 226). He ika anō te kekeno me te poraka ki te Māori. {ngehi, ngei, ngohi}

ika a Mohi [Tangaroa] He ika tai e 30-40 henemita te tipu. Zeus faber.  kuparu (1)

ika a Tangaroa [Tangaroa] Te ika tuatahi ka mau ki te kupenga hou.

ika moana [Tangaroa] He ingoa whānui mō ngā ika tai tino nui, he niho ō ētahi, he pāhau ō ētahi, he tāpihapiha kei te upoko, kei te ua rānei, he whakapae te hiku, he pakihau ōna, he ika whāngote i ana kūao. {mīmiha, momori2, ngohi (ngohi moana), rongo (rongo moana), tohorā, wēra}

ika waka [Tangaroa] Te ika tuatahi ka mau i runga i te waka hou, he ika tapu.

ika whakataki [Tangaroa] Te ika tuatahi ka mau, ka tuia te ihu ki te harakeke, ka tukua anō ki te wai ki te whakataki mai i ētahi atu ika i Hawaiki.

2. [Tangaroa] Te kiko o te ika e kainga ana. Kia haere ake nei au ki te hoko ika hei mahi ika mata mā tātou. {ngohi}

ika tuata [Tūmatauenga] He ika ka taona, ka kainga e ngā kaumoana i te haerenga tuatahi o te waka ki te hī ika (he wāhanga nō te pure). He ika tuata tētahi; i tunua mā ngā kaumoana (W 76).

ika2

[Tūmatauenga] ing. He toa; he toa ka hinga i te pakanga. Ka mutu te patu kei te hurihuri i ngā ika tapu (W 76).

ika huirua [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata tuarua ka hinga i te riri. Ū tonu ki te upoko mate tonu atu tērā, ka hora te ika huirua (TTR 1, 258). {(ika i te whakawaha), matatohunga, tāpiri, tatao1 (1)}

ika i te ati [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata tuatahi ka hinga, ka mauheretia rānei i te pakanga. Ko Tohatoharangi te ika i te ati (NM4 370, 9).  mātāika

ika i te whakawaha [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata tuarua ka hinga i te riri. Nāna te ika, nā tana hoa te ika i te whakawaha.  (ika huirua)

ika whakaotinga [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata whakamutunga ka patua kia mate i te pakanga. He puaki parepare, he ika whakaotinga nā Kawatapuarangi, nā i! (NM4 392, 33) {huka3, (mutunga ika), pīwai2, tāhuna1 (tāhuna pakipaki), whakatiki1 (tangata whakatiki)}

mutunga ika [Tūmatauenga] Te tangata whakamutunga ka patua kia mate i te pakanga. Ko koe kia kamea hei mutunga ika ki roto o Waikare (W 215).  (ika whakaotinga)

ika3

[Tūmatauenga] ing. He tangata ka mate i tētahi āhuatanga, ka patua rānei kia mate. Ka mau ki tana ika tuatahi ko te tamāhine a Maru-te-whareaitu (W 76). {ngohi}

ika huirua [Tūmatauenga] Ngā tūpāpaku e rua ka tangihia i te wā kotahi ki te marae kotahi. Kawea ana hoki tōna tūpāpaku ki Ngāruawāhia ika huirua ai rāua ko Erueti (TTR 4, 23).

ika paremo [Tūmatauenga] He tangata ka patua hei whakarata i a Tangaroa. Nā ko taku taokete, me patu ia hei ika paremo mō tō tātou waka (W 76).

ika takoto a Tiki [Tūmatauenga] Te tinana o te tangata kua mate. Kei te marae te ika takoto a Tiki e tīraha mai rā.  tūpāpaku (1)

ika4

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Ētahi mea kua whakawhāitihia mai ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Ka tahuna te ika tūpāpaku kia kore ai e rere te mate ki te hunga ora.  haupū (3)

ika tuawhenua [Papatūānuku] He rārangi puke matua.  (ika whenua)

ika whenua [Papatūānuku] He rārangi puke matua. Kia mārō te tū, kaua e tū whakahinga, kaua e tū koromeke, kia pērā tō tū me te tū a Māui-tikitiki-o-Taranga i hīia ake nei e ia tēnei ika whenua ki runga, kāore anō i tītaha, kāore anō i totohu (PH 1, 11). {(ika tuawhenua), matuaiwi}

2. [Tūmatauenga] Tētahi wāhanga o te taua. Pae ana te ika tangata (W 76).  ope (4)

ika5

[Tāne] ing. He huinga mea e piri tata ana. Māna e whakarewa te ika whetū riki (KtKM 2, 8).  rāpoi (2)
