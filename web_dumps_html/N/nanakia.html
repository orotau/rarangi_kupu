He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



nanakia

1. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He tangata hīanga, he tangata whakatoi. I mau ngā nanakia e tuhi pāhau ana ki ngā whakaahua o ngā tāngata tōrangapū. {autaia, nauhea, nauwhea, tainanakia}

2. [Whiro] He tangata mahi kino. Ka tika kia tino whiua rawatia ērā nanakia kōhuru, hei utu mō ā rātou mahi kōhuru, mahi kino whakaharahara ki ngā wāhine me ngā tamariki hara kore i mau i a rātou i te tīmatanga o tō rātou whakatikanga (TKP 1, 43).

3. ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore i māharahara ki te takahi i ngā hoa, kāore i pono te piri ki te tangata, e tinihanga ana. Ka taka te whakaaro i roto i tētahi tangata nanakia kia mahia e ia tētahi mahi e kino ai taua wahine ki roto i ngā whakaaro o tōna tāne taumau. Kātahi ia ka whai ki te wahine rā. Whai noa, whai noa, kāore i tahuri mai ki a ia (Pi 33, wh3). {whakamoho}

4. ‑tanga [Tāne] Tino pai tonu, he pai ake rānei i tērā i whakaarohia. Kei mua o te Whare Mīhana, kei te taha katau, kei raro atu, ko te whare karakia. He nanakia tonu te rahi. Kotahi rau pea ngā tāngata ka uru ki roto (HP 259).  autaia (5)
