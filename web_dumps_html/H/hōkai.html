He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hōkai

1. ‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka āhua tere tonu te haere, arā, ka tere ake i te hīkoi, engari kāore i oma rawa. Me hōkai tonu ina whakawhiti koe i te huarahi.  toitoi2 (1)

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Ka tawhiti tētahi i tētahi. Me hōkai tonu te whakatō i ngā kāpeti, kia kore ai e apiapi rawa ina tupu ake.

3. [Tāne] ing. Te tawhiti mai i tētahi taha ki tētahi. E 50 mita te roa o te hōpua kaukau, e 25 mita te hōkai.  whānui (4)

4. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He rākau, he maitai, he aha atu ka rere hauroki, ka rere hāngai rānei ki tētahi hanga, i waenga rānei i tētahi anga, ko tāna he tautoko, he whakapakari i te tū a taua hanga. (i) He papa rere tītaha ngā hōkai i ngā pakitara o te whare. (ii) Ko ngā wāhine me ngā tamariki kua hui ki te tā o te kaipuke, e pipiri ana ki ngā taura hōkai o te rewa (WM 10, 13). {hōngai, hōngoi, kaumahaki, tauteka}

5. [Tāne] ing. Ngā raukura nunui katoa i te paihau manu (ō te kōtuku me ētahi atu manu). {hikutau}

6. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] ing, mahp. He korowhititanga ka puta noa ake i ngā io, i ngā uaua o hea ake o te tinana, he tohu.  hukihuki (3)
