He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kanga

1. kangā ‑hia ‑hanga [Whiro] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka karanga i te kino kia tau ki runga i te tangata, ki tētahi mea rānei. (i) Kanga kau ana te tangata ki a koe, e kore rawa e piri tōna kanga ki a koe; hoki ana anō tōna kanga ki a ia, hei whakamate anō i a ia (MT 9). (ii) Na ka rongo a Ngātoroirangi, kātahi ia ka utu i te kanga a Manaia, ka mea ia, 'Kia pēnā ia, taona ana ki ngā kōwhatu o Maketū' (N 77). (iii) Kia kangā te tangata e moe ana ki te wahine a tōna papa (PT Tiu 27.20). {kohukohu2 (6), oraora}

2. ‑hia ‑hanga [Whiro] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka whiu i te kupu kino, i te kupu whakahāwea, hei whakaatu i tō riri ki tētahi mea. (i) Kia tūpato ki te pukuriri rā, ki te hē ō mahi, ka huri ki te kanga i a koe. (ii) E pōuri ana a ia mō ana kupu kanga ki a Te Kuīni (Wa 4, 26). {kohukohu2 (5), whawharo}

kānga

[Rongo] ing. reo kē. He huawhenua ka hua mai i tētahi tipu teitei tonu, kotahi, e rua rānei ngā puku ka tupu mai i te tipu kotahi, he hanga rite ki te pātītī hiraka e kōpaki ana i ngā kai o roto, he rau kei waho atu i tērā, ka noho rārangi mai ngā pata kōwhai ki te puku; he māra, he wāhanga rānei o tētahi māra kua whakatipuria ki tēnei huawhenua. (i) Ahakoa te mōhio kāore e pai mō tana tinana, ka pania tonuhia tana kānga ki te pata, ka ruiruia ki te tote, ka kai. (ii) Ka maroke tana kānga, ka haere ki te whawhaki hai tuku ki te wai.

kānga kōpiro [Rongo] He kānga pakari kua waiho ki te wai kia pirau haere, he kai. Kia tino roa te kōhua i te kānga kopiro, kātahi anō ka maoa, ka tika hei kai.  (kānga wai)

kānga pirau [Rongo] He kānga pakari kua waiho ki te wai kia pirau haere, he kai.  (kānga wai)

kānga piro [Rongo] He kānga pakari kua waiho ki te wai kia pirau haere, he kai.  (kānga wai)

kānga wai [Rongo] He kānga pakari kua waiho ki te wai kia pirau haere, he kai. (i) 'He roa tonu tāua e tiaki ana kia ngāwari ngā rīwai. Māku anō koe e whakamōhio atu.' 'E hia te roa, e Kui?' 'He rite anō ki te kānga wai tōna roa i roto i te wai, he rahi tonu ngā marama' (Wh 42, 4). (ii) Inā kē te reka o te kānga wai ka kīnakitia ki te kirīmi me te huka. {(kānga kōpiro), (kānga pirau), (kānga piro)}
