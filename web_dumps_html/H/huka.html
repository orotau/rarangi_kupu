He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



huka1

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He mirumiru moroiti tini whāioio ka kitea i te mata o te wai, i te kai moī, i te waha rānei ina pāngia e ētahi momo mate. (i) Ko te toroa uta nāku i tautara ki te akerautangi; ko te toroa tai nāku i kapu mai i te huka o te tai (NM1 30, 13). (ii) Ko te huka i te kōhua kai e tohu ana kua moī ngā kai. (iii) Ka kite i te huka i te waha o tana kurī, ka mōhio kua paihanahia. {hukahuka, hukanga, kohuka1, ware2 (2)}

2. [Ranginui] ing, āhua. He tioata wai ririki ka noho mai ki te whenua me ētahi atu mata whakamarumaru kore, ina heke te pāmahana ki raro i te kore. I ngā pō kore kapua, kore hau o te takurua tērā te tīkona ai o te papa e te huka.  hukapapa

huka kapo [Ranginui] ing. He ua kua totoka ka rere iho i ngā kapua pērā i te ua. Kua emiemi te huka kapo ki ngā awa o te māra.  ua2 (ua whatu)

3. [Tāwhirimātea] He tākohu i te kōhauhau ka huri hei hukapapa kōmāmā, he whā mā te hanga i te takanga iho ki te whenua. Ka kite ia i te huka o runga o Tongariro (W 67).  hukarere

4. ‑tanga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Te iti o te pāmahana, ina whakaritea ki ētahi atu mea; te mahana kore. Kua komeme noa ngā raho i te huka.  māeke

5. [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] ing, āhua. He āhuatanga e māharahara ai te tangata, i te mea e whakauaua ana i tētahi mahi, i tana noho rānei. He maha ngā huka e whakararu ana i taua whakahaere, hoi anō, ko te kore moni te mea nui.  raruraru (5)

6. [Rongo] reo kē. He kai reka ka whakamahia whānuitia hei whakareka i ētahi atu kai. Me rau atu he huka hei patu i te kawa o ngā paramu nā.

huka2

‑nga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. Arā noa atu (te wā ka taka). Arā tō rāua kāinga, kei tua o te hiwi nei; e kore e huka ka tae atu tāua.  roa1 (3)

huka3

[Tūmatauenga] ing. Te tangata whakamutunga ka patua kia mate i te pakanga.  ika2 (ika whakaotinga)

huka4

te huka o te riri [Tūmatauenga] ing. He whakakotahi anō, he whakatītina anō i te iwi ki te patu i te hoariri i muri i te hinganga. Nā te whakapono i ārai atu te huka o te riri o Waikato ki a Āti Awa (W 67).
