He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



anuanu1

1. ‑tanga [Whiro] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka ngākau kawa te tangata ki tētahi mea tino kino rawa atu, tino hē rawa atu, i te taupatupatu o taua mea ki tōna ngākau pai, ki ōna whakaaro tōtika. I taku rongonga i ngā kōrero mō ā rātou kai weriweri, tino anuanu au. {mākinokino, maninohea, weriweri, whakahouhou, whakarihariha}

2. ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Tino kino rawa atu, tino hē rawa atu i te taupatupatu ki te ngākau pai, ki te whakaaro tōtika o te tangata. Kātahi te kai anuanu ki te ihu ko te kōura mara, engari kia hoatu ki te waha, e, kāore i tua atu i tēnei kai. {auau1, etieti, kiriwetiweti, maninohea, mōkinokino, mōrihariha, mōrikarika, mōtekoteko, weriweri, wetiweti, whakarihariha, whakarikarika}

3. ‑tanga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, tūkē. Te iti o te pāmahana, ina whakaritea ki ētahi atu mea; te mahana kore. Kekekeke ana te kauwae i te anuanu.  māeke

4. ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Kāore e pai ki te titiro atu i te kore e pai o te āhua. Ki a au nei he ika anuanu te hokarari. {anuhea, kino, paraheahea, paranokenoke}

anuanu2

[Rongo] ing. Te umu i taona ai ngā kai mō te pure i te whakatōkanga i ngā kūmara. I te ata anō ka kā te pure, te ingoa o taua pure he anuanu (W 7).
