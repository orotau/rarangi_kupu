He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hīrau

1. ‑a ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mahi kia whīwhiwhi, kia mau tētahi mea. Ko te mahi a te pūngāwerewere, he hīrau ngārara ki tana māwhaiwhai.  porowhīwhiwhi

2. ‑tia ‑hia ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Whiro] Ka mahi kia tūtuki te waewae (o tētahi atu) ki tētahi mea, e hinga ai ia ki raro. (i) He aha i hīrautia nei e koe tōku waewae? (W 52) (ii) Ka hīrautia a Kiri e tana tungāne, kei raro e putu ana, me te auē anō o te waha. {rapahuki}

3. ‑a ‑ngia ‑tia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka kukume iho (i tētahi mea) ki tētahi rākau tokomanga roa. (Ka āta hurihia te tokomanga kia whīwhiwhi ai ki te mea e kūmea ana.) (i) Me hīrau atu te rā koia, e haramai rā (W 52). (ii) Ka taki haere ki te hīrau tāwhara.

4. ‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka mau kino tētahi wāhanga ki tētahi atu, ka mau kino rānei ki tētahi atu mea, he mahi nui ki te whakamatara, ki te wewete. Ki te hīrau te matau i te rimurimu, he mahi nui ki te huti ake.  whīwhiwhi

5. [Tāne] ing. He kakau poto, he kakau roa rānei, he rapa kei tētahi pito, kei ngā pito e rua rānei, ka poua ki te wai, ka kūmea, kia neke ai te waka. Ka taka te hīrau o tētahi o ngā kaihoe ki te wai.  hoe (5)
