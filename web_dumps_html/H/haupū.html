He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



haupū

1. ‑ria ‑tia ‑nga ‑ranga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka āta whakatakoto (i ētahi mea) ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Haupūria ngā tio ki te pereti nui, ka hari ai ki te tēpu. {apoapo, haipū, kāika, kauika, pūkai, pūkei, tāpui, tarahono, whakaahu, whakamoa, whakapīhangaiti, whakapū1 (1), whakaputu}

2. ‑nga ‑ranga ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua noho puke iti mai ki tētahi wāhi. Arā e haupū mai rā ngā rīwai hei kōmaka māu. {haipū, kāika, kāike, kauika, kāuki, pīhangaiti, pū1 (10), pūhangaiti, pūkai, pūkei, pūranga, putu, tāika, taka4 (3), tauputu, tāwheta, tihi, whakaika, whakapū1 (3), whārona}

3. [Papatūānuku] ing. Ētahi mea kua whakawhāitihia mai ki tētahi wāhi kia puke iti te noho mai. Tēnā haria ngā peha huawhenua nei ki te haupū wairākau e takoto mai rā. {ahu1 (1), apaapa1, apaapatū, ika4, kauika, kawa2, kōputu2, mātā1, pēhanga, pū1 (5), pūhanga1, pūkai, pūkainga, pūkei, pūranga, purawhetū, putu, putunga, ripanga, rōpū, tahua1, taipū2 (1), taka4 (1), taupū, tōhīanga, tuataka, whakapipi, whakapū1 (4), whakarae1, whārona}
