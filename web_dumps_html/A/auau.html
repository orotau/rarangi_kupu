He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



auau1

1. ‑tanga [Whiro] āhua, ing, mahp. Tino kore nei e pai ki te titiro atu, ki te rongo atu, ki te whāwhā atu; tino kore nei e pārekareka. Auau ana tērā tangata ki a au, kore rawa au e rata ki a ia.  houhou3 (2)

2. ‑tanga [Whiro] Tino kino rawa atu, tino hē rawa atu i te taupatupatu ki te ngākau pai, ki te whakaaro tōtika o te tangata. Kātahi nā te hanga auau, ko te mīti kua muia e te iro.  anuanu1 (2)

auau2

‑tanga [Ranginui] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Ka rite tonu te puta ake, te mahia rānei. (i) Nā tana hoahoa ki a Ngata rāua ko Te Kōti i auau ai tana tūtakitaki ki ngā āpiha a te kāwanatanga (TTR 3, 47). (ii) I noho auau tonu te tangata rā i taua hōtēra i ōna hokinga ki Taupō. (iii) Ahakoa he tangata auau tonu nei te pukuriri – kāore i mahue i a ia te kowhete hunga kua kotiti te haere – kātahi kē te tangata ngākau pono (TTR 5, 93).

auau3

‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka whakaputa (te kurī) i tētahi oro kaha, āhua rite ki te pakō, ki te pakē rānei. Ina auau te kurī, kua mōhio koe he tauhou e hipa ana i tērā taha o te taiapa. {ao3, apū2, au4, haru, pahu1, pahupahu, pari3, tau7 (5), tawetawē, whakaao, whakatau2}

auau4

[Rongo] ing. He kete purapura rīwai. Kia rua auau, ka kapi te māra (W 21).  rumaki (6)
