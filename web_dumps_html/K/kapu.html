He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kapu

1. [Tāne] ing. Te wāhi i te kapunga o te ringa ka totohu whakaroto ina piri ngā mati katoa. Ko te rongoā nei me riringi ki te kapu o te ringa, ā, ka ngongo ake mā te ihu, ā, ka tuha mai mā te waha (TT 43, wh188). {pārō}

2. [Tāne] Te taha whakararo o te waewae. (i) Inea te kapu o te wae kia mōhiotia ai te rahi o te hū. (ii) Ka wera ngā kapu o ana waewae i te onepū. {kapukapu, raparapa1 (1), takahanga, tapuae, taputapu, tapuwae}

3. [Tāne] hou. He toki maitai he rite te hanga o te mata ki te kapu o te ringa o te tangata. He tohunga te tangata rā ki te mau kapu.

4. [Tāne] reo kē. He oko paku whai kakau hei inu mā te tangata. Ka riringihia he wai ki tana kapu, ka inu.

5. [Tūmatauenga] reo kē. He taonga rite ki te oko ka hangā ki te hiriwa, ki te kōura rānei, kotahi tōna waewae, tērā pea he kakau kei ia taha, he taonga ka whakawhiwhia ki te toa o tētahi whakataetae, o tētahi āhuatanga rānei. Kāhore he hōnore o te kapu ki te kore i wini, engari he hōnore tonu tō te wini ahakoa kore kapu (TT 66, wh537).

6. [Tāne] ing, āhua. He momo mutu kākā.

7. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te taonga ka karakiatia e te tohunga kia mana ai tētahi rāhui. {whatu1 (5)}

8. ‑nga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp. E tōrino ana te āhua o te tupu (o ngā makawe). He roroa, he pango, he kapu ngā makawe.  māwhai
