He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hinga

1. ‑nga [Tāne] maho, ing, āhua. Ka tau atu, ka takoto atu (tētahi mea) ki te papa e tū rā ia. (i) I a ia e hīkoi ana, ka paheke i te hukapapa, ka hinga ki raro. (ii) Kia roa e kani ana i te rākau, ka hinga i a ia. (iii) Nō tētahi haerenga ōna, ka hinga tana hōiho, ka marū tōna tinana (KRK 88). {makahinga, pore3, taihinga}

2. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakamatea. (i) Ko te kai he kurī māori, hinga iho ngā kurī kotahi tekau (W 51). (ii) Ki te tōtara whatīa-rua te taua ka hinga i te hoariri, ki te kura takahi punitia, e kore e hinga (T 2, 49). {marū}

3. ‑nga [Tāne] Kua riro te mauri ora, kua kore i ora. (i) Kāore hoki i ārikarika te hinga a te pakeke, a te taitama, a te kōtiro, a te tamariki hoki (HP 36). (ii) I hinga i ngā mate o te ao, otirā he koroheke anō tētahi wāhi o te mate (PH 4, 3).  mate (1)

4. ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka raru i te whakataetae, i te pakanga, arā, kei raro e putu ana. (i) Ka hinga te tīma o Muriwhenua i a Murihiku. (ii) Ka tū he pakanga, ka hinga i konei a Te Whānau-a-Apanui (KRK 150). (iii) E tama, kua hinga au i a koe! {mīere1 (3)}
