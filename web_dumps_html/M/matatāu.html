He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



matatau

1. ‑hia ‑ria ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Kua whai i ngā pūmanawa e tutuki pai ai tētahi mahi, kua tohunga tonu, kua ringa rehe rānei ki tētahi mahi. (i) Me matatau anō te hēkeretari ki te patopato. (ii) I tino whakakoretia e ia ētahi atu reo i tōna whare, i waenga i tāna whānau kia ū tonu ai ki te reo Hīperu, kia tino matatau ai āna tamariki (H 5).

2. ‑nga [Tāne] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mau pai, ka mau tika ki te hinengaro. (i) Kia tino matatau au ki te nuinga ake o ngā tikanga o ēnei whenua, ka maha ake ai aku kupu (Pi 14, wh2). (ii) Ka mea atu ia me haere anō rāua i te tuatahi kia kite i āna whenua me ōna iwi; kia matatau ai hoki a Paoa ki ngā whanaunga o tana wahine (IwiT 22:14).  mōhio (1)

3. ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] āhua, ing, mahp. E whakaatu ana i te pirihonga, i te aroha ki tētahi atu tangata. He wahine matatau ki ana tamariki.

matatāu

‑tanga [Tāwhirimātea] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka neke ki tahaki. Ei, kaua e matatāu. Me uru mai koe ki te whakaahua nei.  kotiti (1)

mātātāu

[Papatūānuku] ing. He raenga e koutu mai ana i te hiwi, i te maunga.  karahiwi
