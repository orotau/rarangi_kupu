He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



ia1

[Tāne] tkp. Te kupu matua ka whakamahia e te kaikōrero hei tohu i te tangata, i te kararehe, i te aha atu kua kōrerotia, kua tohua rānei e ia i mua tata atu, e māmā ana rānei te tautohu atu. (i) He wahine manawanui a Kiri, e kore ia e tere hōhā i ngā mahi heahea a ngā tamariki. (ii) Kaua e homai ki a au - hoatu ki a ia! (iii) Ka totohu te waka me ngā tāngata katoa o runga i a ia ki te takere o te moana.

ia2

1. [Tāne] pmu. (Tētahi hanga) kua whakahuatia, kua tohua rānei i mua, ka noho hei kaupapa mō te kōrero ka whai ake. Me kōrero te ātaahua o Roimata. He rite ngā karu o ia wahine ki te āria.  taua2

2. [Tāne] Ina tū i mua i te tūingoa, e tohu ana i te hāngai o tētahi mahi, o tētahi āhuatanga ki tēnā, ki tēnā o aua mea, kāore kau tētahi e hapa. (i) He tiaki tonu hoki tāna i te kāinga i ia rā, i ia rā (W 74). (ii) Otirā ia iwi tāna tikanga anō, ia iwi tāna tikanga anō (W 74). (iii) Me whiwhi ia pouako i tētahi tauira o te pukapuka ārahi māna ake.

ia3

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He wāhanga i tētahi takotoranga wai, takotoranga aha atu rānei, e ū ana ki tētahi ahunga, e kaha kē atu ana te rere i ētahi atu wāhi. (i) Ka riro te kurī i te ia o te wai i a ia e whakawhiti ana i te awa. (ii) Kei te nuinga o ngā upoko o ēnei tuhituhinga e kitea iho ai te ia o ēnei kōrero (B 12). {au1 (4)}

ia toto [Tāne] hou. Tētahi o ngā pū e rere ai te toto i roto i te tinana. He momo ia toto te iatuku, ko tāna, he tuku toto ki ngā wāhi katoa o te tinana.

2. [Tangaroa] He manga wai e hohoro ana te rere. Me te ia wai e tangi ana te umere (W 74).

ia4

[Tāne] hono. He kupu e tohu ana i te kore e hāngai, i te taupatupatu o te āhuatanga whai i muri mai ki tētahi atu kei mua e rere ana. (i) E puaki ana te kupu pai, ko te mahi ia, kōrori kē (KtKM 1, 1). (ii) I mea a Te Hētana he hē anō kei roto i te pire, engari he hē ririki, ko te kaupapa ia o te pire, he pai (Pi 27, wh10). (iii) Ko tā te hunga tika e minamina ai, ko te pai anake; ko te tūmanako ia a te hunga kino, ko te riri (PT Wha 11.23).
