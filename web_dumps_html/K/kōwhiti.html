He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kōwhiti

1. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka kukume ki waho, ka tango ake. Ka mahi te tamaiti ki te kōwhiti mai i te toheroa i te onepū, tē unuhia.  unu1 (1)

2. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka tīkaro mai i ngā kai o roto i te mātaitai. He tokomaha tonu mātou ki te kōwhiti pūpū. {kōhiti, whakapūreo, whakapūrero}

3. ‑a ‑ngia ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka āta tohu i tētahi mea, i ētahi mea rānei o roto i tētahi huinga, i runga i te whakaaro koia te mea e tino tōtika ana, e tino eke ana, e tino hāngai ana rānei. I te pai o ana karu putē ka kōwhitia ia mō te rārangi tuatahi o te kapa.  kōwhiri

4. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tupana te tinana i te tumeke, i te pānga o tētahi āhuatanga matawhawhati. Tangi ana te waea i waenganui pō, kōwhiti tonu atu a Rena i tana moenga.  ohorere (1)

5. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka tākiri kia iti noa (o tētahi wāhanga o te tinana) i runga i te ohotata. (i) He uaua te kuhu atu i te miro ki te kōhao o te ngira mēnā e kōwhiti ana te ringa. (ii) Kua kōwhiti te karu i te tapahanga o te whēwhē.  korowhiti (2)

6. [Ranginui] ing. Te marama hou ka puta ake i muri tata mai i te rārangitanga o te rā, te marama me Papatūānuku, he ānau tino whāiti tōna hanga. Ka tīmata mai i te kōwhiti, ka rahi haere ā, hua rawa, ko Ōturu tēnā.  kōhiti (4)

7. [Tangaroa] He tuangi kua tangohia mai i te kota. Kotahi hāora noa iho i pau e noho mai ai tētahi oko kī ana i te kōwhiti.

8. [Tāne] He taputapu whāiti ka hangaia ki te rākau ki te maitai rānei, hei hiki, hei neke rānei i tētahi mea. Ka poua atu tētahi pito ki raro i te mea e nekehia ana, ka pēhia whakararotia kia neke ai. He mea neke te kōhatu nui ki te kōwhiti māitai.  hua2 (1)

9. [Tāne] He tauira tukutuku he rite te āhua ki te tauira purapura whetū, ki te pūkanohi rānei.

10. [Haumia] He wāhi i karia ai te aruhe.  kōhiti (5)
