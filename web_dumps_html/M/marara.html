He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



marara

1. ‑tanga [Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka takoto mai ki hea, ki hea, kāore e whāiti mai ki te wāhi kotahi. (i) Ko te haere i marara noa ki te kimi oranga mō rātou (WMA 7, 4). (ii) I te wā e iti ana te tai, ka marara noa atu te noho a te kuaka i runga i ngā tāhuna i ngā take moana, ki te rapu kai (Wh 40, 4). {hautea (whakahautea), hautea, hora1 (4), katea, kāteatea, katoha, kātohatoha, kaupāpari, kūwawa, maheu, mahora, makatea, mirara, mōriroriro, pahara, paihore, papata, pirara, pīwawa, rara2, rauraha, rauroha, renga2 (5), rengarenga, takoha, tīrangaranga, tīrango1, tīrangorango, tīrara, wawā1}

whatu marara [Tūmatauenga] ing. He wāhanga nō te karakia ka whakahaeretia mō ngā maramara kūmara ka tanumia ki te ara ka takahia mai e te hoariri, e 'wera' ai ō rātou waewae, arā, ka ngākau kore, ka oma.  mahunu (whatu mahunu)

2. ‑hia ‑tia ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua, tūkē. Kua wehea kia noho motuhake mai i te wāhanga matua, i te nuinga, i tētahi rōpū, i tētahi atu hanga rānei. Heoi wehea ake ērā tokorua, marara ana ki te wai, ki uta (W 181).  tauwehe (1)
