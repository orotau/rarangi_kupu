He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



niho

1. [Tāne] ing. Ngā hanga mārō i te waha e taea ai te ngau, te ngaungau. I roto i tōna waha te whānau niho e wānanga ana me tō rātou mōhio ka mate rātou, ka pirau hoki, mehemea ka kai rare a Hōri i ngā wā katoa (HM 5, 2). {karihi}

niho kaiū [Tāne] He niho tuatahi ka panaia e te niho pakeke ā tōna wā. Kia ono pea ngā tau o te tamaiti, kua tīmata te makere mai o ana niho kaiū.  (niho kai waiū)

niho kai waiū [Tāne] He niho tuatahi ka panaia e te niho pakeke ā tōna wā. Mate atu he niho kai waiū, puta mai he niho tautini. {(niho kaiū)}

niho kata [Tāne] He niho koikoi kei te noho tuatoru mai i te rārangi waenga o te pae niho; e rua kei te kauae runga, e rua anō kei te kauwae raro. Ka pai ngā niho kata hei tīhaehae i te kiko. {(niho rei)}

niho kōhari [Tāne] He niho kauoro kei te wāhanga whakamuri o te waha; e ono kei te kauwae runga, e ono anō kei te kauwae raro o te waha pakeke. Mā ngā niho kōhari rawa e taea ai ngā pīnati te nakunaku.

niho pū [Tāne] He niho tara rua kei muri tonu mai i ngā niho kata i te waha pakeke; e whā kei te kauae runga, e whā anō kei te kauae raro. He niho pakeke te niho pū - kāore e kitea i te waha o te tamaiti nohinohi.  (niho pūrākau)

niho pūrākau [Tāne] He niho tara rua kei muri tonu mai i ngā niho kata i te waha pakeke; e whā kei te kauae runga, e whā anō kei te kauae raro. Arā ngā niho kōhari, kei kō atu i ngā niho pūrākau. {(niho pū)}

niho rei [Tāne] He niho koikoi kei te noho tuatoru mai i te rārangi waenga o te pae niho; e rua kei te kauae runga, e rua anō kei te kauwae raro. Mataku katoa au i te inati o ngā niho rei o te tangata rā.  (niho kata)

niho taniwha [Tāne] He tauira whakairo mō te kete, he tauira tukutuku anō, he tapatoru te āhua, e whakaahua ana i ngā niho o te taniwha.

niho tapahi [Tāne] He niho ripi e noho ana ki te wāhi whakamua o te pae niho; e whā kei te kauae runga, e whā anō kei te kauae raro. E kīia ana ngā niho o mua he niho tapahi, i te mea koirā tā rātou mahi nui, he tapatapahi kai.

niho tāpiki [Tāne] He niho e inaki ana i tētahi atu. E mau tonu ana te kiko o taua tohorā ki te niho tāpiki o Kae, nā konā ia i raru ai. {(niho tāpiri)}

niho tāpiri [Tāne] He niho e inaki ana i tētahi atu. He momo whītiki maitai ka whakamaua ki ōna niho hei whakatika i te niho tāpiri.  (niho tāpiki)

2. [Tūmatauenga] Te wāhi tino koi, tino whāiti o tētahi taputapu pērā i te māripi, i te kāheru. He puruhia ēnei toki, kāhore he niho (W 221).  mata1 (4)

3. [Tūmatauenga] He kaha whai mana. (i) He kore nō te kāwanatanga i whakaae kia hōmai tētahi wāhi whakataunga kōrero whai niho ki te iwi Māori (TTR 2, 117). (ii) Ki te kore e ū, e kore tēnei taniwha e whai niho (HM 7, 3). (iii) Mehemea ka kitea te kapu o te rāhui e te kaiwhānako, kua kore he niho o taua kapu (W 221).

4. [Tāne] He hanga koi, he makiki, ka tipu i te tātā, i hea atu rānei o ētahi tipu pūtaratara pērā i te tūmatakuru, i te ongaonga, i te kōtimana. Engari te aka kiore, kāore he niho o tērā (W 221).  ngita2
