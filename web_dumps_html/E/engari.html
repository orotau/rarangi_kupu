He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



engari

1. [Tāne] hono. He kupu e tohu ana i te taupatupatu o te āhuatanga whai i muri mai ki tētahi atu kei mua e rere ana. (i) I whānau mai he kōtiro mā rāua, engari i mate. (ii) Tērā e maharatia he pōrangi engari hoki ko tana mahi e kore e taea e te pōrangi (KRK 176). {erangi}

2. [Tāne] I muri i te kīanga whakakāhore, e whakataki ana i tētahi whakaaro e tautoko ana, e whakaū ana i taua kōrero tuatahi. (i) Kei hopu tō ringa ki te aka tāepa, engari kia mau ki te aka matua (W 27). (ii) Kāhore ngā tikanga a te iwi i tīmata ake i te whakatupuranga kotahi, engari nō ngā whakatupuranga nui noa atu (KRK 51). (iii) Kāore hoki he karakia ki a ia mō ngā take ririki, engari mō ngā mea nunui anake (KRK 43). (iv) Kore rawa he tamaiti i mate wheke engari i mate ururoa katoa (KRK 85).

3. [Tāne] thu. Hei whakakāhore i te kōrero whakakāhore, i te pātai whakakāhore rānei; hei whakaū i ērā atu tūmomo kōrero, pātai rānei. (i) Kāore ōna take. Rangi: Engari. He pai tonu a ia ki tāna mahi. (ii) Pare: Kāore ō tamariki? Rangi: Engari tonu. E whā, he tāne katoa. (iii) Pare: He pai tēnā whakaaro. Rangi: Engari. He whakaaro tino pai. (iv) He toto Māori anō ōna? Rangi: Engari tonu - he Māori te māmā.  ehara (4)
