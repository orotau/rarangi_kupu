He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



kete

1. [Tāne] ing. He hanga hei rau atu i ngā momo mea katoa, ka rangā ki te harakeke, he kīwei tō te nuinga hei kawe haere ki te ringa. (i) I taku taenga ki te kāinga e takoto ana ngā kete tio e rua māku (Wh 11, 6). (ii) Ko tēnei wahine he tohunga ki te raranga whāriki, raranga kete hoki (Wh 26, 5). (iii) Ko taku kete tuatahi i haria e taku kuia hei kete rīwai māna. {hao, rawhi2}

kete kōwarawara [Tāne] He kete kōkau noa te hanga. Ahakoa he kete kōwarawara, e kore e makaia atu, inā hoki nā taku kuia i raranga.

kete pāhao [Tāne] He kupenga mō te hao kōuraura.  (kete tīhao)

kete tīhao [Tāne] He kupenga mō te hao kōuraura. {(kete pāhao)}

kete whakawaitara [Tāne] He kete whakairo.

kete aronui [Tāne] Tētahi o ngā kete o te wānanga e toru, ko ngā taonga matua o roto ko ngā mātauranga e pā ana ki te aroha, ki te maungārongo me ngā mahi katoa e puta ai te pai ki te tangata, ki te whenua, ki te rākau, ki te wai, ki te oneone, arā, ki ngā mea katoa e kitea ana e te tangata.

kete tuatea [Tāne] Tētahi o ngā kete o te wānanga e toru, ko ngā taonga matua o roto ko ngā mātauranga e pā ana ki te mākutu, ki te whaiwhaiā.

kete tuauri [Tāne] Tētahi o ngā kete o te wānanga e toru, ko ngā taonga matua o roto ko ngā mātauranga e pā ana ki ngā karakia mō ngā whakahaere katoa e pā ana ki a Ranginui, ki a Papatūānuku, me te mana o ngā mea katoa e hiahiatia ana kia mahia e te whānau a Papatūānuku.

2. [Tāne] Te puku o te kupenga.  pukehina

3. [Papatūānuku] Te wāhi o te tinana wahine, o te tinana whāereere rānei e tipu ai te kukune.  Kupu whakarite. He māhanga kei te kete o tōku hoa rangatira, koirā i kaha tonu ai te konaki haere.  kōpū (1) tangata (1)

4. [Tāne] He momo karakia e whakamahia ai he kete. Ka hoatu i te oranga mō rātou, ko te kete, ko te ahi, ko te taumata (W 115).

kete pure [Tāne] He kete ka whakamahia e te tohunga i ngā karakia whakahohoro tapu.
