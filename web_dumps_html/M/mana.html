He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mana1

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui] ing, mahp, āhua, maho. Te tū rangatira e tautokona ana, e whakaaetia ana e ētahi atu; te kaha ki te ārahi, ki te tohutohu i ētahi atu. (i) He rangatira whai mana a Hongi. Nō Tūhoe te mana me te rangatiratanga (W 172). (ii) Ka karangatia e te manuao kia hoki taua tima, kāore ōna mana ki te haere i runga i taua moana (HTK 1, 16). (iii) Ko te rironga tērā o te mana tangata, o te mana whenua, o te mana kōrero ki raro i a Aotea (B 10). {ihi2, kanoi, maru, tikanga}

2. [Tūmatauenga] ing. Te kaha e taea ai ngā whakaaro, ngā mahi rānei a tētahi atu te whakarerekē, te whakawai. Koirā te mana o te hikareti, he tino uaua te kore e hoki atu anō ki a ia.  awe1 (4)

3. [Tūmatauenga] He kaha nō roto tonu i te tangata, nō te wairua, nō te hinengaro o te tangata. Ehara i te tino mate rawa te mate o Tāwhaki, ā, nōna ake anō te mana i ora ake ai anō ia (W 172).

4. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] āhua, mahp. E whai take ana, e pūmau ana i runga i ngā tikanga o te iwi, me te titiro a te nui tangata, e kore e turakina e wai rānei. (i) Kia mana rā tā tāua awhiawhi (W 172). (ii) Ahakoa kāore i turetia, ki te Māori o taua wā kua mana tonu atu (HP 40).

5. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka puta ōna hua, ka whakatinanatia (o te kupu). (i) Ka hāmama ngā waha ki te karakia, ka koa hoki ki ā rātou nei mea ka mana (W 172). (ii) Ko te whakaaro o Te Rauparaha, ka mana nei anō tana kanga ki ngā tāngata o Kaikōura, ka whakamutu te patu (W 172). (iii) Nōnahea i mana ai te whakahau kia whakamutua te kai paipa i ngā pāparakāuta?

6. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka utua tētahi hara, ka ngakina rānei tētahi mate. Ka whakaaro a Te Rauparaha me pēwhea rā ka mana ai te mate o Te Pēhi (W 172).  ea1 (2)

mana2

[Tangaroa] ing. He ika rite ki te kōura tino iti, nō te karangatanga kōurapaka. Paratya curvirostris.  kōuraura

māna

[Tāne] tkp. He kupu e tohu ana mā tētahi tangata i tua atu i te kaikōrero me te kaiwhakarongo te mea, te mahi, te aha atu e kōrerotia ana. (i) Māna kē tērā inu. Anei kē tāu. (ii) He kōtiro pai a Mārama. Māna koe e tiaki, e āwhina. (iii) He tangata pai te tumuaki hou. Māna e tōtika ake ai taua whakahaere. {māhana}
