He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hika1

1. ‑ia ‑nga [Tāwhirimātea] mahw, ing, āhua. Ka mukumuku (i tētahi mea) kia kaha tonu, kia tere tonu ki te mata o tētahi atu mea. Ki te hika koe i ō ringa ka rongo tō kiri i te mahana.

2. ‑ia ‑nga [Tāne] Ka mukumuku (i te kauahi) ki te kaunoti e puta ai he ahi. Me i mōhio rātou ki te hika i te ahi kua ora mai rātou i tērā moutere.

3. ‑ia ‑ina ‑nga [Tāne] Ka mahi te tokorua i ētahi mahi pērā i te kuhu atu i te ure tū ki te puta, e tokomauri ai te tangata. Me mau pūkoro ure ka hika ana koe i te tauhou.  ai2 (2)

4. ‑ia ‑ina ‑nga [Rongo] Ka kuhu atu (i te tipu, i te kākano) ki te oneone, kia tipu ai. Ka hikaia e Ranginui ko ngā otaota hei taupoki mō te aroaro o Papa (W 49).  whakatō (2)

5. ‑ia ‑nga [Tūmatauenga] Ka karakia, me te tahu ahi anō. Kātahi a Ngātoro ka hika ki te pou mua (W 49).

hika2

1. [Tāne] ing. He kupu hei mihi i te tangata ahakoa tāne, ahakoa wahine. (i) E hika ē! (W 49) (ii) Ka ui atu te kuia rā ki a Huhuti, 'E hika, ko hea koe?' (W 49) {tai2}

2. ‑tanga [Tāne] ing, mahp, āhua. He wahine e tamariki tonu ana, e taiohi tonu ana rānei. He hika katoa ngā kaimahi i te wharekai - kei waho kē ngā tama, kei te taha o te hāngī.  kōtiro

3. [Tāne] ing. Te tamaiti wahine a tētahi wahine, a tētahi tāne.  tamāhine (1)

4. [Papatūānuku] He puku iti kei te pito whakamua o te tara o te wahine. Tū ana te hika i te hiamo.  tonetone
