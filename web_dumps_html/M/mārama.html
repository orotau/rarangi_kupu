He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



marama

1. [Ranginui] ing. Te amiorangi e taiāwhio ana i tō tātou ao, ka mārama te kitea i te rangi i ngā pō. He wiki kē, he āhua kē tōna, ā, ka oti te amionga kotahi i te āhua 30 rā. E tīaho ana te marama i waho, engari he nui ngā kapua kei te rangi (Wh 55, 4). {ahoroa, atarau, Hina (Hina keha), Hina, koirau, Māhina}

2. [Ranginui] Tētahi o ngā wehenga tekau mā rua o te maramataka. (i) Ka whakahaeretia te whakamātautau tuatahi i te marama o Whiringa-ā-nuku, 1992 (HM 4, 3). (ii) Nō te marama o Pēpuere, ka haria atu au e taku whāea whāngai ki Tūranga (HP 36). (iii) I tangihia ki Tāne-nui-a-Rangi mō te marama kotahi (HP 8).

mārama

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. E nui ana te aho, kāore i pōuri (o tētahi wāhi). (i) Ko tōku āhua i rite ki te tangata i roto i te rūma pōuri e noho ana. Ināianei kua puta ahau kei te wahi mārama noho ai (TT 26 wh15). (ii) Ka tomo mātou i raro o ngā maunga haere ai i te wāhi pōuri, ka puta atu ki te wāhi mārama haere ai (WM 10, 16). (iii) Tēnā whakakāngia te rama o waho kia mārama ai te ara hīkoi atu ki ngā waka.

whānau mārama [Ranginui] ing. Te huinga o ngā mea e kitea ana i te rangi, arā, ngā whetū, ngā aorangi, ngā amiorangi, ngā matakōkiri, ngā aha atu, ngā aha atu. Kei roto katoa a Tamanuiterā, te marama, a Meremere, a Matawhero, a Pareārau, i te whānau mārama. {whānau (whānau ariki)}

2. ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E tuku aho atu ana ki roto e māmā noa ai te kitea atu o ngā mea kei roto, kei muri rānei. Mārama kehokeho te moana (W 180).  pūataata1 (1)

3. ‑tanga [Tāne] āhua, ing, mahp, tūkē. E māmā ana te kitea atu o te tikanga (o te kōrero). (i) He kupu mārama rawa aua kupu a Te Tamihana, ā he tangata tika a ia ki te whakaatu kōrero (Wa 3, 3). (ii) Ki te tukua mai ā koutou pukapuka tono whakawā mō ō koutou whenua ki tēnei Tari, kia mārama te tuhituhi mai (PWW 1, wh4). {māori2}

4. ‑tia ‑tanga [Tāne] mahw, ing, mahp, āhua. Ka kite i te tikanga, i te hōhonutanga atu o tētahi mea. (i) E pai ana, kua mārama mātou ināianei ki te take o te raruraru (WM 9, 6). (ii) Kei te mārama au he nui ngā whāwhārua o ēnei kōrero hei whakakopi (B 9). (iii) Kei te mārama anō koe ki ngā kōrero kei roto i ana whakairo? (KK 171) {mātau1 (2), mōhio, orotau}
