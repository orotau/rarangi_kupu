He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



Kōpū

[Ranginui] ing. Te aorangi noho tuarua mai i a Tamanuiterā, he aorangi mārama. Koia nei te ingoa ina rere i te ata pō ki te rāwhiti. (i) Ānō ko Kōpū ka puta ake i te pae ngā karu o te maipi (W 138). (ii) Tauhōkai ana Kōpū i te ata (W 138). {Kōpūparapara, Tāwera}

kopu

1. [Tangaroa] ing. He ika wai māori e 20 henemita te tipu. Galaxias fasciatus.  kōkopu (1)

2. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori 15-24 henemita te tipu. Gobiomorphus gobioides.  kōkopu (3)

3. [Tangaroa] He ika wai māori e 8 henemita te tupu, he rauwhero, he kiwikiwi parauri rānei te tinana, he tāhekeheke kei te upoko, he tāingoingo kei te tinana, he tāwakawaka kei ngā paihau. Gobiomorphus huttoni. {toitoi1 (4)}

kopū

1. ‑tanga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua eke ki te nui, ki te maha rānei e taea ana (e tētahi mea) te pupuri. Kopū ana te ipu wai, ki te ringihia atu anō he wai ki roto, ka puhake.  kī2

2. ‑nga ‑ranga ‑tanga [Tāne] Ka puku mai te kiri me te kī o taua puku i te wai, i te mea kua wera, kua roa rawa rānei e hikaia ana ki tētahi mea. Me mau karapu te kaikukume taura kia kore ai ana ringa e kopū.  hōpūpū

‑tanga [Tāne] E kopa ana (te papa) i te wera o te rā, i te haukū rānei. Ka kopū te papa i te kaha o te rā (W 138).  kōrapa (4)

kōpū

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. Te wāhi o te tinana wahine, o te tinana whāereere rānei e tipu ai te kukune. Te tikanga, e iwa marama te pēpi e tipu ana i te kōpū ka whānau mai ki te ao. {ewe, kete} tangata (1)

2. [Tāne] Te wāhanga o te tinana kei raro atu i ngā rara, kei reira e noho mai ana te puku me ngā kōpiro. Kātahi a Hona ka inoi ki a Ihowā, ki tōna Atua, i roto i te kōpū o te ika (PT Hon 2.1). {hōpara1, manawa, riu, takapū}

3. [Tāne] Te wāhi kikokiko o muri i te waewae i waenganui i te turi me te rekereke. Ka tupu tētahi mate kino whakarihariha ki te kōpū o tōku waewae (W 138).  tapuhau

4. [Tāne] He momo kākahu roto. He kōpū te pūweru roto o neherā, he hingareti, he himi, he tarau iti ētahi o te ao hou.
