He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



mātārae

1. [Papatūānuku] ing. He pito whenua teitei e toro atu ana i te tahatika ki te moana, ki te roto rānei. I kitea anō hoki ia i ngā mātārae o Katikati i te tau 1870, ko ia rā te rangatira o tētehi ope Hauhau e hī ika ana (TTR 2, 155). {hū4, koi1, koraenga, koutu, kūmore, kūmū, kūrae, mata1 (6), matakūrae, matamata1 (5), more, ngahu, ponaihu, poraenga, pukerae, rae, raenga, torouka2, tūmū, whakaihu}

2. [Papatūānuku] He raenga e koutu mai ana i te hiwi, i te maunga. Taku manu noho mātārae i whāngaia ki te hau (TT 88, t31).  karahiwi

3. [Tūmatauenga] ing, āhua. He tangata nui te mana, he tangata whakahirahira.  Kupu whakarite. Kei ngā mātārae e pae nei, kua rangatira rā mātou i a koutou.  paewai (1)
