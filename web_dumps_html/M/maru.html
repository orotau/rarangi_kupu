He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



maru

1. [Ranginui] ing. Te tū rangatira e tautokona ana, e whakaaetia ana e ētahi atu; te kaha ki te ārahi, ki te tohutohu i ētahi atu. (i) Nā te maru i a ia, koia mātou i mataku ai (W 184). (ii) Ka riro te mana o te tangata, te maru o te whenua (W 184).  mana1 (1)

whenua maru [Tūmatauenga] He whenua kei raro i te mana tōrangapū o tētahi atu whenua, e nohoia ana anō e ngā tāngata o taua whenua. He whenua maru a Aotearoa nō Ingarangi i taua wā. {koroni}

2. [Ranginui] ing, āhua. Te āhua o tētahi mea ka kitea i tētahi mata ina āraia atu ngā hihi o te rā, o te atarau, o te rama rānei e tētahi hanga kāore e whakaata aho. He maru nui tō Taranaki ka hora ki muri i a ia i te tōnga o te rā.  ata2

3. [Tāne] ing. He wāhi, he whare, he aha atu ka ārai atu i ngā kino o Tāwhirimātea, i tētahi atu āhuatanga kino rānei. Ka haere te tangata ka rokohanga e te ua, ka hanga i te ponga hei maru i te tūpuhi (TT 88, t91). {pāruru, pātūtū, tāwharau, tūrutu, whakamarumaru, whakaruru, whakaruruhau}

4. [Tūmatauenga] He huinga tāngata e tautoko ana i tētahi tangata whai mana. Ahakoa haere te Pirimia ki hea, kei reira tana maru mau pū ki te tiaki i a ia.  apataki

5. [Tāne] He mea e noho mai ana, i tāia rānei ki tētahi mata, e whakaatu ana i tētahi āhuatanga. Tēnei anō rā ō maru i waiho te mau nei kei taku tuakiri (W 184).  tohu1 (3)

6. ‑nga ‑tanga [Ranginui] āhua, ing, mahp. E āhua pōuri ake ana, e āhua mātao ake ana nā te mea e āraihia atu ana ngā hihi o te rā e tētahi mea. I te tīkākā o te rā, ka noho ia ki te taha maru o te whare.  marumaru

7. ‑nga ‑tanga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka tino kī me te iti o te wāhi e wātea ana. Maru ana tērā te marae i te rā o te nehunga.  kikī (1)

marū

1. ‑tanga [Tāne] maho, ing, mahp, āhua. Kua pōuri, kua rerekē rānei te tae o te kiri i te mea kua pākia e tētahi mea i pahū ai ngā iaia toto o raro iho (i te tinana), i kino ai rānei te kiko (o te huawhenua, huarākau). (i) Ka tika mai te pōro kirikiti ki taku waewae, marū tonu atu. (ii) Kaua e pēhia ngā āperekoti, kei marū. {hume, mongamonga, wairau}

2. ‑tanga [Rongo] Kua tukitukia, kua wāwāhia kia moroiti ngā wāhanga, kia tino hē rānei te hanga. Ka marū tana ringa i te mīhini e tōia ana i muri o te tarakihana - e rua ngā mati ka poroa.  mongamonga (1)

3. ‑tanga [Tūmatauenga] Ka whakamatea. Kua marū kē ngā poaka te tukituki (W 184).  hinga (2)

4. ‑tanga [Rongo] Kua pai ki te kai i te tika o te wā e tunua ana. Ka werohia atu ngā rīwai ki te paoka kia mōhio ai mehemea kua marū.  maoa (1)

5. ‑tanga [Rongo] mahp, ing, āhua. Kua pakari, kua pai ki te kai, ki te tunu (o te huarākau). Ka ngau tō puku ki te kai koe i te āporo kāore anō i marū.  maoa (2)

6. ‑tanga [Rūaumoko] mahp. Ka haruru, ka tangi mārū. Ka rongo mātou i te tereina e marū mai ana.  ngunguru (4)

mārū

1. ‑tanga ‑nga [Ranginui & Papatūānuku] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka ngāwari noa, kāore e taikaha. He mārū te heke iho i runga mai o ngā maunga (W 184).  mōwai (3)

2. [Tāne] ing. He rākau 15 mita te tupu. Leptospermum ericoides.  kānuka
