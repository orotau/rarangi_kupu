He aha te kupu e kimi ana koe i te ī-papakupu?

 
Te āhua o ngā kōrero ka takoto: poto    roa



hara1

1. ‑nga [Whiro] mahp, ing, āhua. Ka mahi i tētahi mahi e takahi ana i te tapu, i te tika, i te ture rānei. (i) Utu ana te tangata i hara me te wahine i hara, me ngā tāngata hara kore hoki, ā pau ana ō mātou taonga i runga i ngā ture Māori o mua (WM 13, 23). (ii) Kua whakawātia ā kua hara ia mō tōna okanga i te Tumuaki o Wīwī, ā kua whakataua ināianei kia mate ia (HTK 2, 26).

2. [Whiro] ing, āhua. He mahi e takahi ana i te tapu, i te tika, i te ture rānei. (i) Ka tiro whakarunga a Matua Eru me te kī, 'Tangaroa, he aha rā taku hara?' (Wh 59, 1) (ii) Heoi, mā Te Atua e muru ōku hara (HP 238). (iii) Ka mau rāua e whiuwhiu paramu ana ki te whare o te kiritata, whakaritea ana ko te horoi i ana matapihi te utu mō tō rāua hara. (iv) Tukua kia whakapāngia te whiu ki a ia, arā, tā te Ture whiu i whakarite ai mō tōna hara (KtKM 2, 2).

hara2

‑nga [Tāne] mahp, ing, āhua. Kāore i pā (tētahi hanga rere) ki te mea i hiahia rā te tangata kia tika i a ia. Ka āta tihe tana hoa whakataetae i a ia e tuku ana i tana kōpere, hara tonu atu.  hihipa
